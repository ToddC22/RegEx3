Procedure: Xbox: Subscription cancelled in error by an Xbox support agent

[Asset 4049349]

The information in this article applies to the following markets:
  • All markets

 


Who should use this article?
  • All Xbox agents who handle billing or account management interactions.
What is this procedure about?
  • This procedure describes how to assist a customer who had their Xbox Live Gold subscription, or EA Play canceled in error by an Xbox support agent.
Determine if the subscription was canceled by a support agent
  1. Use MST to confirm that the subscription has been canceled. If the subscription status is “Delayed Cancel” and the customer wants the subscription to continue, turn auto renewal back on; do not engage the Microsoft-based Advanced Capability Advocates or refund the customer since no subscription time has been lost.  
  2. If the subscription has been canceled, check the System Activity details and the comments in MST to determine who canceled the subscription:  
    • If the subscription was cancelled because the subscription ended, the customer can purchase a new subscription or add a prepaid subscription card. There is no time owed the customer, nor does the customer need a refund. Educate the customer about the subscription history so the customer understands when the subscription ended.
    • If the subscription was canceled with the System Activity details “Subscription for offering cancelled. Delegate Comment: Suspicious or fraudulent sign up and or account activity. Account has been locked. Refer to KB article 931798(Xbox) or 941243(Zune) for proper handling. Purchases or Point Redemptions may continue to be blocked for a day or two after the account is unlocked.”, this means the subscription was canceled due to the purchase or renewal being charged back. No replacement should be given in this scenario.

      [Asset 4024617]
    • If the subscription was canceled by an agent, you should see an agent alias in the System Activity details and the comments added by the agent in MST:  

  3. Go to Orders in MST to determine if the customer was refunded for the subscription.

    If the customer was refunded for the subscription and claims the subscription was canceled in error by an agent, do not engage the Microsoft-based Advanced Capability Advocates. Explain to the customer that since they were refunded for the subscription, they can purchase a new Xbox Live Gold subscription either online or on the console.

    If the customer was not refunded and claims the subscription was cancelled in error, issue tokens for any remaining subscription time that the customer is owed. Do not engage the Microsoft-based Advanced Capability Advocates.
