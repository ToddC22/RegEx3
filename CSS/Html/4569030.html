Technical Guide: Known issues with Microsoft Teams for your personal life

[Asset 4049349]

 

Who should use this article?

  • All Consumer

What is this article about?

This article explains known issues with Microsoft Teams for your personal life.

Known Issues and Workarounds

Topic

Issue

Workaround

Notes

Calendar 

Group events created by you do not appear in your personal calendar.  

Check your spam mail folder as the event invite emails very likely have gone there. 

 

 

If you edit or cancel an event, the chicklet posted in the chat won't reflect the changes. 

Also, when you tap a deleted event in chat, you get a confusing error message. 

None currently

 

 

Personal and group events are not differentiated in personal calendar. 

None currently

If you use calendar actively both for personal and groups, it will be confusing as there's no visual indication for personal and group events in your personal calendar. And you can't only show personal or group events there. 
 

There's no 'daily' option for recurring events.

    

Calling 

If you are in your work account, you won't receive notifications for incoming Teams for personal calls. 

None currently

You should get a missed call notification though and that's shown in Activity Feed and Calls in Teams for personal. 

Note: You won't get a missed call notification in Activity Feed for missed group calls on Android. 

 

Adding a user to a call adds them to the related group chat too. 

Create a new group chat for the call with the needed participants before starting the call. 

This is especially bad when you start a call from a group chat with just two participants (effectively 1:1) and then during a call add another participant. The new participant will be added to the group chat and they will see the full chat history. There's no warning that the full chat history will be shared and no option to not to share chat history with the new participant. 
 

Missed group calls are not shown in Activity Feed and are shown in Calls as 1:1 calls with no badge in app bar. 

    
 

When joining a live event, if you see error 'Live event unavailable', you need to sign out from TFL and the live event link should then work and allow you to join as a guest. 

    

 

No background blur on Android. 

None currently

 

Groups 

There's no option to delete a group chat. 

You can hide the chat until the next message in it arrives. And when the last participant leaves, the group chat will be deleted. 

 

Location 

Location settings under Settings looks strange. 

None currently

This is by current design, will get improved later. 

Notifications 

When receiving a chat message from a non-contact, their Skype ID might be shown in the push notification. 

None currently

 
 

Call notifications for TFL won't come through if you are in TFW or used TFW the last before backgrounding the app. 

    
 

There are still relatively few events triggering notifications to Activity Feed on TFL, more to be added soon. 

    

People 

When you change your phone number or email on your Microsoft Account, you won't be searchable with the new details. 

Uninstall the Teams app and reinstall. 

 

 

When you create a chat with a device contact, an invite will be sent to them even if they might be using Teams for personal already. 

None currently

 

Presence 

Group chat participants are shown offline. 

Send a chat message to the participants shown offline. 

 
  Presence changes with delay. Move away from the screen and come back. 

Presence should get updated within one minute. 
Search

Group names containing an apostrophe may not appear in search results. 

    

Sign in/Sign out 

If you accidentally verify your phone number for a 'wrong' Microsoft account, there's no feature in the app to move it. 

Sign into https://account.live.com/names/Manage with the 'wrong' Microsoft account and remove the phone number alias.

Sign out from the 'wrong' Microsoft account in the Teams app.

Sign into the Teams app (or add account) with the correct Microsoft account and you should be able to verify the phone number for this Microsoft account now. 

 

 

When you sign in with your Microsoft account, all associated accounts associated with that Microsoft account will be added even if you need to initially select one. All these accounts will also be removed when you sign out from any of them. 

None Currently

 

 

After creating a new work/school account to associate with your Microsoft account, it might not be visible when signing in on mobile. 

Also, you might get asked to redeem an invitation to free version of Teams again when signing in. 

If the work/school account is not shown when signing in, it should appear in 15-20 minutes and you can then try again. 

If prompted to redeem an invitation to free version of Teams again, you can just do that or wait about 12 hours. 

 

Tasks 

After a chicklet is posted in a chat, editing the task list won't update the chicklet. 

None currently

 
 

If you select Hide completed, this setting will revert to default Show completed when you restart the app. 

    
 

On Android, once a due date is set for a task, it can't be cleared. 

    
Invites

The rollout for Android is gradual, the invite links to Teams for personal won't work until you have the new app version that supports Teams for your personal life.

 None currently  
Joining live events and meetings Trouble with iOS when trying to join live events and meetings.

Sign out from Teams for Personal.

This will allow them to join live events and meetings as a guest.

 There will be a fix for this issue in an upcoming release.
