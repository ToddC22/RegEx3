PCTS team is a break fix team relating to the CSP portal. This article defines the support boundaries for all the technical issues in the CSP tab under the Microsoft Partner Center UX/API.

Note: The PCTS team operates from Monday - Friday (24*5), Saturday: 11:00 AM GMT - 08:00 PM GMT

RSC:

  • Queries (What, How, When, Where) under the CSP tab of the Microsoft Partner Center portal
  • Assistance with onboarding onto the CSP program for all Partner types (Direct, Indirect Provider, Indirect Reseller)
  • Multi-channel and multi-partner scenarios (ex.: transitioning from Direct/EA to CSP, transitioning from one CSP to another)
  • Requests to update Partner billing and legal details
  • Issues and questions related to pricelists, catalog, offer matrix, including price discrepancies
  • Subscription and User Management
  • Assistance with getting access to special qualifications (like EDU)
  • Issues and questions related to invoices and recon files
  • Requests to issue old invoices with updated details
  • Payment missing queries
  • Requests for forms/letters (like CSP status confirmation letter, DAF, Bank forms)
  • Credit requests
  • Update MPN ID on CSP account
  • General Partner Center Tool Navigation (How to questions)
  • Customer/Partner detail changes on Partner Center, Invoice, Recon File
  • Non-technical issues relating to Breaking customer/CSP relationship

MPN:

  • All queries related to the MPN program (how to enroll, questions around membership options, benefits)
  • Activating MPN benefits (IUR, assistance with creating requests for the PreSales Technical Consultant team, activating Visual Studio subscription, etc.)
  • MPN subscription and “billing” (i.e. MPN program orders) queries
  • Partner of Record (POR) – missing sales, cloud performance requirements
  • MPN Partner Dashboard issues
  • Partner University issues
  • Partner Center business profile and referrals issues (UX)
  • Assistance with migrating of MPN membership from Partner Membership Center (PMC) to Partner Center (PC)
