1. Overview

Before using this Tool User Guide, be sure:

  • You were provided a LivePerson Conversational Cloud login.
  • You successfully completed LivePerson Conversational Cloud training.
  • You are familiar with chat fundamentals.

2. Tool steps

2.1 Sign into Conversational Cloud

Step

Image
  1. Launch Edge.

[Asset 4032652]
  1. Navigate to https://authentication.liveperson.net/login.html.

[Asset 4032653]
  1. Enter 60270350 for Account number.

[Asset 4032654]
  1. Enter your user ID in the format vendor_site_advocatename.

[Asset 4032655]
  1. Enter your password.

[Asset 4032656]
  1. Select Sign In.

[Asset 4032657]

 

Notes:

  • Passwords expire after 90 days.
  • You can’t use any of your last 12 passwords.
  • Password must be at least eight characters long and contain one number and one special character.
  • You must login at least once every 21 days to keep your profile active, otherwise it will be automatically deactivated.
  • If you fail login three times, your account will be automatically deactivated. Note: Attempting to login from outside of the IP allow list counts as a failed login.
  • If you fail login three times, you must wait 30 minutes before attempting to re-enable your account.

2.3 Set Status to Online

After you sign in, set your status to Online so you can receive new chats. Conversational Cloud has three statuses available: Online, Away (with Away States), and Back Soon. As an advocate, you will use Online and will choose an Away state. Conversational Cloud will not automatically set your status to online.

Step

Image
  1. Select the avatar in the bottom left-hand corner.

[Asset 4032764]
  1. Select Online.

[Asset 4032769]
  1. You should now see a green icon with a check mark next to your avatar.

[Asset 4032770]

Return to top

2.4 Set Status to Away

If you are going on break or the end of shift, set yourself to Away, without affecting your current conversations. You won’t get new chats while you are Away, but you will need to conclude your existing conversations.

Step

Image
  1. Select the avatar in the bottom left-hand corner.

[Asset 4032764]
  1. Select Away.

[Asset 4032771]
  1. Choose an Away State.

[Asset 4552166] [Asset 4552167]
  1. You should now see a red icon next to your avatar.

 [Asset 4032764]

2.5 Accept new chat automatically

Conversational Cloud will automatically accept new chats. You will hear a tone or see on-screen notifications when you get new chats. Each new chat will open a new window (or Advocate Workspace) represented by a row on the My Conversations tab.

Step

Image
  1. While your status is set to Online, watch for messages or sounds that indicate you have accepted a new engagement.
  1. You are connected automatically.

[Asset 4032778]
  1. Each new engagement will open as a row in the My Conversations list.

[Asset 4032779]

2.11 Navigate to Dynamics Plasma to connect Conversational Cloud (SMB ADVOCATES ONLY)

Logging into the Dynamics Plasma widget allows you connect Conversational Cloud to MSX Apportal. This means that a new lead will be automatically created in USD/CRM Online each time you take a new chat in Conversational Cloud. It is important that you login to the Dynamics Plasma widget each time you login to Conversational Cloud, or else your leads will not be automatically created. Follow the instructions below on how to login.

Note: If at any time during the login process you are asked for your 2FA card, follow the instructions onscreen.

  1. Select the Dynamics Plasma widget.

[Asset 4552168]  

  1. Select Dynamics Plasma in the first box and enter your Microsoft alias in the second box. Then select Login.

[Asset 4552169]

  1. Select Authorize me if the new tab doesn't open automatically.

[Asset 4552170]

  1. A new tab or window in your browser will open. Enter you Microsoft email address in the box, and then select Next.

[Asset 4552171]

  1. If prompted with the screen below, select Accept.

[Asset 4552172]

  1. The message below will appear. Wait five seconds and the tab will automatically close.

[Asset 4552173]

  1. You will then see the message below, indicating that you have successfully signed in and your lead has been created in USD/CRM Online. You can now select any other widget that you wish.

[Asset 4552174]

Return to top

2.13 Use predefined Content

If your business uses predefined Content, it will be available through a widget at right.

Step

Image

  2. Place the cursor in the location where you want the predefined content to appear.

[Asset 4508860]
  1. Select the icon for Predefined Content if the widget is not visible.

[Asset 4508861]
  1. Expand the content categories as needed.
