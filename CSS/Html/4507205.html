Procedure: Customer out of office (OOF)

[Asset 4462485]

Description

To ensure a consistent experience of Microsoft Support, this procedure should be used when a customer advises they will be out of office (OOF) or you become aware that a customer you’re working with is now OOF.

1. Steps

When a customer is out of office, it is always preferred that a support engineer is able to continue working the case. If this is not possible, the engineer must establish a plan of action to ensure the case does not become stagnant.   

To facilitate this, establish either:

  • An alternate contact to continue working the case with.

Or

  • The customer’s date of return and communicate a next contact commitment for a time around their return.

Establishing an alternate contact for the case and determining a date of return

  • If the customer informs you of their OOF:
    • Establish and document an alternative point of contact to progress the case or document their date of return if no alternative contact can be provided.
  • If the customer’s OOF is unexpected:
    • Check the customers OOF message for an alternate contact or date of return.
    • Look for an alternate contact in the case details.
    • Where an Incident Manager (IM) is listed in the case details, consider contacting them for assistance in establishing a plan for the case.

 

Customer’s Date of Return

No case should be kept open longer than 1 week without progress. When the customer’s return is greater than 1 week:

  • Premier: Contact the IM and request their assistance in creating a plan for the case.
  • Non-Premier: Customers or partners, if their return date is greater than 1 week, initiate the unresponsive customer procedure.

No date of return or alternative contact

  1. Communicate and document a customer commitment for 1 week's time. Should the customer not be able to meet this commitment, follow KB 4457094 Procedure: Unresponsive Customer.
  2. Where an alternate contact cannot be established or the customer’s return date is unknown, follow KB  4457094 Procedure: Unresponsive Customer.
