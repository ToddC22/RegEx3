1. Overview

Important

***Xbox Design Lab controllers for Xbox One stopped taking orders on October 13, 2020.***

Xbox Design Lab controller is a controller that Xbox customers can design to fit their personality. Here are the key takeaways of the new controller:

  • Xbox Design Lab controller is a customizable Wireless controller for Xbox One. Refer to https://xboxdesignlab.xbox.com/, to view over a billion possible color combinations!
  • For sale online only through Xbox Design Lab and only available in the US, CA, PR and throughout EMEA.

Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lichtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, UK, United States (& Puerto Rico), Canada

  • The controller pricing starts at $69.99 in the US and $84.99 in CA.
  • Starting prices will vary in EMEA countries. Customers have the option to add different add-ons and engraving, for an additional cost.
  • This controller has the same specifications as the Xbox One Wireless controller.

2. Contact handling and customer scenarios

Note: Follow the standard escalation process if you are unsure about how to handle a customer scenario.

  • There is no purchase on behalf of (POBO) customer
    • Since the controller is only sold online at https://xboxdesignlab.xbox.com/, advocates cannot POBO these controllers. Use the import cart feature or complete an order that was already started by a customer.
    • If customers need sales assistance guide them to https://xboxdesignlab.xbox.com/, so they can customize and purchase their controller. Advocates can offer to help customers through the online process, but you cannot complete or place the order for them.
  • Return Policy
    • There are no remorse returns allowed for this controller. It cannot be returned because it is customized specifically for the customer.
  • Cancellation Policy
    • Orders that have been submitted by customers cannot be cancelled because the design is immediately sent to the factory to be built.
      • If the customer calls wanting to make changes to their order, cancel their order or update the shipping address, remind customers that once the order is submitted, it cannot be changed or cancelled. There isn’t a way to make any changes to the order once it has been placed.

Customer scenarios

Scenario

Advocate action

The customer says they received and were charged for a Design Lab controller even after they contacted CSS to try to cancel the order.

Advocates need to inform the customer that there are no changes, cancellations, or returns on Design Lab controller orders once the order is placed.

The customer says the Design Lab controller they received is different than the one they ordered.

Since the product is customized, customers cannot exchange this product online or at B&M Microsoft Stores. Refer to KB 3055957 Procedure: Original Xbox Support Boundaries for support boundaries and how to escalate.

The customer says the Design Lab controller is broken and does not work.

Xbox support: Assist the customer with a warranty exchange. When the defective controller is received at the warehouse, it will trigger a new controller exchange to be created with the exact same design.

Sales and B&M support: The customer cannot return their Design Lab controller at a B&M Microsoft Store. Refer customers to Xbox support at support.xbox.com for resolution.
