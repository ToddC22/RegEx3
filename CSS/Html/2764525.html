1. Overview

Officially, the manufacturer's warranty on the Surface is only valid if the customer’s device was officially released in the customer’s country AND purchased through an authorized retailer. For official warranty documents see Warranties, Terms & Conditions.

Microsoft is neither required nor obligated to provide warranty service options to customers who purchased their device outside of the country it was originally intended.

  • For example, while Japan is an officially launched country, a device sold in the United States, if used in Japan is not officially covered by the terms of the manufacturer’s warranty as the United States warranty does not cover usage in Japan.
  • Microsoft may offer warranty options for Surface devices that are outside the warranty coverage, as a customer courtesy.
  • This offer is not a customer entitlement and is subject to change at any time.
  • We currently provide out of region replacement support if the customer resides in an official launch country or a non-launch EU/EFTA country (with some exceptions below). See the specific regional information for your customer’s country in the following regional articles:
    • KB 4514977 Procedure: Surface: Services Exchange and Repair Offers Overview

Customer has China region device but lives in another country

Out of region support turned off for China Market, our tools will initiate below messaging for a device sold/purchased in China where a customer attempts a return from outside of China.

Out of region service for this device is unavailable

  • Thank the customer for contacting Surface and inform them that our records show the service warranty on this device can only be applied in the country of purchase in compliance with Microsoft's standard manufacturer's warranty.
  • Assist the customer by asking them where they had purchased their device and point them in the right direction.
  • If the customer mentions how they were able to do this in the past, inform them that you are required to follow the warranty policy for out of region devices and apologize for the inconvenience.
  • If pressed, follow the standard escalation process and let the customer know that we will get back to them.
  • Close and thank the customer for being a Surface fan.

Messaging:

Q: Why can I not get service for my device purchased in China in X country?

A: "Microsoft strives to get our customers back up and running as quickly as possible by extending additional support options whenever possible. These options are based on availability of in-country options."

Only if pressed (reactive):

"For Microsoft to comply with local trade regulations, we are not able to ship or pick up your device to China for it be repaired. Current warranty terms and conditions do not extend service for devices purchased abroad."

China – Exceptions FAQ:

Q: Why are you servicing Hong Kong/Taiwan devices only? What about the one I purchased in country X, not Hong Kong or Taiwan?

A: “Microsoft strives to get our customers back up and running as quickly as possible by extending additional support options whenever possible. These options are based on availability of in-country inventory.”

  • Only if pressed (reactive): “While we hope to provide this support in the future, current warranty terms and conditions do not extend service for devices purchased abroad.”

Q: Why are you limiting service options to [devices listed]?

A: “Microsoft strives to get our customers back up and running as quickly as possible by extending additional support options whenever possible. These options are based on availability of in-country inventory.”

Q: On my Hong Kong/Taiwan device, why are you reimaging the device to Hong Kong/Taiwan and not to China?

A: “It is Microsoft policy to reimage devices based on the original locale of the device.”

Q: When my Hong Kong/Taiwan device is not repairable, you will send me a China device. Why not a Hong Kong/Taiwan device? I want the TPM chip.

A: “For Microsoft to comply with local trade regulations, we are only able to offer a China device.”

Q: Since you can’t provide what I’m asking, I would like a refund. Can you process a refund?

A: “We are unable to provide a refund.”

  • Only if pressed (reactive): “We are providing you what we can as a courtesy. Current warranty terms and conditions do not extend service for devices/covers purchased abroad.”

Q: What is the warranty after servicing?

A: “It is 90 days or remainder of warranty – whichever is longer."

2.4 Saudi Arabia and UAE (United Arab Emirates) handling

Only the below configurations will be supported OOR.

This means that if there’s a customer with a US version of said configurations, it can be replaced for an equivalent device released in UAE/Saudi Arabia.

If the customer has a 1TB device, it means it can’t be replaced as UAE/Saudi Arabia never released it.

  • SP4 i5/4/128GB
  • SP4 i5/8/256GB
  • SP4 i7/8/256GB
  • SP4 i7/16/512GB
  • SP4 Cover
  • Signature cover from outside of UAE/Saudi Arabia: Replaced with default BLACK SP4 cover.
  • Colors not released (something other than Black/Blue) in UAE/Saudi Arabia: Replaced with default BLACK SP4 cover.

2.5 Device type not released in customer's country

If a customer lives in an official launch country but that country does not officially sell the type of device or cover the customer owns, we cannot exchange the item.

  • This is because the exchange center does not stock replacements for what is not officially sold in that market.
  • India provides additional options for out of region Surface Pro 3 units owned by customers.

2.6 Special handling Surface 3 (4G LTE) or Surface Pro LTE (Model 1807)

Due to hardware differences around mobile connectivity, the version sold officially in country is what can be serviced or exchanged.

  • For example, a US version can only be exchanged in the US and the Europe version can only be exchanged within Europe.
  • Customers with the US version in Europe and vice versa will not be able to get their devices exchanged.

Scenario

Comments

Customer owns a Surface 3 (4G LTE)
  • US version can only be exchanged within the US.
  • Japan version can only be exchanged within Japan.
  • Europe version can only be exchanged within Europe.

Customer owns a Surface Pro LTE (Model 1807)
  • US version can only be exchanged within the US.
  • Japan version can only be exchanged within Japan.
  • Australia and New Zealand versions can only be exchanged within Australia and New Zealand.
  • Hong Kong versions can only be exchanged within Hong Kong (China exception does not apply while Model 1807 is not launched in China).
  • Europe version can only be exchanged within Europe.

Surface 3 (4G LTE), Surface Pro LTE (Model 1807) and lives outside of the officially released country

No service options.

3. Customer lives in a non-launch country

If the customer currently lives in a country where Surface is not officially sold, we do not accept exchanges for devices because there are no support options offered (that is, there is no call center or a local service center available).

  • For example, Surface devices are not officially sold in Cambodia or the Philippines. Therefore, customers who are in these countries cannot have their Surface devices exchanged within the country.

Return to top

3.1 General options

Customer visiting a launch country
  • If the customer is visiting a launch country, they can send the device in for repair. Refer to turn around times (TaT) in the relevant KB to make sure the customer will be able to get the device back while they are still in the launch country.
    • KB 4514977 Procedure: Surface: Services Exchange and Repair Offers Overview

Friends or family who live in a launch country
  • If the customer isn’t visiting a launch country but has friends or family who lives in a launch country, they can set up the service on behalf of the customer. In this scenario, sending the device to and from the non-launch country to friends or family in a launch country and then to the exchange center and back is the customer's responsibility.
  • Microsoft will only pay for shipping to and from the exchange center and an address in an official launch country.
  • There is no option for a customer in a non-launch country to ship directly to an exchange center.
  • If the customer resides in a non-launch EU/EFTA country, see the section below.

4. Messaging

Use the messaging below for customers in launch countries AND non-launch EU/EFTA countries:

"The Surface warranty is only valid in the country listed in the warranty for that Surface device for customers who purchase the device from an authorized retailer. Microsoft is not required to provide repair service, either in warranty or out of warranty (paid service) to customers who use Surface devices in a country not covered by the warranty. From time to time, Microsoft may offer service for Surface devices that are outside the warranty coverage as a customer courtesy. This offer is subject to change at any time and may involve a fee for service."

Use the messaging below for customers in non-launch countries:

"The Surface warranty is only valid in the country listed in the warranty for that Surface device for customers who purchase the device from an authorized retailer. Microsoft is not required to provide repair service, either in warranty or out of warranty (paid service) to customers who use Surface devices in a country not covered by the warranty."

If the customer rebuts based on previous experience:

"From time to time, Microsoft may offer service for Surface devices that are outside the warranty coverage as a customer courtesy. This offer is currently unavailable."
