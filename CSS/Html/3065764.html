What is this article about?

This is a side navigation article that discusses the tool usage for Membership Support Tool (MST) within ASD. MST is used for  customer support sessions to have a unified view of the product, services, and history for each customer.

MST allows advocates to perform support actions without using other tools, for example refunding orders, managing subscriptions, and other things. Be sure to review your support boundary guidelines and other procedure articles to understand what you can and should be doing. Availability of this tool does not constitute permission to use that feature on behalf of a customer.

Note: Not all scenarios presented in this TUG are appropriate for your line of business.
