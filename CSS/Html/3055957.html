3. Original Xbox support

Note:

Due to the age of the original Xbox console (2001), we have discontinued support, repairs, and exchanges, as well as the distribution of accessories and games. Refer customers to Original Xbox console: FAQ.

  • Because Original Xbox accounts are no longer supported, gamertags are unrecoverable (it is not associated with and cannot be associated to a Microsoft account).
  • These accounts can be easily identified as ASD shows the customer’s gamertag as their Microsoft account:
[Asset 4553188]
  • Messaging:

"I apologize for the inconvenience, but your original Xbox gamertag cannot be recovered, as it was created prior to the new Xbox Live service. You may create a new gamertag, but the original gamertag is unusable."

4. Xbox Design Lab custom Xbox One controller

See xboxdesignlab.xbox.com/ for information on how to purchase an Xbox Design Lab controller or frequently asked questions (FAQ).

Note:

Do not refer customers to a Brick and Mortar (B&M) store under any circumstances. Stores cannot help with Xbox Design Lab controllers. Customers must purchase the controllers online themselves.

Can I return my order? (The colors don't look like the one I ordered.)

If the customer states the Xbox Design Lab controller they received isn’t the one they ordered or there is something wrong with it, determine if this is a warranty claim or if the customer received an incorrect controller.

If you determine this to be a warranty claim, direct the customer to support.xbox.com to make the claim or transfer the customer to Xbox CSS.

If the customer confirms the controller is different than the one they ordered:

  1. Send an email to the customer via ASD and ask the customer to reply to that email by attaching three clear photos of the controller:
    • One photo of the front
    • One photo of the back
    • One photo of the numbers on the sticker inside the battery case (must be readable).
  2. Transfer the case to a Microsoft-based Advanced Capability Advocate (ACA).
  3. ACA determines if the controller matches the one ordered.
    • If it matches a hard message is delivered to the customer regarding our no returns policy. No further action.
    • If it is different, ACA consults FT4 via TFS.
  4. FT4 validates the number inside the battery cover matches the SAP Order number for the order.
  • If it matches, the refund is approved by FT4 and the TFS bug is reassigned back to ACA. ACA refunds the original order.
  • If it is different, FT4 determines if the customer is committing fraud.
  • Call Type: Xbox One Hardware
  • Platform: Xbox One
  • Why the customer called: I need help/information about Xbox Design Lab controller order.
  • Hardware Products: Controller Xbox Design Lab
  • Root Cause/Issue: Customer received broken/damaged device.

The controller I received did not have the design I originally selected. What can you do to help?

If the customer claims the Xbox Design Lab controller they received isn’t the one they ordered or there is something wrong with it determine if this is a warranty claim or if the customer did received the incorrect controller.

If it is a warranty claim, direct the customer to support.xbox.com to make the claim, or transfer the customer to Xbox.

If the customer confirms the controller is different than the one they ordered:

  • One photo of the front
  • One photo of the back

  1. Transfer the case to ACA.
  2. ACA determines if the controller matches the one ordered.
  • If it matches a hard message is delivered to the customer regarding our no returns policy. No further action.
  • If it is different ACA consults FT4 via TFS.
  1. FT4 validates the number inside the battery cover against the SAP Order number for the order.
  • If it matches the refund is approved by FT4 and the TFS bug is reassigned back to ACA. ACA refunds the original order.
  • If it is different FT4 determines if the customer is committing fraud.
  • Call Type: Xbox One Hardware
  • Platform: Xbox One
  • Why the customer called: I need help/information about Xbox Design Lab controller order.
  • Hardware Products: Controller Xbox Design Lab
  • Root Cause/Issue: Customer received the wrong order.

My controller is defective; can I return it?

Follow the standard controller troubleshooting steps. If troubleshooting does not resolve the issue, use the activation date for the controller (found in the Controller Detail tab in MST) to determine the warranty status of the controller and create an accessory exchange order.

If the controller cannot be repaired or there is physical damage to the controller that occurred in the shipping process, advise the customer that that we can send a replacement of the exact same design.
  • Call Type: Xbox One Hardware
  • Platform: Xbox One
  • Why the customer called: I need help or information about Xbox Design Lab controller order.
  • Hardware Products: Controller Xbox Design Lab
  • Root Cause/Issue: Customer received a broken or damaged device.
  • Solution Delivered: Troubleshoot – Informed customer of policy.

My controller is defective; can I get a replacement?

Follow the standard controller troubleshooting steps. If they do not resolve the issue, use the activation date for the controller (found in the Controller Detail tab in MST) to determine the warranty status of the controller and create an accessory exchange order.

If the controller cannot be repaired or if there is physical damage that occurred during shipping, inform the customer that we can send a replacement of the exact same design. If the customer would like a replacement, follow the steps in MST.

The controller ID is above the barcode in the battery chamber of the controller: XXXXXXXXXX-XXXXXX (10 numbers, then 6 numbers) Make sure there are no dashes entered into the controller ID pop-up.

Note: It is crucial that you collect the correct address of the customer for any replacements on the Xbox Design Controller. Due to the customization of the product, if there is an address error, the controller will be returned to Microsoft creating a long delay in sending the customer the controller again.
  • Call Type: Xbox One Hardware
  • Platform: Xbox One
  • Why the customer called: I need help/information about Xbox Design Lab controller order.
  • Hardware Products: Controller Xbox Design Lab
  • Root Cause/Issue: Customer received broken/damaged device.
  • Solution Delivered: Created fulfilment for Controller (In Warranty).

or

  • Solution Delivered: Troubleshoot – Informed customer of policy.

I purchased a Design Lab – Event Exclusive controller that is defective; can I get a replacement?

(Purchased at E3 or another event directly from Microsoft.)

Event Exclusive controllers are identifiable by the controller ID which is above the barcode in the battery chamber of the controller. The words Event Exclusive are listed in place of numbers, preventing customers from using online repair tools.

If the controller cannot be repaired through troubleshooting or there is physical damage to the controller due to shipping, inform the customer we are unable to send a replacement of the exact same design due to this being a limited production, event exclusive design.

Refund the customer's order via their Microsoft account in MST. The customer may keep their Event Exclusive controller as a souvenir and is welcome to place an order for a new Design Lab controller.
