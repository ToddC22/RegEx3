Services Partners

These partners address customer needs through design, advise, architecture, build, implementation, migration, integration and/or management of the solution. Services partners include SIs, MSPs, VARs, resellers, agencies, advisory firms and strategic consultancies.

ISV Partners

These partners build commercial (B2B) software-based intellectual property (IP) on our 3 clouds, sell those repeatable software solutions through direct/indirect channels, and monetize them primarily through software licensing models. Valid ISV examples include Adobe, iCertis, SAP, Citrix, and so on. This definition excludes (B2C) ISV solutions, customer-as-a-partner solutions that are not sold independent of the customer’s services or hardware offerings and excludes partners such as Netflix, who sell content vs software.

After every completed cadence call, the CED specialist needs to review the partner classification based on the partner’s behavior and progress in the CED program.

P1: High engagement partner = partner that actively works towards CED milestones, joins sessions, completes self-paced work, has demonstrated progress

P2: Medium engagement partner = partner working towards CED milestones, slow progress, occasionally misses cadence calls, CED needs to push and follow-up, but the partner reacts positively

P3: Low engagement and low progress = partner doesn’t complete self-paced work, misses cadence calls. These are the partners we work to either reactivate the interest in the program, or complete the drop process

Partners will be removed from the CED program after missing 3 consecutive cadences, provided the partner accepted the missed sessions, and the CED team completed the process to follow up on a missed cadence :

Strike 1 after initial failed communication, use templates included on link

Strike 2 after 72 business hours, use templates included on link

Strike 3 after 72 business hours, use templates included on link

Example:

  • Partner joined session on January 15 and agreed on session for January 30
  • Partner accepted and missed session on January 30
  • CED completes the 3 attempts to reschedule the missed session and partner is unresponsive

Outcome:

CED specialist will send to the partner the offboarding email. See template here.

CRM : use Drop taxonomy for services and cadence then close the Solution

Exception: Before completing the offboarding process for Cloud Competency and Co-Sell, the CED specialist must review the case with the Team Lead.
