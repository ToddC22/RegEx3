1. Details

Description

The product table below will help support engineers understand what Microsoft product support team is using which tool, so support cases can be properly transferred/collaborated among the support engineers.        

Note: M365 Commerce advocates use Rave Tool to handle Microsoft 365 /Dynamics /Intune /Azure AD customer engagement subscription and billing related Questions.

Cases are being transitioned to Dynamics platform over time. During the transition some cases may still be in Service Desk or may be moved to Service Desk due to technical challenges.
