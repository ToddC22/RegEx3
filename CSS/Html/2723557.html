Office 365 ProPlus is being renamed to Microsoft 365 Apps for enterprise. 
For more information about this change, read this blog post.

This article describes the technical support boundaries for the Office client applications that are included with Office 365 commercial subscription service plans. This article doesn't cover the boundaries for SharePoint Online (SPO), OneDrive for Business, Exchange Online, Skype for Business Online (formerly Lync Online), or Teams.


Note: This area is generally referred to internally as Microsoft 365 Apps for enterprise.


The Office client apps are included with some Office 365 commercial subscription service plans. The applications are regularly updated, but typically include Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and OneNote on many platforms including Windows, Mac, Mobile, and Tablet.


Some common plans that include the Office client apps are as follows:

  • Microsoft 365 Apps for enterprise
  • Microsoft 365 Apps for business
  • Microsoft 365 Business
  • Office 365 E3/E5
  • Microsoft 365 Business/E3/E5
  • Office 365 G3/G5
  • Office 365 A3/A5
  • Office 365 A1/A2 there is a self-sign up for the client for qualified educational institutions.
