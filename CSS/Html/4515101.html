5.3 US minor outlying states

Make sure the customer is aware of the exchange process prior to submitting their order. Once the order has been created and submitted, the customer will need to pack up their device using their own shipping materials. They will need to send the device to our United States Exchange Center using the courier/shipping service of their choice and at their own expense. The service center address is:

Microsoft Returns Center
9630 Plaza Circle
El Paso, TX 79927
USA

Make sure the customer is aware of the following:

  • The customer is responsible for the package until it reaches our exchange center. If there is an issue with the delivery, it is their responsibility to contact the courier/shipping service to locate the package.
  • For this reason, it is highly recommended that the customer familiarizes themselves with the courier policies/procedures in these situations.
  • We highly recommend that the customer use a courier service that provides tracking information.
  • Be sure to let the customer know that they should receive their replacement device five to seven business days after their original device has been received at the exchange center.
  • As the customer is responsible for shipping their device to the exchange center and can choose the courier service, we are not able to predict the total turnaround time.
  • The replacement device will be shipped back to the customer using FedEx Next Day service.

Once the device has been received, the customer will receive an email letting them know that it has been received that their replacement should be shipped within two business days.

Return to top

5.4 Fulfillment process

The exchange process for customers in US minor outlying states is the same as it is for customers in the continental United States.

Be aware that fulfillment orders will be shipped using FedEx SmartPost. The turnaround time for these orders is the same as fulfillment orders in the continental United States.

Return to top

5.5 US Military addresses (FPO/APO)

Surface devices and accessories are available to customers with Military addresses, and therefore, device exchanges and accessory fulfillment are also available to these customers.

The process for these exchange orders is different than for customers living in the continental United States.

  • Make sure the customer is aware of the exchange process prior to submitting their order.
  • Once the order has been created and submitted, the customer will be sent an empty box and a return mailing label. The customer will need to pack the device in the box and send it to the service center via Smart-Post.
  • Once the service center has received the device, they will notify the customer via email and ship the replacement within two business days.
  • The email will include a tracking number. The replacement will be sent to the customer via Smart-Post, so the customer will need to check both the FedEx site as well as the USPS.com site to track their replacement device.
  • Unfortunately, given the limitations of delivery to these international Military addresses, we cannot provide a turnaround time.

See section 3.2 Creating a repair order for customers at US Military addresses of KB 4053533 TUG: MST: Devices section.
