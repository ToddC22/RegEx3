1. ORST overview

Accessing ORST

RAVE Read Only: Search the customers Microsoft account. The tool options appear on the search results page.

RAVE Case Management: After adding the customer to a case, select their account. ORST is on the Tools tab.

[Asset 4532563]

ORST sections:

Geo Blocked Generates a new key redeemable in the country customer is currently residing. For more information about geo-blocking refer to KB 2967407 Procedure: Office: Geo-blocking.

MSA: Identifies the Microsoft account used to activate a specific Microsoft Office product key.

Make it Right: Used to generate new Microsoft Office product for Solve approved scenarios.

AVE: Used to help customers having issues activating Microsoft Office university using their product key and verification code.

2. Geo blocked tab

2.1 User interface presentation

UI element Description
  1. Pin / Product Key

Enter 5 x 5 redemption pin

  1. Country Redeem

The country from which the customer is attempting to redeem the product.
  1. Country Purchased

Select the country where the customer purchased the product.

Before generating a new PIN, you need to enter the country where the customer purchased the product.
  1. Email

Before generating a new PIN, enter the email address where the customer wishes to have the activation link sent. Remember to verify with the customer that you have entered the email address correctly.
  1. Preferred email language

Get the customer’s preferred email language before generating the new key.
  1. Retailer/Store purchased

Enter the name of the retailer the customer purchased Office from.
  1. Check Status button

After you have entered the required information select Check Status to check the status of the redemption product key.
  1. Generate PIN button

After checking status, this button will be available if the option to provide a new redemption product key exists.

  1. Resend Email button

Make sure the customer has checked their junk mail filter.

If an email with a new activation link was previously sent, you will be able to resend the email to the customer if they did not receive it. You can update the customer’s email address later if needed before resending.
  1. Reset button

Select to clear all the information previously populated in the tool.

2.2 Checking the Status

Once you select the status of the redemption pin, it will display one of the following results. Only if the status shows as geo-blocked will you generate a new pin.

Status

Meaning

Action

Invalid

The code the customer is providing is not correct

Have the customer read the number again. Numbers are alphanumeric 5x5

Created

Retailer did not activate the card

Have the customer try again after 24 hours from purchase

Activated

Code is valid. Customer can redeem the product key.

Customer must enter the product key at office.com/setup or office.com/verify (if Microsoft 365 Personal) to redeem Office product

Consumed

Product Key consumed.

Customer must go to Microsoft account, Installs page and sign in using the same Microsoft account used for the product redemption, and install the product.

Geo-blocked yes

Product key has been geo-blocked for redemption

If geo-block is yes, the status section will show up in the tool.

2.3 Generating a new PIN/Product key

  1. To generate a new pin/Product Key, select the country where the product was purchased.
  2. Enter the e-mail address where the customer would like the information on redeeming the new Pin/Product Key sent to
  3. Select the customer’s preferred language from the drop-down menu Preferred Email Language
  4. Select Generate PIN

Return to top

2.4 Resend PIN/Product key

If the customer did not receive the replacement key, you can resend it using the tool. Follow the below steps:

  1. In the Pin/Product Key field enter the original PIN/Product Key
  2. Enter the country of redemption
  3. Select Check Status
  4. Have the customer confirm their email.
  • If the e-mail is correct proceed to the next step
  • If the e-mail is incorrect, enter the corrected e-mail and proceed to the next step
  1. Confirm the Preferred Email Language
  2. Select Resend Email

Note: Make sure the customer checks their junk or spam mail.

Office Redemption Support Tool will also display messages above the fields and buttons indicating whether an action was successful or if there was an issue.

  • Messages in green text indicate success.
  • Messages in red indicate there was an issue.

Token generated:

  • Once you generate the new token in ORST the customer will receive an email containing an embedded link to redeem the token. 
  • The customer will need to select on the link and follow the instructions on the screen to redeem the token.
  • You can also resend the same token by using Resend Email option in the tool.
    • Ensure customers email id is correct.
    • Check junk/spam mail folders.

3. MSA tab

Use this tab to identity the Microsoft account used to redeem a specific Microsoft Office 5 x 5 pin.

Get information below using DTM

  • Product Key card screenshot/pic
  • Proof of Purchase (should have the below info)
    • Name of retailer
    • Invoice / Bill number
    • Purchase amount
    • Date of sale
    • Product description

Check if the serial number on the POSA card matches the serial number on the POP/Invoice. If it does, enter the product key, the customer’s first and last name, the date when the customer originally activated Microsoft Office and their phone number.

  • If the customer doesn’t remember the exact date that they redeemed it, you can use the Geo-block tab in ORST to get that information.

Japan specific instructions:

  • If the customer has PIPC Office redeemed and forgot their Microsoft account, get the POP/invoice of the PC, which has the OEM PC model name.
    • If it’s not mentioned, then get a picture of the label on the device that could tell us the OEM PC Model name.
  • Check if that PC model comes with Office PIPC. If yes, use the same process as above to retrieve the associated Microsoft account.

Return to top

4. Make it right tab

Use this tab to generate new Microsoft Office product keys for specific exception scenarios. You can only use this tab to send tokens if there is a documented scenario found in either:

  • KB 3203023 Procedure: Office: Office Solve scenarios (Goodwill version)

  • KB: 3076290 Procedure: Office: Office Solve scenarios

Every advocate who uses ORST should be familiar with the Information and procedure sections discussed in both articles. Monitoring efforts are in place to ensure compliance to procedure. Failure to do so may cause your team to get a CAA.

Country Redeemed and Country Purchased

For Country Redeem and Country Purchased, choose the countries where the customer resides.

Email

Enter the customer’s email and then check history. The email in most cases should be the Microsoft account the customer has or wishes to have their Office product associated to.

Preferred Email Language

Select the Preferred Email Language of the customer you are assisting.

Retailer/Store Purchased

If applicable to the scenario, add the retailer where the customer purchased.

Make it Right SKU

You must only select the SKU as detailed in the scenarios from the KBs above. There are no exceptions.

Pin Generation Reason

Unless otherwise directed in the scenarios, always select Other. Once selected the Comments box will open. Enter the comments found in the KB.

SRID

If not already provided automatically for you, enter the case number.

Check History

Use this to see if the customer has already received a token from us before.

Generate MIR Pin

Inform the customer that they will receive separate emails from Microsoft Online Customer Service support orst2@microsoft.com. These emails contain information about how to redeem the token.

Resend Email

Select this function if the customer says they never received the email.

Return to top

5. AVE tab

Use AVE to help customers that are having issues activating Microsoft Office University by entering their product key and verification code. You will need to enter the customer’s product key and choose the country, city, school name and school email address, and select the reason for failed verification and then Get AVE. This will provide you with a new activation verification code, sent to the customer by email. Enter the customer’s product key, choose the country, the school name, and school email address.

  • Choose the reason for failed verification
  • Select Get Ave. This will send an email to the customer with their new activation code.
  • Choose the reason for failed verification
Return to top
