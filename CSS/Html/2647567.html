Team Name Change – Global Financial Services

The team discussed in this TSG, Global Financial Services (GFS) was formerly known as Global Credit Services (GCS) or Worldwide Online Credit Services (WOCS).

Note: Some queues, email aliases, and documentation might still retain the team’s older names.

 

Overview

This TSG provides guidance on how to escalate issues to the Global Financial Services (GFS) team and assist customers with commerce issues.

GFS handles refunds for invoice customers, requests to sign vouchers, and invoice payment inquiries beyond the scope of what ASMS supports.

Note: The guidance in this TSG is for legacy customers.
 

Policy

  • GFS is responsible for engaging the SAP team for SAP issues.
  • The GFS team is expected to respond to tasks within two US business days.
  • GFS is available 24 hours a day, 5 days a week during US business days (24/5).
  • The following table details the expected GFS response times on a per-scenario basis.

Making an Invoice Payment

Invoice payments must be paid by wire transfer or check. For more information on paying through wire transfer, see TSG 2658076.

Invoice customers may contact support asking if they can pay using a bill pay system. Advise the customer that bill pay systems must be able to generate and mail a physical check.

Note: Most bill pay systems generate a check when the payee is not on the system’s list of payees. Using a bill pay system to pay multiple invoices at one time can be a challenge because most systems do not provide a way to add a note to the check specifying what the payment is for.
 

Regenerating an Invoice

Under exceptional circumstances, a one-time invoice can be regenerated for customers.

If you receive a case from a customer requesting an invoice be regenerated, follow the guidelines in TSG 2651579.
 

Wire Transfers

For credit card customers paying by wire transfer, follow the process outlined in TSG 2651536.

Note: One-time wire transfer payments are not available for Modern invoice credit card customers.

For more information on wire transfer accounts and payment addresses, see TSG 2658076.
 

Engagement and Escalation Path

GFS Team Engagement

If you have a case related to the following scenarios, engage the GFS team by sending a collaboration task using the template under the applicable scenario that best aligns with your customer’s situation. Be sure to include detailed information and a summary of what is expected from GFS.

Note: Tasks will need to have their routing overridden using the queue specified in the scenario.

There may be times that GFS will need additional information but will close the task. In these instances, when you respond, send the task back to the queue (not the previous GFS agent directly).

Refunds for Checks or Wire Transfers

Use this template when there is a need to process a check or wire transfer refund.

Note: Refunds for invoice payments should be attempted in the Customer Management Agent Tool (CMAT) prior to engaging GFS to process a refund. You can apply a credit to the account, and if that credit results in a zero balance then anything remaining from the credit will get processed as a refund check or wire transfer by GFS. For guidance on handling credits in CMAT, see TSG 3084129.

After the refund has been submitted to Payment Central for AP processing, GFS will provide information to track the status, including a transaction ID. For more information on refund status inquiries, see the GFS Refund Status section.
