Summary

Who should follow this procedure?

  • Any FTE supporting Office 365 (CSS Only)

  • Any vendor or advocate supporting Office 365 (CSS Only)

  • Frontline who have requested access to the submission form (see procedure for frontline)

  • Any Frontline Manager (CSS Only)

What is this procedure about?

  • This procedure describes how individuals (FTE, Vendor and Frontline) who support Office 365 within CSS can request an extension when an Office trial account (used exclusively for demoing and testing Office 365) expires.

When should this procedure be followed?

  • All individuals within CSS who are supporting Office 365 should always follow this procedure when requesting a trial extension.

  • Please request at least a week before the trial is set to end to give the team enough time to extend the request without it impacting business processes.

Assumptions

  • MS internal employees who are not within the CSS organization or do not meet the criteria will go to the Office 365 Demo site where they can create a 90-day demo account. The Office 365 Demo site is here: https://demos.microsoft.com.

  • Individuals supporting Office 365 within CSS:

    • Created the trial account explicitly for testing and demoing purposes and the trial is approaching the expiration date. 

    • Will no longer contact customer and partner care (CPC) by telephone to request trial extensions.

    • Will no longer be required to complete the request form used prior to 8/17/2015.

    • Will not email O365trialext@microsoft.com for trial extensions requests.

    • Will use the new form at https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/teams/Office356TrialExtensions instead.

    • Trial cannot be in deprovisioned state. It is not possible to extend trials once they enter this state.

    • Have requested access to the form (please see procedure for frontline).

  • This procedure does not apply to partner tenants or expired IUR accounts. Partners with expiring trial or IUR accounts must be directed to partner support.

Procedure for Frontline (@mssupport.microsoft.com / @partners.microsoft.com)

  • You may not submit a trial extension request until you have requested access to the site. When prompted, please apply and we will approve the request within 1 business day.
  • When approved and submitting your request, you are required to have your manager’s alias filled in on the form in the designated box.

Procedure

 When your trial is set to expire/roll into Grace Period (at least a week before the trial ends), within the next month, you can request a trial extension as detailed below:

  1. Make sure the technical contact email on your domain is set to your @microsoft.com, @mssupport.microsoft.com, @partners.microsoft.com, or @office365support.com alias.
  2. Company name is set as MSTEST_CSS_XXX_ALIAS, where XXX is a 3 code identifying the supplier you belong to (Concentrix CNX, TekExpert TEK, Gig-Economy GIG), like:

    • MSTEST_CSS_CNX_myalias for Concentrix

    • MSTEST_CSS_GIG_myalias for Freelancer

    • MSTEST_CSS_MS_myalias for MSFTE

  3. When you login with your admin account, go to Admin center > settings on the left panel > organization profile > at the top of the page you should see your tenant name > there will be an edit button beside it and that’s where you’ll see the option to change the technical contact associated with the tenant.

Note: The user must have a valid Microsoft alias regardless of which domain the user belongs to. However, the supplier name MUST be provided when the request is sent.

  1. Go to https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/teams/Office356TrialExtensions and fill out the extension request form. (Emails to O365trialext@microsoft.com will no longer be fulfilled and we will only be monitoring the form submissions starting January 22nd, 2017.)
  2. Please allow 7-10 business days to process your request. Please do not continue emailing the inbox checking on your requests as it can further delay response time and clog communications. We are working as quickly as possible to fulfill all the extension requests as they come in.
  3. The responder will extend the trial account for one year and send confirmation to the requestor.
  4. Subsequent annual extensions are permitted but will require the request per above to be submitted annually.

Guidance

  • DO NOT create test cases from Test Tenants. Rave does not have a test environment, every test case created will be routed to production and may incur cost.

  • Tenants can hold multiple SKUs in one test account.

  • Engineers should only have one test tenant.

  • No extension of subscriptions for products the engineer does not support.

  • Once a trial has entered deprovisioned state, we are unable to extend the trial.

  • Must use the website form provided to have request completed.

Questions and Comments

If you have questions or are part of the exceptions lists from prior agreements and are unable to fill out the form, please email O365trialext_help@microsoft.com for further assistance.
