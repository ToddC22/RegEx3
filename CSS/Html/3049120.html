2.2 Assure auto-renew change

Customers who purchased Assure on the Modern platform and turned on set auto-renew, will not have the discounted price when renewed. Details are below:    

  • [Modern] For customers who purchased Assure from Membership Support Tool (MST) or Microsoft Store online on or after 3/19/2018 and have auto-renew turned on, their Assure subscription will be renewed with full price.
  • [Modern] For customers who purchased an Assure token at Microsoft Store Retail and turned on auto-renewal, their Assure subscription will be renewed with full price. 
  • [Legacy] For customers who purchased Assure from the Answer Desk App or Microsoft Store online before 3/19/2018 and have auto-renew turned-on, their Assure subscription will be renewed with 10% discounted price (no change).

See section 4.4 for information on identifying whether the customer's purchase is through [Modern] or [Legacy] in the Subscription Type field in MST.

3. Software Support offers 

3.1 Software Support offer availability and pricing

  • There are three main Software Support offerings:
    • Assure: Annual support subscription.
    • Premium Software Support: One-time paid support.
    • Assure Upgrade:  within 30 days of one-time paid support purchase, a customer can upgrade to an Assure subscription by paying the difference.
  • See the Price List site for offerings by market.
  • You can also reference pricing in MST by selecting country and local in the Orders section after verifying the customer (see KB 4053527 TUG: MST: Purchase on behalf of (POBO)).
  • Customers can find out more about our software support offers via support.microsoft.com at: https://support.microsoft.com/help/4467230.

3.3 Technical requirements for Software Support

  • A customer must be verified as the account owner before they can make a purchase. See KB 4506610 Procedure: Microsoft account verification.
  • Microsoft account. 
  • Broadband internet connection.
  • Windows 10, Windows 8, or Windows 7.
  • Microsoft Edge or the most current version of Internet Explorer available for a supported operation system, or Firefox 3.6 or higher.
  • 300 MHz or higher processor clock speed recommended, 233 MHz minimum required (single or dual processor system).
  • Intel Pentium/Celeron, or AMD K6/Athlon/Duron, or compatible processor recommended.
  • 256MB RAM or higher recommended (Minimum may limit performance and some features).
  • SVGA (1024 x 768) or higher display.
  • Recommended use of default browser security setting levels.

3.6 Assure subscription 

  • Price: $149 (USD) per year; pricing for additional markets referenced here.
    • Note: Starting on July 1st 2019, there is a promotional price offered in the United States and Canada. This is a limited time offer, so continue to check back on price. The promotional price will be: $99 (USD) for United States and $130 (CAD) Canada.
  • One year of software technical support for Windows, Office.
  • Auto-renewal: Assure subscriptions have the auto-renewal option turned on by default when signing up. The Assure subscription automatically renews without interruption on the renewal date.
  • Support for software on up to five personal devices.
  • In-store personal training included (where accessible).
  • Microsoft account required.
    • It is not possible to transfer ownership of an Assure subscription to another Microsoft account. If a customer wishes to use Assure with another Microsoft account, they will have to return the original subscription and repurchase an Assure subscription under the preferred Microsoft account.
    • Assure cannot be digitally purchased as a gift. 
      • Assure annual subscriptions can be purchased at Microsoft retail stores in the US and Canada as a physical product which could be gifted. For details see KB 2846205 Procedure: Token Redemption and Support Scenarios for Assure subscription. Note: Since the closure of Microsoft retails stores, customers will no longer be able to purchase Assure as a physical product and/or have it gifted going forward.
  • An Assure subscription is associated with the customer’s Microsoft account and not their device. It is possible that the owner of the Microsoft account has multiple devices with different PIDs but benefits from having one Assure subscription associated with their Microsoft account.
    • The Microsoft account can have up to five alternate e-mails and five alternate mobile numbers for verification purposes however, ASD won’t show associated subscriptions when searching with secondary or another alias associated with the Microsoft account.
    • If a customer contacts us with a non-primary alias to redeem Assure services, request the primary email address to find the active subscription.

3.7 Assure upgrade

  • A customer who purchased a one-time Premium Software Support offer, can upgrade to an annual Assure subscription within 30 days of purchasing Premium Software Support.
    • To upgrade, the customer may purchase the Assure Premium Software Support plan by paying the difference between the Premium Software Support offer and the Assure Premium Software Support plan.
  • Markets in scope for an Assure Upgrade and pricing associated can be viewed here.
  • Assure upgrades can be made with the following steps:
  1. Access the customer’s account.
  2. Verify the account ownership.
  3. In the Orders section of MST, verify the purchase date for the Premium Software Support subscription is within 30 days.
  4. Select New Order in the Orders section and search for Assure Upgrade.
  5. Perform Purchase on Behalf of (POBO).

4.4 Assure auto renewal 

  • Assure has an auto-renewal option turned on by default to continue coverage without interruption on the renewal date.
    • Customers who purchased Assure from the Answer Desk App or Microsoft Store online before March 19th, 2018, and have auto-renew turned-on, will have a 10% discount when their subscription renews. Assure subscription will be renewed with a 10% discount.
  • Customers receive a reminder email prior to their renewal date.
  • Customers can turn on or off auto-renewal or change the payment method at any time through https://account.microsoft.com/.
  • If auto-renew is off at the time of expiration, the customer needs to purchase a new subscription separately.
  • To determine if a customer’s purchase is through [Modern] or [Legacy] review the Subscription Type field in MST.
