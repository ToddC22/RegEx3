3.2 Walk-in Center (WIC)

Walk-in centers (WIC) allow a customer to visit a local Microsoft authorized center to facilitate an exchange. Using the state and city, narrow down the WIC locations available in the fulfillment tool. Provide the selected WIC location verbally or in email.

Locations can also be found by visiting Find Surface Walk-in Center contact information.

In warranty: Customers who are in-warranty will only need to visit the WIC once the replacement has been shipped from the service center to the selected WIC. The WIC will only hold the replacement for seven business days before shipping it back to the service center.

Out of warranty: The out of warranty fee must be paid before a replacement unit is shipped. The customer must bring a Demand Draft or Banker’s Check to the WIC. The draft or check must be made out for the exact amount of the fee.

  • The Demand Draft/Bank Check name should be made out to: Microsoft Corporation India Pvt Ltd
  • The payable should be made out to the selected walk in center city (such as Delhi, Bangalore, Channai).

The fee can be found in Surface warranty information in the frequently asked questions about service section under the question How much does non-warrantied service cost? Out of warranty pricing is tax inclusive for India.

Once the fee has been paid the replacement will ship from the service center. The WIC will only hold the replacement for seven business days before shipping it back to the service center.

3.3 Email Print Label (SEI)

India E-way bill (e-permit), shipping label, and commercial invoice for Standard exchange

To be able to return the non-functioning device the customer will need to have following documents ready which will be included in a separate email to the customer:

  • Shipping label/airway bill
  • E-way bill (e-permit)
  • Commercial invoice (or declaration form).
  • Delivery Challan if product value exceeds 50.000 INR
  • Declaration Form if product value is below 50.000 INR
Select to expand image for example of e-way bill

[Asset 4519611]

 

Select to expand image example of delivery challan

[Asset 4519612]

 

Note that as per India regulations the E-way bill and Delivery Challan are only valid for 15 days after date of issue, which means that the non-functioning device or accessory needs to be handed over to our shipping partner (FedEx) no later than 10 days after the customer receives the documentation via email. In case the e-permit does not reach the customer or needs to be resent, or they can’t ship device within 10 days, this can be requested from amsindia.msft@ivytech.com.

FedEx will call the customer within two business days to schedule a pick-up for the non-functioning product. If the customer does not receive this call, contact FedEx at +9111-66207713 Monday - Friday during normal business hours.

Alternatively the customer can visit any FedEx drop off point by visiting https://www.fedex.com/locate/index.html?locale=en_IN. Ensure the FedEx location you choose accepts packages for drop off. Suitable locations have a last pickup time mentioned in the location description.

3.4 Advanced Exchange (SAD)

The service center will ship a replacement first. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, the customer should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the return instructions included to arrange collection.

Return to top

3.5 No Action Required (NCA)

The service center will ship a replacement and the customer is not required to return the non-functioning product.
