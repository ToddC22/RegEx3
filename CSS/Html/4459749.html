2. Frontline steps to be completed before sending a WT Missing Payment to MPN Billing team

      2.1. Check order status in ProSe. If there is no order - assist partner to create the order.

      2.2. Inform partner it may take up to 1 week for the payment to be processed if they used the correct Microsoft partner membership account.

If partner contacts the frontline team immediately after payment is completed by his bank, accept the escalation, collect the needed proof of payment, and send the escalation to MPN Billing team using the below template. (it is very important to identify the account were the amount was sent). Note that the MPN Billing team may not be able to find the payment immediately. In that case, please keep the escalation opened and follow up with MPN Billing team every 2 days (maximum 2 times).

If the correct Microsoft partner membership account is displayed on the proof of payment and MPN Billing is not able to find the payment, even if you tried 2 times, inform your TA about this situation.

 

      2.3. If the order is in OfflinePi tool, the status is Expired however partner sustains that he made the payment; please ask him to provide us some documents where Microsoft account # is displayed and also guide him to create a new order ID without paying the fee one more time.  Having these steps performed, you will be qualified to escalate the case to MPN Billing team and ask them to activate the membership.

4. Short Payments in PC

Please note that in case of short payment, partner must be asked to pay the difference amount however there are couple of exceptions:

  1. Partner is a WHT -> in this case please ask the partner to send us the certificate as a proof.
  2. The difference amount is less than 10% of unit price. (In EMEA we have a maximum difference accepted of 35 (local currency) for MAPS and 50 (local currency) for Gold/ Silver level)

5. Microsoft Payment Central (MPC) process

Very Important: This section is in Frontline support scope only for partners who don’t speak English, however it will help to have an overview about the process of collecting the private information aligned to the new GDPR rule. 

For Non-English partners – refunds scenarios, when the activity is dispatched to MPN Billing team, please add to your comments the language they speak. It could be something like: “The email sent by Microsoft@e-mail.microsoft.com, to the partner providing the MPC link should be in …. language. “   

Partners will receive an e-mail from Microsoft@e-mail.microsoft.com asking them to log in to the Microsoft Payment Central Payee Portal: https://paymentcentral.microsoft.com and fill out required details for the refund. The Payment Central profile must be completed within 30 days and within this time partner will receive 4 reminders. After that the link will expire, and the request will need to start from the beginning.

See Step by Step details on how Payment Central works for the customer in Microsoft Payment Central (MPC) ‘How To’ Guide.

Note: Microsoft Payment Central is available to be used for all regions and countries for both PMC (CC and Wire Transfer) and PC (CC–CyberSource and Wire Transfer) platforms.

Below is the sample email sent directly from Microsoft Payment Central to the customer (English):

Subject: Action Required Invitation for payee account set up

Dear (NAME),

Greetings from Microsoft! You have been invited to onboard as a payee. This will enable Microsoft to move forward with the release of a pending refund, rebate, or partner payment.

In order to receive this payment, please provide your payment information by completing the steps below within next two (2) business days.

Next Steps:

1. Go to Microsoft Payment Central

2. Sign in with the email address at which you received this message

3. Complete required information (To avoid the request to be rejected because of inconsistent information, please ensure that the information needs to be the same as that you provided the agent on the SR)

4. Submit

Note: If this email address does not correspond to a Microsoft Account, you will need to set up as a Microsoft Account/ Click "Sign Up Now" at the bottom of the login page to set up your Microsoft Account. For more information regarding Microsoft Accounts, visit our help page.

If you have any questions regarding your payee invite, please reach out to your Microsoft Business Contact/Customer Service Representative. If you require assistance using Microsoft Payment Central tool, please visit the Microsoft Payment Central Frequency Asked Question Page. Otherwise, you may Request Support.

Thank you for your cooperation in this process. We value your business and are diligently to release your pending payment.

Kind Regards,
Microsoft Accounts Payable Help Desk

How to help the partner to set up payee’s account in Microsoft Payment Central Portal

Note: The individual that has purchasing rights/role can add additional users to manage the profile by choosing Manage Users>Add new users.

1. Go to Microsoft Payment Central and select Contact Info to begin the process.

Note: If the partner receives an error when trying to sign in, whatever admin username they registered for O365 should work as a sign in for Payment Central.

 [Asset 4459750]

 

2. Enter the details of Contact Info

  1. Account Type: Individual, Organization
  2. Country/Region the Payee is based in
  3. Organization Type: Government Entity, Partnership
    or Other Corporate Form
  4. Click Next
 [Asset 4459751]

 

3. Enter the details of the Business profile

  1. Organization Information: Organization Name,
    Doing Business As (DBA) (optional) or DUNS number (optional)
  2. Contact Information (required)
    • Email Address – Business POC
    • Email Address – Accounts Receivable contact
    • Phone Number
    • Address (Address, City, State, Country, Postal Code)
  3. Click Finish

Note: Tax Information/ID is collected ONLY

If the bank country requires tax id to disburse funds to the Payee

For example:

If PayFrom Ireland (1062) to Ireland Payee – NO TAX ID is needed/collected.

PayTo UK – NO TAX ID is needed/collected.

 [Asset 4459752]

 

4. Enter the details of Bank profile and Bank Account sections

Bank Profile section:

  1. Bank Profile Namei.
  2. Bank Account location (country)

Note: Disbursement Currency should automatically populate or select the correct currency

  1. Payment Method: Check or Electronic Payment Transfer
  2. Click Next

Bank Account section:

  1. ABA/IBAN Routing Number
  1. SWIFT code (optional)
  2. Account Holder Name
  3. Account Type
  4. Account Number

Note: when the customer only has an 8-digit institution and transit number less than the required 9-digit number. The customer can just have to add zero before their transit in the field of Bank Account.

  1. Click Finish
