1. CED Initial Contact

The specialist will need to find the partner searching by their MPN ID within ConnectUs.

I. To do so he will have to open the tool using this URL: https://connectus.partners.extranet.microsoft.com/

II. After entering credentials please go to the WORKPLACE tab and choose the option “PARTNERS”.

[Asset 4491231]

III. Search the partner on the Search for records tab using the MPN ID or Company Name:

[Asset 4491232]

IV. After you find the company select the location where the partner it’s related; if there is no specific location within ConnectUs, you may select Head Quarters option; and then click on the partner’s company name.

[Asset 4491250]

V. After you’re on the partners profile, go to the arrow next to the company name and choose the option SOLUTIONS.

[Asset 4491251]

VI. On the search bar introduce the * and hit search, this will allow you to verify all solutions created for the partner, make sure there are no other CED solutions open.

[Asset 4491252]

VII. Then, click on the +ADD NEW SOLUTION option.

[Asset 4491253]

VIII. This is the info that you’ll see; you’ll need to fill all the required spaces next to the red star and also make sure that even if it’s not a “mandatory” space to create the solution all of it has to be completely filled out.

g. For the “Offerings” option, there’s always CED Engagement (Build & GTM).

2. CED Onboarding

After the recruitment process is completed, the partner starts with the Onboarding process; the specialist will need to create a new activity within the same initial solution. To do so please follow these instructions:

1. Within the Solution view scroll down to the “Activity Template” and fill up all the required fields and click on the “Create Activity” button: [Asset 4491267]

2. The General information should be filled up with the information described above, for the Contact Reason Codes 1, 2 and 3 choose the most accurate according to the results from the contact, make sure each attempt for contact has 72 hours in between:
