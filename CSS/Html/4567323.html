Procedure: Global: Contacting Global MPN, CSP, Co-sell and Commercial Marketplace Support teams in Service Desk

[Asset 4090831]

Who should use this article?

All Commercial Support roles and teams using Service Desk

What is this article about?

This article details how to transfer Service Desk cases to the Partner Frontline Support team for teams, including but not limited to the following:

  • Partner Center Technical Support (PCTS)
  • Developer Programs (main programs: Visual Studio Subscriptions, BizSpark and ADT4T)
  • Learning Programs (Courseware, Microsoft Certified Professionals - MCP product information/purchase outside PC, Microsoft Certified Trainers, Learning Download Center)
  • Volume Licensing
  • Surface Partners - Microsoft Authorized Device Reseller (ADR)
  • Azure (Identity, ASMS)
  • Office

This article details how to transfer Service Desk cases to the Partner Frontline Support team when the issue is determined to be one that they support, as follows:

MPN

  • Enrolment and re-enrolment inquiries for Network members, Action pack subscribers and Competency members
  • Renewal and billing inquiries Company Profile Management (association, MCPs management, account information updates)
  • Benefits Management (Licensing benefits and product usage, How to)
  • Competency Requirements
  • Partner Portal Site Navigation/Walkthrough (including Partner Center for MPN, Partner University, etc.)
  • Technical support options obtained through Action Pack or competency
  • Escalation of partner program issues

CSP

  • Enrolment and sign-up inquiries for all CSP partner types
  • Billing queries (e.g. payment, tax, credits)
  • CSP licensing
  • CSP customer management
  • CSP subscription management

Co-sell

  • Business profile in Partner Center/ Partner Finder
  • Leads in Partner Center
  • Co-sell opportunities
  • Deal Registration
  • Analyze: Co-sell Opportunity and business profile insights

Commercial Marketplace

  • Partner wants to understand more about the Commercial Marketplace offering and enrollment.
  • Partner wants to understand more about Marketplace Rewards program.
  • Partner is trying to create, publish or manage an offer via Cloud Partner Portal or Partner Center.
  • CSP needs help with marketplace purchase.
