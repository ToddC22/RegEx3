1. What is the education or military discount?

Students, parents, teachers, plus members of the armed forces (active, former and retired) and their families save 10% on select products from the Microsoft Store. Education or military discount* only valid on select products, and may not be combinable with other offers. Education discount available to K-12 and higher education students, faculty, and parents. Military discount available to active, former, and retired military members, and their families. Microsoft reserves the right to modify or discontinue the terms of these offers at any time. Other exclusions and limits may apply.

False representations of eligibility voids this offer, and Microsoft reserves the right to collect the full price of product(s) ordered. Bulk orders not valid and may be cancelled. Number of products per purchase may be subject to limitations. Personal use only. Public entities, government entities, nonprofit organizations, educational institutions, enterprise purchasers, and business purchasers do not qualify.

While not a requirement to benefit from student or military offer, bringing an ID to the B&M store when placing purchase will guarantee the discount.

*Only military customers in US, UK, or IE are eligible for a military discount.

1.1 What is not eligible for the education or military discounts? 

The products below are not eligible:

  • Windows and software products (digital games, digital apps, in-app content and subscriptions, movies, TV shows, and associated content).
  • Digital apps, games, in-app content and subscriptions, movies, TV shows, and associated content.
  • Gift cards and service/subscription cards (for example, Skype or Xbox Live).
  • Products that have been personalized or customized.
  • Xbox consoles, games, and accessories.

1.2 Storefront experience

  1. Customers will see EDU/MIL messaging on the product page:

Special pricing for eligible students, parents, teachers and members of the Armed Forces. May not be combinable with other offers.

CHECK YOUR ELIGIBILITY >

  1. By selecting Check your eligibility, the customer will need to sign into the flow with their Microsoft account. Once logged in, the customer will need to go through Microsoft account verification:

[Asset 4536831]

  1. Customers are presented with the following screen. They have an option to select here to read through the terms & conditions. Then select SHOP NOW to proceed.

[Asset 4536832]

  1. After selecting SHOP NOW, customers will see the following message:

[Asset 4536833]

  1. By selecting OK, the customer will go back on the store.com website. The customer can then add the product to their cart and finalize checkout. Strikethrough EDU/MIL pricing and an affirmation message will appear automatically on eligible products.

Note: In future, every time the customer comes back to store.com and logs in using the same Microsoft account they used for the verification flow, we will be recognizing their EDU/MIL status and will automatically show the prices with the appropriate discount on them.

Summary:

  • For EDU/MIL verification flow on Microsoft Store purchases AVE flow is not needed nor available.
  • Instead – store.com offers a low-bar verification flow for EDU/MIL, which does not require customer to use any other email address or proof other than their Microsoft account.
  • Microsoft account used in verification flow will be linked to EDU/MIL status for future visits to store.com (when signed into the Microsoft Store).

3. Academy Verification Engine (AVE) offers/promotions

Microsoft offers some specific promotions to students where AVE verification flow is needed to be performed to prove that the customer is eligible for an offer.

One of the examples of promotion where a customer would need to go through AVE flow is Microsoft 365 Education.

  1. The Get Started link will take customer to Microsoft 365 Education flow.

[Asset 4536837]

  1. Customers need to sign in with their school email address.
  2. Customers are given five different paths to verification and then a 5x5 verification code is emailed to their school email.

[Asset 4536838]

  1. They type in school email address and after selecting verify, a verification code will be sent to them.
  2. If they don’t have an email but know the school, they can verify with a pop-up window that will allow them to enter their school credentials.

[Asset 4536839]

  1. Customers can input their ISIC number.

[Asset 4536840]

  1. If the customers already received their 5x5 verification code (not their product key) and entered for verification.

[Asset 4536842]

  1. Customers can submit information to verify as well. This may take 3-5 business days.

[Asset 4536843]

  1. Once the customer receives the verification code, they are able to check out the product.
