AES: Process: Microsoft Advertising: Uploads (Bulk Campaign Management)

[Asset 4509089]

Who is this article for?

  • Back Office Support (BOS) advocates supporting the Advertiser Enablement Services (AES) program
  • Frontline teams (Microsoft Advertising sales and MAST Service/Support advocates) who work directly with Microsoft Advertising clients.

What is this article about?

For BOS advocates, this article covers how to process Uploads (Bulk Campaign Management) requests. The Back Office Support (BOS) team executes these requests as part of Advertiser Enablement Services (AES) program, using the Editor tool or Microsoft Advertising UI to make bulk changes or assignments to an advertiser’s Bing Ads account quickly and easily.

For frontline teams, this article covers an overview of the service, requirements for submitting requests, scope of service, and service level agreement.
