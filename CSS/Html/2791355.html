1. Overview

Partner communication of the FY19 incentives started on June 29th, with a direct mail to all incentives-eligible partners announcing that details are available on the Partner Incentives MPN portal, aka.ms/partnerincentives.

To view this page, you must be logged in, since it is behind the MPN program.

The MPN Partner Incentives portal contains content based on eligibility; only partners who have signed the MPN Agreement or are eligible for a specific program can download the content. All other people will get an 'Access Denied' message.

In order to be able to download the content partners should reach out to their Local Channel Development Manager (CDM) or Partner Development Manager (PDM) for specific questions related to their eligibility status.

This article is about the CSP Partners who are not yet onboarded on the CSP incentives programs. Partner Frontline Advocates (RSC) will need to assist partners on how to create a ticket online or provide the email contact of the incentives team.

4. OCI Escalation Paths﻿

If partners have already submitted a case and have not obtained a resolution or an update in more than 3 days, ask partners to use our geo-based Escalation Aliases:

Region OCI Contact – T2 (to be shared with Partners) T3 – Internal only (to be shared with Field and CFA)
North America NAOCCIES@microsoft.com  
EMEA﻿﻿ EOCCIESC@microsoft.com﻿﻿ ExecEscCIOPS@microsoft.com
LATAM LOCCIESC@microsoft.com  
Japan and APGC APOCCIES@microsoft.com  


Partner should include the COSMIC case ID on the email follow-up for reference. If partner has contacted the escalation alias however there is still no update, escalate the issue to your respective SDMs/TAs.

If partner already reached out to the geo-based escalation aliases listed above however has not received a response, MPN/CSP escalation specialist should update this tracker with all the necessary details so the case can be discussed on the weekly triage meeting with FL and Incentives team.

5. Ticket submission online

1. Partners should login to https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/support/contact-support

  •   Category : Partner Incentives
  •   Topic : Select as appropriate
  •   Issue : Choose the applicable issue. 

2. Select Submit issue.

 4012902

3. Enter contact information.

 4012903
          
4. Enter a title and description of the issue. If applicable, also enter the Incentive Claim ID. Click Submit when finished.

  4012904 

The partner will be contacted within 24 business hours.

Note: If there is an issue in using online incentives support process above, the RSC is able to route to the regional support contacts using the email addresses listed in section 3. For users authenticating with Azure Active Directory (AAD) through partner.microsoft.com, after they select "Incentives", they will be redirected to this new link in Partner Dashboard - https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/dashboard/incentives/claims/web/support.
