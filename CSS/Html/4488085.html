Summary

  • What: This scenario should be used when advocated from CS VL/Dev/Lex), TS, or Azure Support teams receive a VL, DEV or learning case which should be handled by the appropriate team e.g. VL, DEV or Learning team.
    Notice VL , DEV and Learning are 3 different teams with different SAPS.
    Example: case came into VL by mistake and needs to be transferred to Developer team.
  • Who:  This process should be followed by:
    • Tier 1 and Tier 2 Commercial CS
    • Technical Support Engineers,
    • Azure billing

Detailed Process Steps

The customer reports an issue and it is determined that the customer's issue is supported by either VL, DEV or Learnings.

Prior to Transfer

  1. Select Status: Identifying the issue
  2. Case Title:  Update using the following format:
    • "Misroute from <LEX/DEV/VL choose one>:  <Original  Case Title>"
  3. Notes:
    • Document that the question is outside the scope of the transferring team, and therefore you are transferring to the VL, DEV or Learning team (choose one)
    • In case of any doubts regarding support boundaries, please consult with Tier 2/ Team Management.
  4. Verify that the Country is correct in Service Desk, this will dictate to which queue the case is transferred.
  5. Verify the Language, which also detects the queue and in which language the customer will get the survey.
  6. Change the SAP of the case to reflect the new issue for which you are transferring the case:
    1. Product Family:  choose the applicable receiving team:
      • Volume Licensing
      • Learning
      • Developer, Student and Startup Programs
    2. Product:  choose the appropriate product, for example:
      • Volume Licensing:  Enterprise Agreement, Open License, Open Value, etc
      • Learning:  Courseware, MC/MCT, Imagine Academy, etc
      • Developer, Student and Startup Programs:  Visual Studio, Bizspark, etc
    3. Support Topic:  Choose the one which fits the issue
  7. Leave the RCA empty, otherwise you will be unable to transfer the case.
