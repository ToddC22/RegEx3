3.2 Email Print Label (SEI)

The customer will receive a separate email from UPS with a prepaid return shipping label and a commercial invoice to return the non-functioning product.

Customers should pack the non-functioning product into a sturdy box that they must provide before they drop-off the non-functioning item to the nearest UPS location.

Return to top

3.3 Prepaid Box with Label (SST)

Surface Studio only. The service center will ship a prepaid empty box to the customer within two business days. The empty box will contain a prepaid UPS shipping label and commercial invoice.

Return to top

3.4 Advanced Exchange (SAD)

The service center will ship a replacement first. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, they should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the prepaid UPS shipping label and commercial invoice.

Return to top

3.5 No Action Required (NCA)

The service center will ship a replacement and the customer is not required to return the non-functioning product.

5. New Zealand consumer law

New Zealand consumers have the right to repair, replace, or refund in certain situations. See KB 4100081 Guideline: Microsoft and the Australian and New Zealand Consumer Law. Where a consumer has that right to repair, replace, or refund, you must follow the New Zealand consumer law policy, and you must not charge or collect any fee for out of warranty (OOW) service.
