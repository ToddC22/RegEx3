What is this article about?

This article lists all the standard workflows (Lean or Rio) that are collected at the time for engineers. As new workflows continue to be added by each technology, please visit the source CSSWiki site (expand GeneralPages or SharedPages for your specific technology) for the latest update. If the Champions listed on the product have changed roles, please look up author and edit history in CSSWiki directly for information to request a workflow update. For Modern Workplace, many of the workflows have been integrated into Rave Diagnostics.
