AES: Process: Microsoft Advertising: Action Extensions

[Asset 4509089]

Who should use this article?

  • Back Office Support (BOS) advocates supporting the Advertiser Enablement Services (AES) program.
  • Frontline teams (Microsoft Advertising sales and MAST Service/Support advocates) who work directly with Microsoft clients.

What is this article about?

This article aims to educate readers about the Action Extension service utilizing the Microsoft Advertising UI, as executed by the BOS team. It contains step by step instructions of the processes required to execute this service for our BOS advocates. Additional resources are available at the end of this module.

For frontline teams, this article covers an overview of the service, requirements for submitting requests, scope of service, and service level agreement.

Summary of Service: Action Extensions

Action Extension allows advertisers to highlight a call to action button on advertiser’s ads. This action button entices end users to immediately click and interact with the ads. The Action Extension helps advertisers to increase conversions and bring more customers to their products and services.

[Asset 4513763]

The Back Office Support (BOS) team, as part of Advertiser Enablement Program (AES), offers Action Extension service that helps the Frontline Teams to propose, enable, edit, and delete Action Extensions on their behalf. Hence, they can focus more on their revenue-driving activities and customer-enabling tasks.
