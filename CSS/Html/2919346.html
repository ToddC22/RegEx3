[Asset 4049349]

Who should use this article?

What is this article about?

This article provides support boundaries for two aspects of Skype:

  • Skype commerce issues: Use this section for customers who have questions about their Skype accounts, request more information on Skype products, or have billing related issues.
  • Skype technical issues: This section applies for all usability and technical related questions as well as issues using Skype products.

To limit the number of redirects to Skype.com, advocates should handle the known scenarios outlined within this document.

2. Support channels and languages

Skype customer support is offered via live chat. We do not offer phone support and cannot offer a call back option.

Return to top

2.1 Handling requests for phone support

When a customer requests phone support, the advocate should explain that we only offer live chat or email support and we are not able to assist our customers via phone.

Advocates may use the following response, if applicable, to explain Skype’s support channels:

All of our Skype support is conducted by email and live chat, as we believe this is the most effective way to resolve our customers’ queries. It means you can get support whenever you are connected to the internet and can take more time to fully describe the problem you’re having. This enables us to make sure your issue is handled by the best person, so you get the right answers as quickly as possible.

Using the following link, you can contact us and you will be offered the appropriate support channel depending on your inquiry: https://login.skype.com/.

Live chat support, in English, is available on our website 24 hours a day, so if you are offered an option to contact via live chat, you can get a quick response and help from an English language support representative whenever you need it.

2.3 Handling customer contact in a non-supported language

  1. Advise customer to contact us (chat/email) in one of the supported languages, so that the appropriate language advocate can assist with their query.
  2. Once the customer’s preferred supported language has been identified, transfer the chat or email to the appropriate queue.
  3. Even if the advocate is able to write in the non-supported language, assistance should still be provided in one of the supported languages.
  4. Advise the customer that we are looking into adding more support languages in the future and apologize for the inconvenience.

Start making Skype calls (audio or video)

Primary

Yes

No

To have the best calling experience with Skype, users (both the caller and the person being called in case of Skype to Skype calls) should check the following:

  • Check their internet connection: favor a stable connection with enough bandwidth. For best results:
    • They should use a wired connection.
    • If they’re using a Wi-Fi connection, they should try moving closer to the router to get a better signal.
    • They should close any other programs that might be using their connection.
  • Check their computer: computer’s hardware and software can affect calls. For best quality:
    • They must use the latest version of Skype.
    • Check that they meet the minimum requirements to run Skype.
    • Make sure their computer is up-to-date and they’ve downloaded any operating system updates they need.
    • If possible, they should use a headset or a separate microphone and speakers.
  • Check your Skype settings.
    • Go to Tools > Options to check your audio and video settings.
  • Make a free test call.
    • Users can practice making a call and check that their speakers and microphone are working properly. Just type echo123 into the search field in Skype to get started.
  • Calling destination.
    • If the person they are trying to reach isn’t already in their contact list, they should add them first, and check that they’re online and available.
    • If they attempt to call a landline or mobile number, they will need enough Skype Credit or a subscription to call.
  • Get comfortable.
    • Users should choose a quiet, comfortable spot in a well-lit room.
    • For best video quality, they should use a light-colored background and make sure they don’t have bright lights right behind you.
    • For best audio quality, they should limit background noise so that their voice can be clearly heard.

For more information on how to make a Skype call, users can browse through the different FAQs available on the Skype support website (make sure they select their platform at the top of the page):

https://support.skype.com/skype/calling/make-skype-call

6.2 Partner service level agreement

Queries should be resolved within standard partner SLA. Skype advocates use reasonable commercial efforts to meet the following targets:

  • Email: All emails are acknowledged (auto-response can be considered) and resolved within 24 hours and 95% are responded to within one hour.
  • First Contact Resolution: 80% of the relevant issues that Skype is contacted for will be resolved during the first point of contact.

Follow the escalation process in KB 2907961 Procedure: Skype: Consumer escalations.

6.4 Tools

When you receive a request from a partner advocate, always respond back to them only. Never respond directly to the customer. These customers cannot be fortified as it involves third-party information that needs to be looked into. You need to use stand-alone Backoffice (BO) for these cases. No Personal Data information will be given.

Return to top

6.5 Request types

  • Fraud

Most of the incoming volume is linked specifically to fraud requests. Partners are unable to unblock accounts or look up any information in the fraud tool since they don’t have access to it. Partners do all initial verifications to identify customers, so we do not have to ask further information on these users, but only reply to the specific request from the partner.

  • Technical

Any Skype usability including technical troubleshooting, should be handled by the partner. Note that some of the partners do not have the option to change customer’s email address so they can send us to requests to do this for them.

  • Billing

All refunds and payment related questions should be handled by the partner. Partners might turn to us in cases where customer has made some purchases directly through Skype since they are unable to refund these.

11.7 3 Hong Kong

Skype has signed an agreement with 3 Hong Kong which includes attachment of Skype Unlimited World subscription with new or upgrading mobile contracts for 24 months’ commitment, distributed via voucher PINs over SMS or email.

All mobile subscribers at all points of sale, acquisition, and retention, need to choose a mandatory VAS (Value Added Service) bolt-on with their contract. For the period of exclusivity Skype is featured as one of the top 3 VAS commitment choices on the list presented to all users.

3 Hong Kong users have the option to take a Skype Unlimited World calling subscription connected with a 3 Hong Kong mobile service as a mandatory VAS add-on.

After selecting Skype Unlimited World calling as their VAS add-on, users receive a code in a text message every month for 24 months, which they need to redeem their subscription on skype.com.

Escalation Scenarios

  1. A customer states they cannot redeem their subscription because the PIN is not working.

    Make sure the customer followed the guidance in     How do I use Skype vouchers and prepaid cards?. If the customer followed the guidance and the PIN is still not working, escalate to Skype CS.

  2. A customer is claiming they cannot use the subscription from Skype.

    Make sure they have redeemed it properly, by asking if they have followed the correct process as described in the redemption flow. In case the customer says that they have, but they still cannot use the subscription, escalate to Skype Customer Support.
  3. After completing the code activation, the customer receives an email saying that this product is associated with 'recurring payment and is eligible for refund' What does this mean?

    Advise the user that unfortunately the information is incorrect. These subscriptions are not eligible for refund, and they are not associated with a recurring payment. Customers receive their codes every month, which they need to use to activate the subscription.
  4. Why is skype.com showing a different price for the subscription the customer is redeeming than the one offered in their contract with 3HK?

    During the redemption flow, customers refer to the original price from Skype for the subscription. The price that 3 Hong Kong offers these subscriptions for is actually cheaper and this might confuse users. Explain that the value on the redemption flow is the original value, not necessarily the actual amount the customer is charged.

Escalations from 3 Hong Kong

Any queries that 3 Hong Kong are unable to resolve are escalated through partnersupport@skype.net and handled by Skype English advocates, via a separate queue. 3 Hong Kong advocates have been asked to include the following information in any escalation emails:

  • Customer’s Skype Name
