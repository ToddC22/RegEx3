3. RSC Call to Action

If Direct Reseller experiences problems with onboarding they can access support via the Live chat on the MPN CSP site (https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/cloud-solution-provider). Standard escalation to PCS should apply to these tickets.

If the Partner is facing an issue when filling the “Customer support info”, ensure the “Technical Contact” in CMAT matches the Support Email the Partner enters in the Customer support info section when onboarding.

4. Support and Escalation 

If a Direct Reseller calls regarding billing issues, an SR should be created and escalation to be made to PayOps, as they are directly billed by Microsoft.

Clarification of the status Qualified/Not Started:

“Not Started” means that the partner has not begun transacting yet for whatever reason (either they’re approved but haven’t set up any subscriptions or if they are waiting to be approved).

“Qualified” means that their enrollment has been approved but they have not yet accepted the terms agreement to complete onboarding and activate the tenant.

Note: Partner doesn’t need to have the confirmation email that he is approved, he can just log in to Partner Center with the enrollment credentials and accept the agreement at the top of the Dashboard page.

All other support scenarios for O365, Azure and Dynamics can be raised directly by the Direct Reseller via Partner Center, following the instructions provided by the platform after creating a new Support Request for their Customer.

All existing escalation paths remain in place as for all other CSP Partners.

5. Emails from the Credit Collections Team

As part of the CSP Application process, Partners need to pass a credit check. Therefore the credit team will reach out to applicants requesting financial documents. These emails will come from ucmwrcsp@microsoft.com. Partners should respond to these emails with the information requested or if they have any questions.

Important: Credit check will take several days to complete. Partners are advised NOT to purchase a Microsoft support plan until partners have been informed that they have passed the credit check. More information can be found in this document.

If a Partner employee claims they have not received any credit request check, RSC agents should confirm that this individual is the actual Primary Contact for their tenant. If so, RSC agents will advise to look for emails from this sender: ucmwrcsp@microsoft.com. If no email was found, the Partner should reach out to the same email address to proceed with the credit check.

RSC agents should NOT be providing any further information to rejected partners beyond the information stated by the WOCS Risk Team. Each partner receives a communication from WOCS indicating what has happened with the status of their application.
