What is this article about?

This article provides information about Microsoft Financing with a Dell Preferred Account (DPA). A DPA is a revolving line of credit offered by WebBank and serviced by Dell Financial Services (DFS) that can be used for purchases in a physical or online Microsoft Store only in the United States.

In addition, a DPA is a payment method for Microsoft Store products and the only payment method for Surface All Access promotions offered through Microsoft Store.

1. Overview

1.1 What is a Dell Preferred Account (DPA)

  • DPA is a revolving line of credit which allows a customer to pay for their purchase all at once or gives them the flexibility to make monthly payments. With this account there is no prepayment penalty.
  • DPA financing is offered by WebBank, Member FDIC, and serviced by Dell Financial Services. WebBank determines qualifications and terms of credit. Microsoft is not extending credit and does not determine credit terms or eligibility.
  • All Microsoft Store products qualify for financing with DPA.
  • Microsoft provides promotional offers exclusive to customers who have DPA. The promotional offers have their own unique terms and conditions apart from standard purchases using DPA in physical or online stores.
  • DPA is only available in the United States.

Return to top

1.2 DPA guidelines

  • DPA can be used as a payment instrument in Microsoft online stores in the United States. It can be used for products other than those in a promotion if added to the customer’s account.
  • DPA cannot be used as a payment instrument for Purchase on Behalf of (POBO).
  • DPA cannot be used for pre-orders.
  • DPA is the only payment method for Microsoft All Access promotions.
  • Purchases made with DPA that are not promotional will have their own terms.
  • See KB 4058088 Procedure: Payment instruments scenarios to assist in adding DPA as a payment instrument for customers.

1.3 How a Dell Preferred Account (DPA) works

Customers can apply for DPA by selecting one of two options on the Microsoft Financing landing page: https://www.microsoft.com/store/b/financing.

Surface All Access: Surface All Access makes it easy to start working right away with Surface, accessories, and Microsoft 365 subscription, with one low monthly price for 24 months, world-class protection, in-store training, and dedicated customer support. And since Surface All Access plan now comes with Office 365, customers can start working smarter with the latest versions of the productivity apps they rely on every day. Refer to section 2.1.

General financing: The program is cost threshold based; this means the cart total dictates the financing offer based on customer’s available credit. For example, 12 months special financing on purchases of $750 or more. 18 months special financing on purchases of $1,200 or more. A program page has been created to outline general financing and drive greater awareness of the offerings clearly and succinctly. The page was built to clearly articulate general financing with pertinent and applicable info while also providing a landing page for all relevant teams to point consumers to for more information. The link to General Financing will be found on following pages:

  • Placement on PC: https://www.microsoft.com/store/b/pc?icid=CNavDevicesPC
  • PC gaming: https://www.microsoft.com/store/b/pcgaming?icid=CNavPCGaming
  • Deals: https://www.microsoft.com/store/b/sale?icid=CNav_salepage

What is the difference between Surface All Access and General financing?

  • Surface All Access is valid for Surface product purchases only.
  • Surface All Access offers 24 monthly payments.
  • General financing monthly payment durations are variable depending upon purchase amount but will not exceed 18 months in duration.
  • General financing will allow customers to finance all products sold in Microsoft Store up to customers credit limit.

2. Surface All Access

2.1 What is Surface All Access

  • Surface All Access makes it easy to start working right away with Surface, accessories, and Office 365 subscription, with one low monthly price for 24 months, world-class protection, in-store training, and dedicated customer support. Also, since Surface All Access plan now comes with Office 365, customers can start working smarter with the latest versions of the productivity apps they rely on every day.
  • Surface All Access is a Surface consumer program featuring financing provided by WebBank, Member FDIC, through DPA. DPA allows you to pay for your Surface under a 24-month equal payment plan and includes access to standard Microsoft retail stores support. Customers can also opt to add Microsoft Complete for an additional cost, see the Microsoft Complete for Surface details page for more information.
  • Surface All Access is exclusive to online Microsoft Stores in the United States (excluding Puerto Rico).

Return to top

2.2 Surface All Access features and benefits

When should advocates offer Surface All Access?

  • SAA is a great way for customers to own any Surface device with a low monthly payment. You can suggest it to overcome pricing objections from the customer.
  • When customers ask about financing options.

Are new Surface devices included?

  • All Surface devices can be purchased via SAA. For new released products, they will be added to the SAA bundles once the pre-order phase is over.

Do we have additional financing offers?

  • Business (SMB) Customers have their own version of Surface All Access for Business. Their program is powered by LiftForward. Transfer all contacts about financing for commercial customers to the SMB team.

Surface All Access

  1. Surface All Access becomes available in Microsoft online store on June 10th, 2019.
  2. Surface Plus customers who opt to upgrade their device are renewed with Dell as part of Surface All Access program – February 1st, 2019.
  3. Currently available in Microsoft physical stores.

3. Support contacts for Microsoft Financing

Dell Preferred Account for General Financing and Surface All Access

For Xbox All Access refer to KB 4521039 Procedure: Xbox All Access (XAA); the information below is only for Surface All Access.

  1. Existing Dell Preferred Account customers who have questions about their account with Dell should be cold transferred to Dell.
  2. Cold transfer the customer to Dell.

1. Make the customer aware of Dell’s Customer Service Home Page, where they can explore answers to their questions and find contact information (https://dfs.dell.com/Pages/CustomerService.aspx).

2. Provide the customer with Dell’s phone number for the appropriate department prior to transfer:

  • Dell Financial Services and Asset Protection Services (Pre-Sales) - Account Inquiries or Issues and/or Fraud Prevention: 1-844-380-1523/seven days a week from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM (CT).
  • Dell Financial Services Customer Care (Post-Sales) - Customer Inquiries, Billing or Promotion: 1-800-283-2210/Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM (CT).

Surface All Access for Business

Surface All Access for Business is a Microsoft program which allows any small business accepted into the program to pay a monthly fee for a new Surface device and accessories at the end of the chosen term (see KB 4023107 Informational: SMB: Surface All Access for Business). If customers qualify for membership in the Surface All Access for Business program, the underwriting and acceptance is through LiftForward. The entire credit relationship (and the terms of that relationship) are between the customer and LiftForward (see KB 4023107 Informational: SMB: Surface All Access for Business).

If the customer is Commercial (LiftForward), transfer the customer according to the following scenarios:

  • Accounts & Billing transfer to LiftForward. Contact LiftForward at 888-514-6539; Email partner-support@liftforward.com; Chat: Go to www.liftforward.com and select the orange chat circle.
  • Technical Support transfer to Commercial Surface Technical Support (see KB 2769823 Procedure: Transfer codes for other lines of business).
  • Pre Sales, Return & Refund, Exchange Support transfer to SMB (see KB 2769823 Procedure: Transfer codes for other lines of business).
