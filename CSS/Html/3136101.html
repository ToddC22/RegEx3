1. Article Overview 


 

1.1 Whom this article applies to

 

  • The following scope of services:  Microsoft Partner Network, Cloud Services

  • The following scope of agent roles: All MPN RSC agents

  • The following regions: All regions (global)

 

1.2 Scope of this article
 

This article provides the following information:

  • How to assist partners that experience issues with the activation of the Internal Use Rights (IUR) licenses for Microsoft Online Services that they receive as an MPN benefit.

  • Steps for RSC agents to determine if a new token/product keys can be generated and provided to a partner.

  • When and how to use the following tools: CMAT, PID Plus.

 
Notes:
 

Be aware that the terms “token” and “product key” are often used interchangeably internal and external Microsoft documents or by partners. 

Very Important! - Partners receive access to volume license (VL) product software and cloud services as a program benefit, but partners do not receive all the benefits of VL programs such as downgrade rights and Software Assurance benefits.

Licenses granted to partners are for the latest released versions of Microsoft software only. Licenses do not provide downgrade rights.

2. How to resolve online services (cloud) product key/token issues

 

The most common token/product key issues are addressed in this section. RSC agents will use the table below to identify the scenario and determine the action based on the Microsoft policy. 
 

 

Scenario description

 

RSC action

Note: Use these links in steps below when you need to view the detailed steps to follow in the tool.  

2.1

A partner accidentally added seats instead of extending the end date of the subscription.

(Example: A partner renews their MPN enrollment. As a result, the partner receives new tokens to extend their existing Office 365 subscription. However, the partner accidentally uses the tokens to add new seats to the subscription.)

 

 

1. Ask the partner to confirm in chat/email that they received the token through IUR and give us all digits of the token, and the subscription ID/GUID of the wrongly activated IUR.

 

The CMAT steps 2 through 6 below are demonstrated in detail with screenshots in section 4.1 of this article.

2. Log into CMAT and lookup the token.

3. Validate the activation status of token and make a note of the consumed/redeemed date.

4. Look up the subscription with the issue.

5. Determine if the seats were recently added or extended.

6. Confirm that the partner meets all the criteria in the replacement token/key policy.

7. Use PID Plus to generate a key for the product and number of seats for which the partner is eligible.

8. Ask the partner to use the new product key in Microsoft 365 Admin Center to extend the end date of the subscription.  For any other question/ issue encountered on M365 Admin Center portal during the process of redeeming the new token provided, please guide him to contact Office 365 team based on this document: https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/admin/contact-support-for-business-products?view=o365-worldwide&tabs=phone . Please note that M365 Admin Center portal is not in scope for MPN team.

9. Use CMAT to change the end date of the subscription to 12 months from the redeemed/consumed date of the old key. Do not extend the new token provided through PID Plus.

Please note that as per design, choosing to add seats and not extend the end date of the subscription will provide them with less than 12 months of benefits.

Please review 4515206 for more details

 ﻿2.2﻿

 

A partner accidentally extended the end date of the subscription instead of adding seats.

(Example: A partner upgrades from a silver to a gold competency and receives additional Office 365 seats as result. The partner intends to add the additional seats to their subscription. However, the partner accidentally uses the tokens to extend their existing end date instead of using the tokens for additional seats.)

 

 

1. Ask the partner to confirm in chat/email that they received the token through IUR and give us all digits of the token, and the subscription ID/GUID of the wrongly activated IUR.

 

The CMAT steps 2 through 6 below are demonstrated in detail with screenshots in section 4.1 of this article.

2. Log into CMAT and look up the token.

3. Validate the activation status of token and make a note of the consumed/redeemed date.

4. Look up the subscription with the issue.

5. Determine if the seats were recently added or extended.

6. Confirm that the partner meets all the criteria in the replacement token/key policy.

7. Inform the Partner that we are removing the extra time from the subscription.

8. Use CMAT to change the end date of the subscription to 12 months from the redeemed/consumed date of the old key.
 

9. Use PID Plus to generate a new key for the product and number of seats for which the partner is eligible.

10. Ask the partner to use the new product key in Microsoft 365 Admin Center to add seats to the subscription.

If they have any other question/ issue encountered on M365 Admin Center portal during the process of redeeming the new token provided, please guide him to contact Office 365 team based on this document: https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/admin/contact-support-for-business-products?view=o365-worldwide&tabs=phone . Please note that M365 Admin Center portal is not in scope for MPN team.

 ﻿2.3﻿

A partner activated seats on a wrong tenant. 

 

1. Ask the partner to confirm in chat/email that they received the token through IUR and give us all digits of the token, and the subscription ID/GUID of the wrongly activated IUR.

2. Log into CMAT and lookup the token. Make note of the redeemed/consumed date of the key.

3. Confirm that the partner meets all the criteria in the replacement token/key policy.

4. Inform the partner that we will set the subscription on the wrong tenant into Grace Period. This will give them 30 days of use/time to remove their data, at which point the subscription will become disabled.

5. Use CMAT to set the old subscription into Grace Period.

6. Use PID Plus to generate a new key for the product and number of seats for which the partner is eligible.

7. Ask the partner to use the new token to add licenses on the correct tenant in Microsoft 365 Admin Center.

8. If partner encounters any issue on M365 Admin Center portal during the process of adding licenses, please guide him to contact Office 365 team based on this document: https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/admin/contact-support-for-business-products?view=o365-worldwide&tabs=phone. Please note that Office M365 Admin Center portal is not in scope for MPN team.

2.4

A partner wants to move all IURs to a new tenant.

 

1. Ask the partner to confirm that they want to move all of their IUR benefits from one tenant to another.

2. Ask the partner to confirm in chat/email that they received the token through IUR and give us all digits of the token, and the subscription ID/GUID of the wrongly activated IUR.

3. Inform the partner that the IURs cannot be moved to a new tenant. Confirm that the partner meets all the criteria in the replacement token/key policy to apply for token replacement

4. Inform the partner that we will set the subscription on the original tenant into Grace Period. This will give them 30 days of use/time to remove their data at which point the subscription will become disabled.

5. Use CMAT to set the old subscription into grace period.

6. Use PID Plus to generate keys based on the number of seats needed for each tenant and within their eligibility.

Note! Replacement allowed only when a partner is going underneath a company change name and as a consequence, they need to change their tenant to reflect on their new organization.

7. If partner encounters any issue on M365 Admin Center portal, please guide him to contact Office 365 team based on this document: https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/admin/contact-support-for-business-products?view=o365-worldwide&tabs=phone. Please note that M365 Admin Center portal is not in scope for MPN team.
2.5

A partner wants 6 or more IUR licenses to be replaced.

1. Ask the partner which products they want key replacements for.

2. Confirm that the partner meets all the criteria in the replacement token/key policy and provide them with the additional keys needed.

2.6

Partner is at risk of losing access to critical cloud IURs while waiting for engineering issue to be resolved.

Description: A partner with either a competency or an Action Pack subscription contacts RSC. They have an issue that after troubleshooting, the RSC identifies is a technical issue that requires engineering support to resolve. Due to the time needed for engineering to resolve the issue, the partner is at risk of losing access to critical cloud IURs.

As mitigation option while waiting for engineering to resolve the partner’s issue, the RSC must escalate each case to MPN Global Support asking the team to analyze the scenario and provide an approval to extend the Partner Cloud IUR through CMAT for up to 30 days, only if partner is eligible for this decision.

Please note that the answer received from Global Support can be different from case to case based on the investigation done.
2.7

Partners who use O365 can’t use the on-prem versions of Project and Visio with their O365.

Partner facing guidance: “We are sorry you can’t use the on-prem versions of these products with your O365. As a workaround please use the cloud versions of Project and Visio 2016. Alternatively, you can purchase the on-prem versions.”

Note: If a Partner would want to run a version of one of these products with O365  please refer them to the license table to check the competencies that provide the Online versions of these products and apply to one of the mentioned competencies.

All other Partners who only get the On Premise versions through core licensing will not be able to run these products with O365.

If the Partners are interested in running Visio Pro or Project on separate environment (clod or on-premise) you can use the Product Usage Guide and license table. The License table is helpful to show Partners how to attain/get the online version of each product which will run compatible with an on-premise version.
2.8

Partner submitted a request by mistake to a wrong support team and the case didn’t reached the RSC team within 30 days.

We can make an exception and help the partner only if he provides a SR number & emails proving the date he engaged with support team for the first time. In case partner doesn’t follow the steps provided, in time, and he reaches the RSC team out of the deadline, RSC team will not allow the exception any more
2.9 Partner has an existing Office 365 purchased separately and would like to utilize the Office 365 E3 benefit provided by the competency. Advise partner that once the key is redeemed, reassign the licenses from Office 365 purchased separately to E3, via Office Portal where only the Admin can do that. Once reassigned it will be updated to Office 365 E3.


What is in scope for Frontline team?

  • Frontline team can help partner locate product keys/tokens

  • Frontline team can help partner point out where partner needs to go to redeem product key/token

  • Frontline team can help partner find MPN IUR licensing info (e.g. product usage guide, volume activation guide)

  • Frontline team CAN’T advise partner which tenant to use during activation as frontline team is not aware of partner overall licensing set up. Such advice could lead partner to activate an IUR on a wrong tenant or combine with other VL licensing that negatively impacts partner IUR license or VL license (one or the other end up lasting shorter amount of time, things like adding new seats vs. extending seats). These are areas where frontline team is not equipped with the skillset or knowledge/materials to help partner navigate. Frontline agents are trained extensively on partner program support, but not on product activation and deployment. Those are unique skillsets that we don’t provide any materials because IURs are provided AS-IS with no license type support.

3. Replacement token/key policy

All the above scenarios apply ONLY for the first replacement key request within 30 calendar days.

RSC agents can generate a replacement token/key for a partner in PID Plus, only if it is the first time replacement and if the request comes within 30 days since the key has been activated.

As a general rule no exception will be granted for the second replacement key or for the requests beyond the 30 days timeline. For scenarios where there is a strong business justification that you think it should be taken into consideration the request will be escalated to OCP Compliance through MPN Global Support by using the below template (i.e. The company is involved in a merge process or maybe Partner is changing the Company Name).

NOTE: Product Keys are sensitive data information that should be handled securely. Product keys can only be shared with partners over the phone/email to an authenticated user with the correct roles, rights and privileges. When sharing product keys via email, RSC should ensure data protection to protect sensitive information:

  • Sent via encrypted message
  • Password of the encrypted message should be sent separately.

When documenting a case, full replacement product keys should be NOT documented. For reference, RSC can document the last 5 digits of the product key only.

RSC should provide the full product keys that need replacement to MPN Global Support to perform the needed checks.

5. Glossary

 

The following is a glossary common terms related to activation of Microsoft Online Services.

Product Identification Number (PID)

Microsoft products have several unique identifiers. This includes the Product Identification Number (PID) and the product key. In addition to identifying the product and version, these numbers also determine support entitlement and help prevent software piracy.  

Internal Use Rights licenses (IUR)

Internal Use Rights (IUR) licenses for Microsoft Online Services (also known as cloud services) and on-premises software are received as a benefit of an MPN enrollment. These IUR licenses give partner organizations firsthand knowledge of product features and capabilities. Partners can use them to run their business, train their employees, develop and test, and demonstrate to their customers.

Product keys and tokens

Some products and services require a product key for installation and/or activation. Product keys for cloud services may also be referred to as tokens (including in the Partner Digital Download portal). Product keys are used during the installation or activation process for software.  Tokens are used to redeem cloud services.

Both product keys and tokens have a unique series of 25 characters consisting of five sets of five alphanumeric characters. (Example: XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX.) This is also referred to as the “5X5 product key”. Keys are issued per organization and not per end-user of the organization.

Product Key Part Number

Each Microsoft product has a unique Product Key Part Number (PKPN) that identifies the product. 

Tenant

A tenant is the account created in the Microsoft Online Services environment when the customer or partner signs up for a subscription. A tenant contains uniquely identified domains, users, security groups and subscriptions.

Globally Unique Identifier (GUID)

The Globally Unique Identifier (GUID) is the unique number assigned to a tenant.

The Commerce Management Agent Tool (CMAT) is used to perform billing and subscription tasks. When troubleshooting partner cloud IUR token issues, you will use CMAT for:

PID Plus

Microsoft PID Plus is a web-based tool used to generate product keys and Product IDs (PIDs). These product keys are generated when a product a key is lost, destroyed, unreadable, or incorrect.

When troubleshooting partner cloud IUR token issues, agents use PID Plus to create a new product key, when appropriate, to resolve the partner’s issue.

Note: Because the PID Plus tool will generate product keys, access and activity are closely monitored. You should never generate a key unless it is absolutely necessary and only after you have determined definitely that the customer actually needs a new key.
