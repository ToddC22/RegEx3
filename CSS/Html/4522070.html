The newly launched GROW form enables multiple functional teams to submit leads. Note that the additional guidance shared on the form page, specifically Deal criteria for their submissions, is not in scope for Support Engineers.

 

Who should use this article?

  • Commercial Technical Support Engineers
  • Commercial Accounts/Billing/Subscription Management/Commerce Engineers and Advocates
  • Commercial Customer Service Advocates

What is this article about?

This article outlines the guidance for Support Engineers to use when submitting GROW leads. Advise me GROW is a CSS-wide program that allows Support Engineers/Advocates to advise customers of additional products and services Microsoft offers that could benefit their business needs and easily connect them with a Solution Specialist from the Digital Sales organization.

The Advise me GROW program is intended to drive Achieve More outcomes that:

  • Make customers happier
  • Build and retain Microsoft’s Fan base
  • Accelerate the use of our products & services

When should this be used?

Helping customers solve their issues is the priority of CSS Support Engineers.

Break-fix interactions provide an opportunity to learn more about the customer and how they are using Microsoft technology for their business.

An Advise me GROW conversation is suitable to have:

  • When the case is closed.
  • When there is positive customer sentiment.
  • When there is interest to learn more or if there is a business need that may be solved by Microsoft technology.

How to have an Advise me GROW conversation?

Follow the 3 easy steps below for Listen. Ask. Advise.

Procedure: Advise me GROW in 3 Easy Steps

Listen for Opportunities


While working the customer’s issue(s) or question(s), listen for customer needs and business challenges. If you hear an opportunity where customer is….

a.  …interested in a new Microsoft product or service.
b.  …has a business challenge that can be addressed by a Microsoft product or service.

If either the above are true, proceed to have an Advise me conversation when the opportunity naturally presents itself during idle time (i.e. waiting for logs, downloads, running scripts) or during the troubleshooting procedure.

Ask by confirming interest


a. If customer is not interested in a new product(s), and/or service(s), finish resolution of case.
b. If customer is interested, inform the customer that a Solution Specialist will reach out within a week and complete Step 3.

Advise by completing the GROW webform


a. Complete the GROW webform (@ aka.ms/grow) to submit the customer’s information to the Solution Specialist.  A Microsoft Solution Specialist will reach out to the customer within a week. 

**Please note that the GROW form is to be populated by the CSS Support Engineer ONLY, on behalf of the customer.

DO NOT SEND the link to the GROW form to the customer, that is out of process and not compliant and puts the lead interest at risk with no follow-up.

Internal Referral Form:  Complete to Submit Interest.

IMPORTANT:  When sharing customer’s detail, you are responsible for ensuring privacy and consent compliance. Microsoft is committed to honoring customers’ permission to contact them. Therefore, you are responsible for ensuring that the customer has provided permission for his/her contact details to be shared with the Solution Specialist.

Use the form to route customer interests related to:

  • Migrating from on-prem to Cloud
  • Licensing purchase
  • Interest in new Cloud product offerings
  • If your stated product is not listed, then choose the following:
    • For on-prem migration interests to Cloud for Exchange server, SharePoint on-prem, Intune, EMS route to Microsoft 365
    • For on-prem migration interests to Cloud for SQL server on-prem, route to Azure for exploring cloud solutions
    • For on-prem migration interests to Cloud for Win server on-prem, route to Azure for exploring cloud solutions
    • Developer, route to Azure
    • For on-prem migration interests to Cloud for Dynamics on-prem, route to Dynamics 365 for exploring cloud solutions
    • Hololens2, route to Dynamics and immediately contact GROW@microsoft.com as this will require an additional step for the GROW team to facilitate customer engagement

If you select a product and don’t see the customer’s country, then please advise the customer to contact a Microsoft partner to support their business needs.

Please refer customers to the Microsoft Partner Center for support on furthering their interest at following location: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/solution-providers/home

Do not use this form to route customer interests:

  • For newer on-prem products
    • GROW form is not to be used for submitting on-prem product interests, for these, please share the Partner Center with the customer to further their interest, https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/solution-providers/home
  • Non-profit or education interests
    • These would not be captured via the GROW webform. For non-profit or edus, please share the Partner Center with the customer to further their interests, https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/solution-providers/home
  • This form is to capture customer interests gathered by Support Engineers, not for internal questions.
  • May not be used to submit tech support questions.

b. Process: Referral Form Fields Requirements (*asterisk denotes required field). The following steps represents an example of how a form may be completed.

1. Submitter’s functional group
Select Support 
VERY IMPORTANT:  The form will be used across organizations at Microsoft, please disregard deal criteria. For Submitter’s functional group, please select Support. This ensures the lead is supported correctly, reported correctly and recognized accurately. Ultimately, the customer is impacted most by an incorrect selection. Additionally, we will not be able to track the lead if submitted as a different functional group.

2. Support Ticket ID*
Once you accurately select Support, the Support Ticket ID field will appear.  This field will not appear if you do not select Support.
Enter Case Number into free form field
Providing an accurate Case Number ensures tracking Advise me outcomes like CSAT, Sentiment and Verbatims from the customer interaction.
This is key to ensure that you are recognized for your submission across CSS recognition programs like Customer Heroes.

3. Submitter’s Microsoft Email*
Enter complete Microsoft email address.

4. Customer first name*
5. Customer last name*

6. Customer job role*
Ask customer their role within their organization (ex: IT Manager, Operations Manager, Systems Admin, Database Admin, etc..).
This is required, so an entry will be needed.  If a customer role is not known, then enter Unknown.

7. Customer Email address*

8. Customer phone number*
Ask customer for phone number, including area or country code where they can be contacted.

IMPORTANT:  Ensure Phone Number country area code or country code matches the Customer Country field in the form.

9. Customer company name*
Enter complete name of company.  Ideally, no abbreviations or acronyms.

10. Which product is the customer most interested in?

11. Customer country*
Select country from dropdown.  Select the country in which the customer is purchasing.

Procedure: What Happened to My Lead?

Customer business needs or interests captured and submitted by the Engineer/Advocate using the GROW webform are followed up by the Solution Specialist.

Procedure for Full Time Employee Engineers/Advocates:

By submitting the required information such as the a) case number, b) alias, c) customer needs, the Engineer/Advocate will be informed on the progress of his/her Advise me conversation journey, which includes:

  • Feedback Loop #1

Engineer/Advocate submitting the lead will be contacted informing him/her that their customer lead has been assigned to a Solution Specialist, within a week of lead submission.

  • Feedback Loop #2

Engineer/Advocate will be notified of the status of their Lead.

Note: The lead journey in the Solution Specialist procedure may span any duration (days, weeks, months). Additionally, not all Leads result in Wins.

  • Feedback Loop #3

If the lead turns to a customer Win, the Engineer/Advocate is notified of the deal closing & recognized for helping customers Achieve More in support! See aka.ms/VoG for recent wins/celebrations.

Procedure for delivery partner Engineer/Advocates

Reach out to GROW@microsoft.com for any support on lead status.
