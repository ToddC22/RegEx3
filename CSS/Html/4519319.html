3.2 Courier Pickup (CSC)

The customer will need to follow the instructions below to arrange for the collection of the non-functioning product:

Customers should pack the non-working device into a box that they must provide before the courier arrives at their location to pick up the parcel. Pickup occurs only after agreement of a pickup date is reached.

The customer may reuse the packaging from the replacement product to pack the return. The customer is expected to package the device into a sturdy shipping box, and using bubble wrap, newspapers, or other recyclable materials to securely pack the device.

If the courier does not turn up, the customer will need to call and check with Quantium Solutions (HK) Limited hotline at: 2318 1213 during office hours, 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday and quote “Microsoft Pickup” and the Service Request number to arrange for a collection.

In case the hotline is busy, the customer should send an email to customerservice.hk@quantiumsolutions.com, in the email subject please quote “Microsoft Pickup” and the Service Request number as reference.

Return to top

3.3 Prepaid Box with Label (SST)

Surface Studio only. Customers should expect to receive a prepaid shipping box within five business days.

Once the box is packed, the customer should call Quantium Solutions HK hotline at: 2318 1213 during office hours, 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday and quote “Microsoft Pickup” and the Service Request number to arrange for a collection.

In case the hotline is busy, the customer should send an email to customerservice.hk@quantiumsolutions.com, in the email subject please quote “Microsoft Pickup” and the Service Request number as reference.

If the courier does not turn up, the customer will need to call 2318 1213 to check with Quantium Solutions (HK) Limited for alternative arrangements.

Return to top

3.4 Advanced Exchange (SAD)

The service center will ship a replacement first. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, the customer should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the return instructions included to arrange collection.

Once the box is packed, call Quantium Solutions HK hotline at: 2318 1213 during office hours, 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday and quote “Microsoft Pickup” and the Service Request number to arrange for a collection.

Return to top

3.5 No Action Required (NCA)

The service center will ship a replacement and the customer is not required to return the non-functioning product.
