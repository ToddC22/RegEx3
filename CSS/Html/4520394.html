Informational: Office 365 Identity support boundaries

[Asset 4462485]

This article brings clarification about what is under the scope of M365 Identity team and provide guidelines whether M365 Identity or the Azure Identity team should handle a specific issue or technology.

M365 Identity is a suite of products and technologies focused on the identity management of Microsoft 365 customers, we rely on many services created by the Azure team, and both teams might share the support for the same products and this can cause confusion when in need to engage help.

The M365 Identity team works with Rave, the Azure Identity in the other hand works with Service Desk, this is important to know if you need to work with any of the teams.

Note in the tables, the Scenario column uses the following terminology:

  • SSO: Federated users who sign in through single sign-on (SSO) by using their on-premises credentials
  • Standard: Non-federated users who sign in by using their cloud user ID
  • Both: Applies to both SSO and standard users

 Important

  • Given capacity crisis, discrepancies between NA and EMEA/APAC are defined in the article. Please see “Regional Exceptions”
  • Use the appropriate support topics/issue codes under Office 365 Identity using Rave
  • Customers who have Office 365 or Microsoft Intune
    • Are entitled for support

Process that MUST be followed when in doubt or before transferring any case to Azure Identity team
https://internal.support.services.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4531531

If you are not an Identity engineer, please do not transfer to Azure before engaging a M365 Identity resource. 

  • Do not escalate Premier cases to Azure Identity. Instead, escalate these cases to O365 Identity.
  • Do not escalate non-English cases to Azure Identity. Instead, escalate these cases to O365 Identity
