Q: Why can’t I use a payment method issued in another country?
A: All Microsoft products are adapted to the different markets in the regions and countries worldwide including the difference between prices. So, this is the reason of not being able to repurchase the same subscription in the same currency but with another card issued in another country with different prices. The prices are different, and therefore the card needs to be issued in the same country and you need to purchase in each country's currency.

FAQ

Q: Why can’t you refund me for a subscription bought from Amazon? It’s Microsoft.

A: When a retailer is purchasing bulk licenses/subscriptions from Microsoft, they are fully responsible for ensuring full functionality and distribution for sales purposes of those products – to begin with, your payment went to Best Buy/Amazon/etc., therefore we have no trace of it under your personal account, as the purchase was not made online through Microsoft store, while being signed in with your account.

 

Q: Why does it take 5-7 business days to receive my refund when it took only 1 minute to charge me?

A: When you place an order or make a payment, our banking system places a hold on your funds.

They will not appear on the card anymore, as they are under a hold.

After a few days, when the transaction is cleared, the funds will leave your bank account which is called a settlement. When a refund is submitted from our system, we send a message to your bank that we will send the amount back to you, and the bank needs to process this request, and it can take up to 7 business days for the financial institution to revert the money.

Tips: Educate customers proactively before providing a once in a lifetime exception

Example: "Please note that this is a once in a lifetime exception. So, if you ever make any other accidental purchases or if you’ll confront with unauthorized charges in the future, we won’t provide you a refund because, as per our company's policy, you will not be eligible for it. May I please know if you accept these terms and conditions and if you still want me to proceed with the refund?"
