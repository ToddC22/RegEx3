2. Procedure

Microsoft Home Use Program (HUP):

The Home Use Program previously implemented by Digital River is making Perpetual Office available to employees of qualifying organizations. This offer is considered a Software Assurance (SA) Benefit.

 

Starting October 30th, employees of eligible companies will be able purchase Office 365 Home or Personal at a substantially discounted rate for personal use on their personal devices.

Eligibility requirements:

To check the eligibility requirements, please refer the public site and go to the collapse section "Who Is Eligible for the Microsoft Home Use Program Offer?"

[Asset 4527253]


Available benefits under Home Use Program:

To check the currently available benefits, please refer the public site and go to the section “Microsoft Home Use Program Offers”.

[Asset 4527254]

For your information, the below record provides the history activities of each benefit.

  • Beginning 10/30/18 – Office 365 Home or Office 365 Personal annual subscription for home use at a 30% discount (excluding China and Japan)
  • Now until 12/31/2018 for customers with SA –Office Professional Plus 2016, Office Home and Business 2016 for Mac, Project Professional 2016, and Visio Professional 2016 for $9.95* each.
  • From 1/1/2019 to 6/30/2019 for customers with SA – Office Professional Plus 2019, Office Home and Business 2019, Visio Professional 2019, and Project Professional 2019 for $14.99* each. (excluding China and Japan)
  • From 4/1/2019 – Office 365 Home or Office 365 Personal annual subscription for home use at a 30% discount and Office Professional Plus 2019, Office Home and Business 2019, Visio Professional 2019, and Project Professional 2019 for $14.99* each (including China and Japan)
  • Starting from 7/1/2019 – Benefits of Office Professional Plus 2019 and Office Home and Business 2019 will no longer be available. Benefits of Project 2019 and Visio Professional 2019 will continue to be offered to eligible customers

 

What does this mean for the customer?

Customers will now be able to use their work identity (Azure Active Directory (AAD)) to indicate that they are part of an organization that has the benefit available. From there, the users will use their personal identity (MSA) to see the discounts and select the one they want. After this, they will simply land in the consumer store cart with that discounted product ready to be purchased. The discount will be applied to their subscription in an ongoing manner.

Customer Experience:

Admins can see the benefit in the Microsoft Store for Business and can enroll the employees of their organization. Employees can then learn about the offer and become eligible and then complete their discounted purchase.

NOTE: If an end-user contacts Store for Business support with issues getting the benefit, the customer should be redirected to the Consumer accounts and billing team.

Escalation

Escalation to Tier 3
For any HUP issue, T1 / T2 can escalate to T3 following the procedure in 2778091: How to escalate a Service Request to Tier 3, Engineering

T3 Escalation via VSO
After the HUP case has been scoped and determined to be escalated, T3 can open an escalation case via the HUP VSO template.
