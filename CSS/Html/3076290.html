1.3 Proof of purchase (PoP)

Instructions on how to obtain proof of purchase

Reminder: You must use DTM to collect proof of purchase or any customer data as dictated in this article. The needs of DTM criteria from this article may differ slightly from KB 4135034 Tool User Guide: Data Transfer & Management (DTM), however the information needed has been approved and must be collected for Solve solutions called out in certain Solve scenarios.

The customer must provide valid PoP for many Office Solve scenarios. Use the DTM tool to capture these items.

Do not share the required proof of purchase criteria with the customer. This information is internal and is to help you determine if the receipt is valid. If you’re unable to determine if the receipt is valid, get help from your Team Lead (or equivalent). If an image in DTM has poor quality (blurry, unclear, or only part of the receipt is visible), inform the customer we need a clearer quality picture of the full proof of purchase, and request a new image.

Screenshot of the paper receipt

  • Ask the customer to write down their service request (SR) number on both the receipt and on the back of the 5x5 card.
  • Check that the serial number (SN) on the receipt and the PPC/POSA card match.
    • If the SN does not match the receipt and the PPC/POSA card, or you believe the PoP has been edited or photoshopped, escalate to CSS Phishing Alerts (CSSPALERT@microsoft.com) and decline further assistance.
  • We cannot accept unclear images. Make sure the image is clear. If the image is blurry, fuzzy, or unclear in anyway, ask the customer to upload the image again.

Email receipt or PDF attachment from the retailer

  • Ask the customer to provide a copy of the original email order summary. This ensures the product is not coming from a random seller or individual.
  • Upload to DTM.

Photo scan of Office or Microsoft 365 product key card

If you need proof of purchase for a damaged Office or Microsoft 365 product 5x5 card issue, you will need a photo scan of the damaged card. We cannot accept unclear images. Make sure the image is clear. If the image is blurry, fuzzy, or unclear in anyway, ask the customer to upload the image again.

In addition to the above, the proof of purchase receipts (both paper and email receipt, and product key card) must have the following information.

Paper receipt

  • Name of the retailer
  • Invoice or bill number
  • Purchase amount
  • Date of sale
  • Product description
  • Serial number

Email receipt or PDF

  • Retailer's name
  • Retailer's email address
  • Date and time of the email (look top right or left of email).

Damaged Office or Microsoft 365 product key card

  • Back of the card including view of damaged 5x5.
  • Serial Number below 5x5 scratch off.
    • There may also be multiple serial numbers listed in the top and bottom corners of the card.
  • Current service request number written on the back of the card.

Japanese advocates

For PIPC Office: Get the PoP or invoice from the PC.

  • Include the OEM PC model name.
  • If no OEM PC name, get a picture of the label located on the device. This may have the information needed.

Check that the PC model comes with Office PIPC.

Damaged cards: No serial look-up needed for Office PIPC.

2.2 Microsoft 365 subscription stacking

Scenario description

Solve process

Customers sometimes purchase more than one Microsoft 365 subscription thinking it will double the amount of benefits.

  • This is not a subscription transfer scenario.

The customer wants a refund:

  • Refund if inside Microsoft store refund guidelines.
  • Cancel the subscription in MST.
  • Don’t provide a token if a refund is provided.

Note: We can’t refund if purchased from a third-party retailer or from partner billing such as Apple. See What happens if I add another Microsoft 365 for home subscription to my account?. 

The customer would like to split the subscriptions.

If purchased from Microsoft or third-party, then:

  1. Verify the customer’s existing Microsoft account.
  2. Cancel existing subscription if purchased from Microsoft Store.
  3. Include the service request in ASD comments.
  4. Determine remaining time on the subscription.
  • Round up to closest month.
  1. Generate and send tokens to the new Microsoft account.

Notes:

  • Verify the Microsoft account prior to cancelling existing subscription.
  • Microsoft can’t cancel Partner Billing accounts. We do not own the billing relationship.
  • You might need to send multiple tokens based on how much time the customer has left on their subscription. For example: seven months = one six-month token and one one-month token.

ORST

  1. Select Make It Right in ORST tool.
  2. In MIR SKU box, select MIR 31 Subscription Stacking.
  3. Choose the appropriate product and generate token based on customer’s remaining subscription time.
  • Use CTRL if you need more than one SKU.
  1. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  • Comments: Stacked O365 subscription time by mistake
  1. SRID: Provide ticket number.

Advocates located in India can transfer to either:

  • Office technical support located outside of India.
  • Accounts & Billing

2.3 Switch from Office perpetual to Microsoft 365 subscription

Scenario description

Solve process

 

The customer purchased and redeemed Office perpetual, and now realizes the benefits of having a Microsoft 365 subscription.

Does not apply to Home and Student.

 

If the customer is within the refund timeframe, and purchased from Microsoft, offer a refund on Office 2019 and have them purchase Microsoft 365.

Refund extended from 30 to 90 days from purchase date for Office 2019 if the customer wants to exchange to Microsoft 365. The extended refund is available through June 30th, 2020. On July 1st, 2020, the refund period returns to 30 days.

  • Block the Product Key (PK) obtained from the My Account page (Tier 2 needs to do this).
  • Issue a refund.
  • Help the customer purchase Microsoft 365 Family.

Provide a three-month token using Solve 16.

If the customer does not want a refund, purchased from a third-party, or is outside of the refund period:

  • You can offer to sell Microsoft 365 with a three-month token using Solve 16 in ORST.

3.1 Damaged Office or Microsoft 365 product key card

Scenario description

Solve process

The 5x5 numbers off the product key card are damaged.
  • The customer must provide valid Proof of Purchase (PoP). See section 1.3.
  • Look up the serial number using the Token Lookup tool. See KB 4053535 TUG: MST: Token Lookup tool for more details.
  • If you can successfully see the associated 5x5 code, help the customer redeem the product, and read it out while they are on the setup page. Do not provide any Solve tokens if you can give the code back to the customer.
  • If you are unable to look up the token, ask your Team Lead to contact the TA team at ocsta@microsoft.com. Provide complete screenshots of the back of the POSA card.
  • T2 see the ACC blog post [T3] Token Look-up using DARSy if unable to collect the 5x5 from the Token Lookup tool.

3.2 Customer issue with OEM-installed Office/Microsoft 365 product

Scenario description

Solve process

The customer purchased a computer with Office pre-installed (consumer Microsoft 365 or perpetual).

The customer is unable to activate the product on the device.

Example of issues where troubleshooting will not help:

  • Purchased Microsoft 365 Family, and instead Microsoft 365 Personal came with OEM PC.
  • Purchased Office/Microsoft 365, but no product installed on the OEM PC.
  • Product came installed on the OEM PC as per purchase, but it is only a trial, no option to activate.

The following example needs troubleshooting:

  • Correct version of the product didn’t come activated.
    • Walk customer through the activation process.
    • If the correct version successfully activated after troubleshooting, do not offer ORST token.

Accounts & Billing, and Sales: Transfer to Office technical support advocate.

Surface support: Transfer to Office if you need more assistance.

Try to resolve the customer’s issue with general troubleshooting as mentioned in KB 4012605 Procedure: Office: Activation for Office (Errors and known issues section). Follow the guidance in the article first or CAA notices will be issued. If the only way to resolve the issue is by providing a new key, provide a replacement token for the Office product that came with the machine and help the customer activate Office.

The customer must provide PoP for the Office or Microsoft 365 product, for the solve process to apply. See section 1.3.

Information needed for Solve and to add in case notes:

  • Any error messages.
  • PoP verifying the device was new at purchase:
    • If purchased second hand, no Solve applies.
    • If purchased used/refurbished, no Solve applies.
  • Recent repairs or exchanges done on the device:
    • Was the product redeemed and activated before repairs?
      • If after, Solve doesn’t apply.
      • If before, Solve applies.

If everything checks out, provide a token using ORST.

ORST

  • Select Make It Right in ORST tool.
  • In MIR SKU box, select Solve 6.
  • Enter the name of the Retailer/Store Purchased as it appears on the PoP. If it was a gift, enter Gifted.
  • In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  • Comment: Issue with OEM installed Product.

SRID: Provide ticket number.

4.1 Transfer to another Microsoft account (Microsoft 365 Family or Personal only)

The customer owns the Microsoft account

Scenario description

Solve process

The customer wants to transfer their current Microsoft 365 Family or Microsoft 365 Personal subscription to another Microsoft account they own.

  • Not a stacking scenario (see section 2.2)
  • We can’t action partner billed accounts (such as Apple or Best Buy)

 

Note: If the customer purchased from Microsoft and refunds the product within the refund policy window, do not provide a token.

Using ASD and MST:

  1. Create the SR against the account with subscription cancellation.
  • Note: This requires an exception to the normal requirement to associate the SR with the account receiving the ORST.
  1. Verify the customer’s existing Microsoft account. If not verified:
  • No subscription cancellation.
  • No tokens provided.
  1. Once verified, determine how many months remain on the subscription. You can round up to the nearest month.

See ORST steps after performing the following:

  • MST: Cancel the subscription from existing Microsoft account.
  • Include the SR number in the case comments.
  • Generate the tokens using ORST.
  • No need to capture the email or new Microsoft account.
  • You might need to send multiple tokens based on how much time the customer has left on the current subscription. (Example: seven months = one six-month token and one one-month token.)
    • Note: You can select multiple options by using the CTRL key during selection.

ORST

  1. Select the Make It Right tab in ORST tool.
  2. In MIR SKU box, select Solve 1.
  3. Select the appropriate product.
  4. Send multiple tokens based on remaining time on the customer’s current subscription.
  • (Seven months = one six-month token plus one one-month token).
  • Use the CTRL key for selecting multiple SKU’s.
  1. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  • Use these comments only. Comments: Move subscription to another Microsoft account.

SRID: Provide ticket number.

4.3 No proofs associated to Microsoft account

Scenario description

Solve process

The customer lost access to Office.

  • The customer does not remember the Microsoft account password and is unable to recover the Microsoft account.
    • The customer account doesn’t have security proofs*.

* Signing up for a new Microsoft account when redeeming Office doesn’t require security proofs. Security proofs include phone number, and alternative email.

Note: This solve only applies to Microsoft accounts that do not have security proofs. This solve does not apply to customers who have lost access to their Microsoft account because they cannot remember their password or verify their security proofs.

Prior to transferring to Office technical support verify the following.

  • Microsoft account does not have security proofs*.
  • Microsoft account is not in a blocked or suspended state.
  • Microsoft account does not show signs of compromise, such as social engineering.
    • See KB 2991467 Procedure: Privacy: Handling social engineering, fraud, and phishing incidents.

If you can’t verify the above, do not transfer to Office technical support. Help customers to recover their account or provide hard messaging.

*You can check the Microsoft account for security proofs using MST (alerts tab).

5.3 Office 2019 OEM preinstall

Applies to: China, India, Korea, and Japan.

Scenario description

Solve process

The customer purchased an OEM machine with Office 2016 after the Office 2019 release date. They want the Office 2019 version.

  • India, Japan, and China: Purchased between 22nd of January 2019 and 21st of January 2020.
  • Korea: Purchased between 1st of October 2019 and 2nd of October 2020.

Cancel product in MST.

Activate non-activated products before cancelling.

  • For customers in China, India and Korea who have Home & Student 2016, replace with Office Home & Student 2019.
  • For customers in Japan who have Office Personal 2016 or Office Home & Business 2016, replace with Office 2019 equivalent.
    • Confirm which PIPC 2016 the customer owns such as Consumer PIPC or Commercial PIPC.
    • If the customer owns Consumer PIPC 2016, provide PIN using ORST.
    • If the customer owns Commercial PIPC 2016, provide Product Key using PID Plus.*

Requirements:

  • Starts October 2018 (Korea).
  • Starts January 2019 (China, India, Japan).
  • Customers must have PoP with the following information:
    • Name of retailer
    • Invoice/Bill number
    • Purchase amount
    • Date of sale
    • Product description

Notes:

  1. Remember to use DTM for PoP.
  2. If e-receipt or PDF, the included copy of the order confirmation email sent by retailer must have the following information:
  • Retailer name
  • Retailer email address
  • Date and time the email

ORST

  1. Select Make it Right.
  2. Select Solve 8 Office 2019 OEM Preinstall as MIR SKU.
  3. Enter Retailer/Store purchased.
  4. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  • Enter Office 2019 OEM Preinstall in comments.
  1. Enter SRID ticket number.

*PID Plus: Make sure that you search Office 2016 PIPC Commercial under the OEM channel in PID Plus.

2. The customer is unable to read the product key card. The 5x5 POSA card is damaged.

  • Validate the customer’s Microsoft account:
    • Get PoP of the 5x5 card. PoP can be a screenshot of the receipt or email receipt attachment from the retailer and include a copy of the scratched 5x5.
    • If PoP is an e-receipt sent by the retailer, then we need a copy of the email sent by the retailer (to ensure it is not coming from a random seller or individual).
    • PoP must have the following information:
      • Name of retailer, invoice/bill number
      • Purchase amount
      • Date of sale
      • Product description
    • Serial number from the POSA card should match the serial number from PoP.

Look up the serial number using the Token Lookup Tool (see KB 4053535 TUG: MST: Token Lookup tool on how to use the tool).

  • If state is Active, proceed with next steps.
  • If the state is anything other than Active, do not proceed with next steps. Contact your Team Lead. Leads should contact the TA team if they have questions.

If state is Active, inform the customer we will cancel the POSA card they have, and we will send them a new one. If the customer agrees process with next steps:

Deactivate the token (see KB 4053535 TUG: MST: Token Lookup tool).

  1. In ORST, choose Solve 21 Surface All Access and select the Office 365 Home Premium – 2year or Office 365 Personal – 2year.
  2. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  3. In the comments section write: Scratched PK card on Surface All Access.
  4. SRID: Provide ticket number.

Note: Do not confuse this with scenario 2.1.

5.6 Customers are unable to redeem 0365 Services/Email confusion (Japan only)

Scenario description

Solve process

The customer tries to redeem Office 365 Services token after the Japan migration to modern billing (see this blog article for more information).

The customer receives error: “Sorry you don’t qualify for this office. Office 365 Service for pre-installed PC is a special offer for people who previously bought a PC with Office premium pre-installed.”

The customer received email Office 365 Service will expire email.

Some of the customers who see the email interpret the mail that they should renew their 365 service even though they don’t need the service anymore beyond the 12th month. The subscription will become 13-months since they renewed and will want a refund. Refunding causes the cancellation of the account and shows the customer owes one month.

Verify that the customer is downloading a token from Office 365 Services.

  • If the state is Active, proceed with next steps.
  • If the state is anything other than Active, do not proceed with next steps. Contact your Team Lead. Leads should contact TA team if they have questions.

Deactivate the token (see KB 4053535).

Proceed with providing the Microsoft 365 Personal token using ORST.

Use ORST:

  1. Select the Make it Right tab in ORST tool. In MIR SKU box, select Solve 23 Customer unable to redeem O365 Services.
  2. Pin Generation: Other.
  3. Comments: Customer unable to redeem 0365 Services.

The customer received email saying Office 365 Service will expire.

Note: Team Lead/T2 must proceed with this step.

  2. In MIR SKU box, select MTLU 3.

  4. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  5. Comments: Customer confused over 0365 expire email.

5.7 COVID-19 for save and retention

Solve description

Solve process

If a customer contacts support and wishes to cancel their Microsoft 365 Personal or Microsoft 365 Family subscription due to COVID-19 hardship, offer them a two-month MIR token.

This is a one-time only Solve per customer.

This offer also applies to customers who need assistance getting out from dunning status.

Note: We do not have a 2-month SKU. Please tell the customer that they will be getting two 1-month SKU’s.

We needed to change the tokens provided from three months to two months to align with the offer customers cancelling on account.microsoft.com see.

If the customer redeems the token, it will push out the next renewal charge by two months (if they aren't calling the same day of renewal). Do not cancel the account first. First offer the token and explain that no billing will occur for two months. Send two one-month tokens if the customer agrees.

  1. Give the customer two-months free using ORST.

  4. In Pin Generation Reason: Other
  5. Comment: COVID-19
  6. SRID: Provide ticket number.

Next to new pin generated, hover your cursor over the number in parenthesis such as (1). If one has already been provided or if the customer expects a token for calling in, simply thank them for being a Microsoft 365 customer and ask if they need any further assistance before ending the session.

  • Note: We do not have a 2-month SKU. Tell the customer they will be getting two 1-month SKU’s.
