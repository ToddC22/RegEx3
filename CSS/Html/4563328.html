Support Boundaries for AIP/ MIP Support Scenarios

[Asset 4476087]
  • Content Idea (CI): 118686
This article covers the technical support boundaries for Azure Information Protection(AIP) and Microsoft Information Protection(MIP) Support scenarios  It should be used as a guide for Security and Compliance, Azure Information Protection and Office Client support agents when handling AIP/MIP support issues.  Use this to determine which Support teams to engage or Engineering Teams escalation paths to use.

For more information on transferring cases see: https://internal.support.services.microsoft.com/en-us/help/2500396

Note: For MIP (EnterpriseSecurityCompliance) team engagement:
Broad Commercial engage Exchange Online team
Premier engage Security and Compliance teams (EnterpriseSecurityCompliance)
