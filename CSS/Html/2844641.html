What is this article about?

This article provides direction on helping customers who want to exchange their Office product for a different Office product. It also discusses what stacking subscription means.

Note: This KB was formerly known as: Process: Tier 1 – Moving subscription from one Microsoft account to another.

If a customer purchased a one-year Office 365 Home or Personal subscription and the request is after the first 30 days: The subscription can be placed into a delayed cancel to stop the auto-renewal, but a refund will not be possible. The customer will continue to have access to Office 365 for the remainder of their one-year subscription time.

Subscription stacking FAQ:

Q: What is subscription stacking?

A: Subscription stacking is when a customer has purchased two or more years (up to five) of an Office 365 subscription using one Microsoft account. Stacking subscriptions will extend the Office 365 Home or Personal renewal date by another year(s).

Q: Why are subscriptions stacked?

A: Customers want to renew their Office 365 Home/Personal subscriptions before their subscription expires so that they can continue using their Office subscriptions.

Q: How does subscription stacking occur?

A: Subscription stacking occurs when a customer applies another subscription product key to their existing subscription. When applying a newly purchased subscription product key, the customer's subscription is updated to add the duration of the newly added subscription.

Q: What if the customer buys additional Office subscriptions thinking they will increase the number of installs they can have? Can we offer a refund?

A: The number of devices customers can install Office on doesn’t change when they stay with the same type of subscription plan. Whether they stay with the same subscription or convert it to a different subscription plan, their online storage capacity per user doesn’t change. For example, they will not be able to share their Home subscription with 12 additional users, it will remain six users total.

If a customer is requesting a refund, see the scenario titled Stacked Subscriptions from KB 3076290 Procedure: Office: Office Solve scenarios.
