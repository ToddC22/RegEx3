1. What’s new

Effective December 1, 2019, Dynamics 365 SKUs will be available for a 3-year subscription term in CSP. We are expanding availability to include Dynamics 365 Business Central, Dynamics 365 Base and Attach SKUs.

Summary:

  • Dynamics 365 Business Central available with 3-year subscriptions
  • Replacing Dynamics 365 Plans by Base and Attach SKUs with multi-year term
  • The SKU names include the identifier ’36 mo’
  • All CSP partners
  • New and renewing CSP customers
  • All countries where CSP is offered
  • Commercial and government

Notes:

  • Mid-term subscriptions transitions, from 1-year to 3-year, are not supported in CSP
  • There is no change to the partner experience; partners may choose to be billed monthly or annually
  • Changes to the existing CSP agreements are not needed to support the ‘36 mo’ SKUs
  • Contract implies obligation, as there are no technical enforcements of the 3-year subscription term

2. Overview

Effective October 1, 2019, Dynamics 365 will be moving from multi-application, one-size-fits-all Customer Engagement (CE), Unified Operations (UO), and Dynamics 365 Plans to focus on the licensing of individual applications.

Customers need software that aligns to their functional roles and scenarios, and they require the ability to add or remove applications as their company grows and changes over time. The new licensing model will allow customers to purchase the applications they need, when they need them. Each application is extensible, and applications can be easily mixed and matched to configure integrated solutions that align to a customer’s unique business requirements.

Effective October 1, 2019:

  • CE Plan, UO Plan, and Dynamics 365 Plan SKUs will be removed from all price lists.
  • Finance and Operations will be split into two individual applications: Supply Chain Management and Finance. Sales, Customer Service, Field Service, Project Service, Marketing, Retail, and Talent will continue to be offered as standalone applications.
  • New and renewing customers will be able to purchase individual applications as Base and Attach licenses. The Base license is the first application purchased for an individual user within a customer’s organization. Attach licenses are additional applications that may be purchased after the Base license is acquired. We will provide further information as we get closer to the preview pricelist publication.

Note: Below Yammer announcements have been posted for partner communications:

  • Yammer announcement 1
  • Yammer announcement 2

Base and attach applications – enforcement

Partners need to purchase and assign attach applications only after purchasing and assigning base applications.

If the partner purchases these offers through the catalogue on Partner Center, the right sequence will not be enforced.

Enforcement will happen when assigning user licenses to the services, through AAD (Azure Active Directory). Should the partner try to associate attach offers to a user without base offers, the system will not allow you to proceed furtherly. Specifically, the partner will receive a “License Assignment Error”, prompting them to assign a Dynamics 365 base application prior to assigning a Dynamics 365 attach application.

What does not change?

As part of this change, you should expect:

  • No impact or change to your APIs
  • No impact or change to the way your reconciliation file is being displayed
  • No impact on the return/cancellation process, or your support experience

3. Dynamics 365 Business Central Enhancement Plan Renewal Offer (EP/BREP)

Summary: Microsoft created an offer which enables existing customers active on their Enhancement Plan (EP) to renew their EP through the Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) program and receive Dynamics 365 Business Central (cloud) licenses. Customers who choose to renew their EP through the CSP – EP Renewal Offer receive the following benefits: 

  • Price point equal to Enhancement Plan renewal quote
  • Dual use and downgrade rights to their current on premises Dynamics Price List (DPL) product (Dynamics 365 Business Central, on-premises, Dynamics NAV, Dynamics GP, and Dynamics SL)
  • Monthly billing option (subject to CSP partner approval)
  • Enhancement Plan benefits
  • Dynamics 365 Business Central (cloud) licenses when the customer is ready to migrate

Note

The CSP – EP Renewal Offer is a new option for customers to renew their EP.  Customers continue to have the option to annually renew their EP via DPL.

This offer is targeted for current D365 on-prem customers to create a migration path to the cloud while providing some relief for the cost of their on-prem enhancement plan. This results in four new D365 Business Central CSP offers that when purchased, will trigger the Partner to take action to enable the customer to receive 1-year of their on-prem enhancement plan at no cost.

Customer Eligibility: Customers qualify for the CSP – EP Renewal Offer by meeting the following criteria: 

  • Dynamics on-premises license: Customer licensed for Dynamics 365 Business Central, on premises, Dynamics NAV, Dynamics GP, or Dynamics SL via the Dynamics Price List (DPL) prior to June 1, 2020. Offer subject to CSP Geographic availability.
  • Active on EP: Customer has an active EP and is within 30 days of their renewal date 
  • Available SKUs: Commercial/Public Gov only <no EDU>
  • Minimum License Requirement:
    • Customers must meet and maintain the minimum license requirement for the term of their CSP subscription to avoid default. 
    • Customers who lapse on their CSP plan, cancel their subscription, or fall below the minimum requirement, will be liable for their annual EP costs in addition to incurring lapsed fees.  
    • Partners will be contacted to resolve if the monthly review show non-compliance with the minimum requirements by their customer.
  • Lapsed Exception: As the CSP-EP Renewal Offer is in response to the current environment and the business impact, Microsoft is allowing exceptions for customers who lapsed on their EP between January 1, 2020, and May 31, 2020, to re-enroll their EP through the CSP - EP Renewal Offer without penalty. Customers must license the CSP – EP Renewal Offer by July 31, 2020. 

Offer Details: 

  • Licensing Program: Cloud Solution Provider (CSP)
  • Product: Dynamics 365 Business Central (Cloud)
  • Offer Length: June 1, 2020 – June 30, 2021
  • Offer Duration: 1-year initial agreement and two optional 1-year renewals (3 years total)
  • Pricing: The CSP – EP Renewal Offer provides price point 60% lower than the standard commercial list price. For example, the CSP – EP Renewal Offer list price for D365 Business Central (cloud) – Premium user is $40 compared to $100 list for the CSP standard Commercial pricelist
  • Licensing Requirement: Customer must purchase Dynamics 365 Business Central (cloud) licenses with an annualized total greater than or equal to their EP renewal amount.
  • Additional Users: 
    • Customers who met the above licensing requirement and require additional licenses to cover all their named users, may request additional users.
    • No cost licenses will be provided via Microsoft Online Subscription Program (MOSP) promotional codes. Customer must provide a credit card for identification purposes when redeeming those codes (card will not be charged). 
    • Customer must request the promotional offer within 60 days of licensing.
