1. Home Use Program (HUP) overview

The Microsoft Home Use Program (HUP) is a Microsoft’s employee purchase program leveled up.

HUP now enables eligible companies to open a world of amazing Microsoft products to their employees at a special discount.

  • With Office, you have 365 days filled with endless possibilities and new ways to achieve the things that matter to you.
  • With the Microsoft HUP, your employees can do more, with more.

There are two main landing pages for this program:

  • IT Admin or HR facing HUP portal: Used by company benefits or HR admins to promote HUP internally to their employees. Provides access to additional promotional materials for HR teams to share broadly. Admins use the Microsoft Store for Business (MSFB) where they setup their benefits and enable employees to purchase on the new HUP store.
  • Employee Facing HUP program page: Most commonly visited landing page used to explain how HUP works and is the first step to an employee signing up and verifying eligibility for the HUP program.

Notes:

  • The HUP eligibility and admin setup happen on Microsoft Store for Business.
  • HUP orders process through the Microsoft Store.
  • Employee order history and purchase and billing are visible show to CSS advocates in MST/Rave.
  • Customer's purchase history, tracking information, subscription management and download links are available from https://account.microsoft.com.
  • HUP purchase link: http://www.microsoft.com/homeuseprogram
  • For employees that purchased on www.microsofthup.com prior to the modern migration, there is no Microsoft account connection: Follow the instructions at https://www.microsoft.com/home-use-program/order-history.

  1. HUP program page – Open the employee page and enter their work email address.

[Asset 4530201]

  1. Sign in using a personal Microsoft account.
  2. This can be any Microsoft account that they are associating to use with the Home Use Program. If the employee does not have a Microsoft account, they can create one during this step.
  • Note: Many companies have security settings which prevent employees from creating an account, instead, suggest the customer forward the validation email to their personal email to complete the validation at a different time. They will then enter (or create) the Microsoft account they wish to use their product on.

3.2 Purchase 

Office

  1. On the HUP Office page the customer must choose Microsoft 365 Home or Personal, then select Buy Now.
  2. The customer experience goes directly to the Microsoft Store (for consumers) cart, with the product selected and the 30% off discount already showing. They then select Checkout and payment instruments to complete their purchase.

[Asset 4530208]

  1. The customer sees the Cart page with the discounted price. They can change the quantity here if they desire, although there is a maximum quantity on the selector, typically five units per item. They then finish the purchase by selecting Checkout, and entering their payment and shipping details to finalize.

5.1 Eligibility, purchasing and buying

Q: What are the Limitations of the program to employees?

A: Employees who work at a company that satisfies the Home Use Program eligibility requirements. These purchases are meant for personal use only, and not for profit.

Q: What happens if I leave the company?

A: If you have registered for Home Use Program in the last six months, you will be able to access the discounts for selected Microsoft products. If your registration has passed six months, you won’t be able to access the discounts.

  • Exception: If you have already bought Microsoft 365 subscription through Home Use Program and you leave the company, your Office subscription remains active and the discount continues to apply if you maintain recurring billing on your subscription.

Q: How many times per year can I use the Home Use Program?

A: You can buy one subscription of either Microsoft 365 Family or Microsoft 365 Personal at the discounted Home Use Program price.

Q: How long will my discounted price for Microsoft 365 subscription last?

A: Office - You will receive the same Home Use Program discount rate as your original purchase for as long as you maintain recurring billing on your subscription.

Q: How can I take advantage of the HUP offer?

  1. Sign up: Visit the Microsoft Home Use Program page. Enter your corporate email address and select Get Started.
  2. Confirm: If you are eligible, you receive an email to your corporate email address with a link that will validate your eligibility for the program.
  3. Sign in: Once your eligibility is confirmed and you register for the program with your personal Microsoft account that is associated with your personal email address, you can begin the purchase process by viewing the discounts available to you based on your Home Use Program benefit eligibility.

Note: Customers who purchase HUP first enter their work email address and wait for the validation email to arrive. When they select the link to validate the product, the customer enters a Microsoft account. Many companies have security settings which prevent employees from doing this, if this happens ask the customer to forward the validation email to their personal email to complete the validation at a different time when they can enter the Microsoft account they wish to use their product on.

  1. Shop: Your purchase processed and completed in the Microsoft Store. You, the eligible employee, can install Microsoft 365 on your personal devices after the purchase is complete. Once you have purchased Microsoft 365 Home, you can share your subscription with up to five additional members of your household.

Q: How do you become eligible for Home Use Program discounts on Office Professional Plus 2019, Office Home and Business 2019, Visio Professional 2019, and Project Professional 2019?

A: Check with your employer’s Home Use Program administrator to confirm your eligibility. If granted access, follow these steps:

  1. You receive a verification email.
  2. Sign in with your personal account.
  3. You come to the FAQ section and get access to the discount here:
    • Visio Professional 2019
    • Project Professional 2019

Customers who select the link will see the message: "You are not eligible for this discount."

Q: Will customers receive Biz-Bar notifications to check eligibility to renew or buy their subscription?

From May 11th through 28th, 2020, HUP is running an experiment on the in-app Microsoft 365 Biz-Bar. Normally this is to inform the user that it’s time to renew their subscription. The experiment will send a small percent of customers who are eligible for renewal or in grace period (dunning) two Biz Bar messages: “USE OFFICE AT WORK?” and Your employer may be eligible to offer you a 30% discount on Microsoft 365 subscription for your personal use. The customer can select the Check Eligibility button. This doesn’t mean they are eligible, as the Biz Bar messages don’t have a target audience (except for customers eligible for renewal).

After selecting the Eligibility button, the customer will go to the Home Use Program sign up page. The customer should enter their work email credentials to check eligibility.

  • If eligible, they will get an email with a link to sign up with a personal Microsoft account (not their work email) and purchase Microsoft 365 at a discount.
  • If not eligible, they will receive an email that they are not eligible to buy, and the email should direct the customer to make a purchase online at regular price. Using the consumer SKU Chooser Page.

Q: I received this error on the Home Use Program sign up page ‘Your email id already submitted, If you’re eligible a verification email will be sent to you shortly.’ When entering my work email address on the Home Use Program sign up page, what should I do?

A: The employee has likely double-clicked the Get started button. This error shows if you had previously entered in your work email address and selected Get started. Ask the employee to search for an email.

[Asset 4530241]

Q: I never received an email after entering in my work or corporate email address on the HUP Program page, should I expect one?

A: Standard troubleshooting includes asking the employee to check their junk or spam mail. Ensure they are not blocking any emails from account@microsoft.com. If they still have not received an email 24 hours after entering their work email address, refer them to their benefits or IT admin for their employer for further support.

Q: I received an error when signing in after selecting Login to my Personal Account on my eligibility email. What should I do?

A: Most sign in problems appear when customers are trying to login using their work or corporate email address again, instead of using a personal Microsoft account email. Confirm they are signing in using a personal email address.

5.2 Office

Q: Can I take advantage of the HUP offer if I already have Microsoft 365 Family or Microsoft 365 Personal installed on my personal device? (Office only)

A: Yes. If you already have Microsoft 365 Family or Microsoft 365 Personal, you can still take advantage of this discount and apply it to your current Microsoft 365 subscription. Once purchased, an additional year stacks to your existing subscription, and the discounted rate will begin at your existing subscription’s annual renewal date. Be sure to specify the Microsoft account that’s associated with your current Microsoft 365 subscription.

Q: Why am I seeing Office at 30% off pricing on the HUP page, but it changes to full price during checkout or in the cart?

A: If an employee has not completed all steps for eligibility (see the employee walkthrough above), and are on the Office assortment page, they will see discounted pricing. This is because the messaging on this page is static and does not change whether you are eligible or not. Once they select Buy Now and go to the store cart, it will revert to full price. The HUP Office assortment page has static messaging built on the page. It will appear as $69.99 or $49.99 (30% off) to all visitors regardless if you are eligible for HUP or not.

To solve this scenario, guide the customer back through all the eligibility steps, by going to the Microsoft Home Use Program and entering in their work email address and following all the correct steps to become eligible.

 [Asset 4530219]

 

After selecting Buy Now, if the customer sees the full price in the Microsoft Store cart, make sure they’re signed in with an eligible HUP personal Microsoft account.  

 

[Asset 4530253]
 

Q: Can I share my subscription purchased through Home Use Program with members of my household?

A: If you have a Microsoft 365 Family subscription, you can share it with up to five additional members of your household (up to six users total). Each household member can install across their devices including PCs, Macs, iPads, Android tablets, Windows tablets, iPhones, or Android phones get 1TB of OneDrive storage, and manage their own installs from https://account.microsoft.com/services/.

Q: Which email account do I use to install or manage Microsoft 365 and my subscription?

A: Always use your personal Microsoft account to install Microsoft 365, manage your subscription, and sign into Microsoft 365 on your personal devices. Your Microsoft account is your personal email address, not your corporate email address. Log in to account.microsoft.com and select Services/Subscriptions to access or manage your subscription.

Q: How do I renew my Microsoft 365 subscription?

A: When you start your Microsoft 365 subscription, recurring billing is already on, and we will bill you automatically until cancelled. If you cancel or turn off recurring billing, you can renew your subscription at www.office.com/renew. You can check your renewal status on the Services & Subscriptions page.

5.3 Legacy HUP purchase history/product key/installation links

Q: How do I access my Office product keys or order history from the old Home Use Program?

A: If you purchased an Office Professional Plus 2013, 2016, 2019, Office Home & Business for Mac 2016 or 2019, Visio Professional 2013, 2016, 2019, or Project Professional 2013, 2016, 2019 on www.microsofthup.com, you can use the order history link below to access your information and product keys.

You must still work at the same company from which you received the Home Use Program discount. Enter the email address you previously used to make a purchase on www.microsofthup.com. Once submitted, we’ll confirm your purchase. Once confirmed, we’ll send your installation link and product key to the email address you entered.  

If you previously purchased Office, Visio, or Project, go to the Order History page to have your product keys emailed to you. You need to have access to the email address you used to make the purchase.

[Asset 4530255]

Q: What if I no longer work at the company?

A: If you do not work at a company that is eligible for the Home Use Program, you are no longer eligible to access activation and installation information related to your purchases made on www.microsofthup.com.
