Customer Support Experience

Premier customers have three ways of engaging with Microsoft Support, using:

  • Premier support channel.
  • M365 Admin Center from their Premier tenant (Online support).
  • Phone support.

Only the Administrators of the tenant can create cases depending on their role in the company. Please check the About Office 365 admin roles support article.

Each tenant has special tags in Rave to quickly identify the type of the customer. Depending on the tags the customers receive support either from the Premier General teams or Premier Escalation teams. Please check How do we see Premier customers in Rave? section in this article for more details.

Where and how are the cases routed in Rave?

Premier General cases are subject to DAO (Direct Agent Offer). This is the preferred method for routing customer cases to advocates and ambassadors. The DAO system uses a set of internal rules to determine how a case is routed and to which organization. When a case routed using DAO, that case is automatically offered directly to an advocate or ambassador.

The cases are created in Rave and offered by DAO to the advocates, based on the information the administrator provided or has it embedded in his or her administrator and tenant profile.

  1. Administrator regional settings (Language, Time Zone, etc.)
  1. Issue description is specific to Exchange, SharePoint, Teams, etc.
  1. Tenant region (APGC, EMEA, LATAM, NOAM)

How do we see Premier customers in Rave?


Each tenant has special tags in Rave to quickly identify the type of the customer. Depending on the tags, the customers receive support either from the Premier General teams or Premier Escalation teams.

Exceptions are Commerce and Yammer technologies, where all the cases are handled by Premier General teams.

Premier General = External outsourced support delivery teams in the M365 organization (former called CKP) driving frontline Premier support to customers.

Premier Escalation = Internal support teams in different XLTs delivering frontline Premier support to customers on Critsits, Advisory, RCA, Development. 

Please note that below information applies only to the M365 cases routed to the EnterpriseXXXXXX support areas. For example: EnterpriseSharePoint, EnterpriseExchange, etc.

OnPrem technologies are not in scope for this article. If the case is routed to EnterpriseXXXXXXOnPrem support areas, please visit On-Premise Support in Rave. For example, EnterpriseSharePointOnPrem, EnterpriseExchangeOnPrem, etc.

  1. If a case is marked for Premier General, it will be visible under the Quick facts the tag Premier customer. No other tags will be visible. The case will be routed to the Premier Support queue. [Asset 4505769]
  2. If a case is marked for Premier Escalation, it will be visible under the Quick facts the tag Premier customer and near the case number is a Gold Diamond. The case will be routed to the PremierEscalation queue. [Asset 4505770]
  3. If the customer is S400 and Premier, the case can be routed to Premier General or Premier Escalation. Both support teams are responsible for helping S400 customers routed to them. It makes no difference if the case is tagged with the Gold Diamond or not. Please check What is S400 tag in Rave? and How do we handle S400 Premier cases? for more details. [Asset 4505771]
  4. Some cases are marked as Premier Partners. They will be visible under Quick facts with tag Premier support for partners customer and/or Advanced support for partners customer and a Blue Diamond indication. These cases are routed to the Premier General or Premier Escalation based on if the Gold Diamond is present near the case number or if the customer is S400. [Asset 4505772]
What is the Strategic Customer badge in Rave?

[Asset 4547753]

The Strategic badge signifies that a customer has been identified by Microsoft as strategic. The program exists to help our sales and executive teams identify and develop strategy around specific customers and industries. There are few routing or handing expectations set upon tenants that carry this badge. However, these customers are supported by all delivery teams.

Until recently, customer tenants that were stamped with the S400 badge were identified as strategic in RAVE manually. This process is now dynamic and the badge itself has now been redesigned to display Strategic rather than S400.

Though some of these customers are routed directly to Premier Escalations based on certain agreements, many of these customers are routed and supported by Premier General teams by design.

Cases are not to be transferred to Premier Escalations solely based on the Strategic badge.

How do we handle Strategic Customer cases?

Strategic cases can land in any support area and are not exclusive to Premier customers. The Strategic status should not be discussed with the customer. All support teams will continue to support the customer and escalate through normal channels when necessary.

  • If a Premier tenant has the Strategic badge and is delivered to Premier General support, Premier General will support the customer.
  • If a Premier tenant has the Strategic badge and is delivered to Premier Escalation support, Premier Escalation will support the customer.
  • If a non-Premier tenant has the Strategic badge, the case will be routed to the Enterprise or Concierge support teams based on seat count. These teams will support the customer. No transfer is necessary. The case will be tagged as DAO + Standard Support.
What is the Office 365 Engineering Direct (OED) badge in Rave?

[Asset 4547769]

This badge will be present for all customers who have tenants where the Enterprise Cloud property is equal to Enabled. This property can be viewed in RAVE cases by navigating to the customer information screen (clicking on customer name) under the support information section. The property is automatically set for customers who have purchased Engineering Direct as part of the Unified Performance offering or have O365 Dedicated Engineering as part of their legacy Premier contract.

How do we handle Office 365 Engineering Direct (OED) Customer cases?

All cases from customer displaying the OED Badge should be routed to Premier Escalation teams. Any cases received into teams other than Premier Escalations need to be transferred. Please transfer these cases and set the skill to Premier Escalation and remove DAO skilling.

FAQ

CSAM (Customer Success Account Manager - previous TAM) contact details
  • To access customer’s dedicated CSAM please access to Rave and type either the domain or tenant ID.
  • CSAM information is available by navigating to the company info page in the Support information section on the Profile tab.
  • If a CSAM is not available, you can contact the persons as well indicated under “Additional Case Notifications”.

[Asset 4581097]

Please refer also to 4501876: How to search for CSAM/IM or Customer Information in the ticketing system.

IM (Incident Manager) contact details
  • To access customer’s dedicated contacts please access to Rave and type either the domain or tenant ID.
  • The information is available by navigating to the company info page in the Support Information section on the Profile tab.
  • In the “Additional Case Notifications” you may find the backup CSAM as well as the IMs. This is only in case they have been added there.
  • IM team is engaged by the CSAM to work on his behalf and assist the customer on cases.
  • The IMs have Accenture in their e-mail title or signature.

[Asset 4581097]

Customer is not Premier in Rave

You may be contacted by a CSAM or IM (Incident Manager), on a non-Premier case. The issue is that the customer tenant was not CKP matched as Premier in the Relationship Management (RM) Dashboard.

CSAMs need to go to RM Dashboard in https://esxp.microsoft.com/#/relationshipmanagementdashboard/ckp to match their customer tenants as Premier using the Domain Name and Tenant ID. The CSAMs are the only persons that can do this match.

Please ask the CSAMs to do this match, and if they have more questions, ask them to visit the URL for specific matching instructions.

What happens after a case is Nudge?
  1. Nudge automatically e-mail will be sent to the Premier Nudge DL, that contains the TM/QMs of the frontline teams.
  2. The subject of the e-mail Nudge will be as per below, to make it easier for each team to identify their cases.
    Tenant Country | Frontline Team | Support Area | Premier – Rave ticket 12345678 needs attention
  3. The team owning the case needs to acknowledge the Nudge e-mail by replying on the thread and moving the DL to BCC to avoid spamming.
  4. The TM/QM of the team needs to review the case and ensure it is prioritized accordingly by its owner.
  5. The team then needs to reconfirm (if needed) status on thread with person that nudged the case.
How do I close a Rave ticket?
  • You can use this guide for assistance on how to close the Rave case. Closing the Rave case will also close the Service Desk case, this flow does not work the other way (closing Service Desk will not close the Rave case).
  • Closing the Rave case will present the customer with the option to leave feedback on the support widget (where case was opened).
  • The Service Desk survey has been suppressed for Service Desk Shadow cases.
