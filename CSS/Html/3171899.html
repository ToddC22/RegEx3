1. Process for Display Advertising inquiries﻿

While Microsoft products like Skype, Outlook, MSN, Xbox, and Windows show display ads, Microsoft is exclusively a search ads company.  To book ads on these Microsoft products, we partner with companies like Verzionmedia, AOL and AppNexus.

If the company contacts us requesting to place display ads, refer them to the following contacts by target market:

US, UK, CA, JP, BR, IT, FR, DE and ES


https://www.verizonmedia.com/advertising

All other global markets

https://www.xandr.com/contact-us/

Mexico

http://cmi.mx/digital/media

Aus & NZ

https://www.nineforbrands.com.au/solutions/

Publisher guidance:  For publisher questions/solutions, refer the customer to https://www.verizonmedia.com/advertising for more information on how to work with Microsoft.
