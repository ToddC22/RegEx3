Procedure: Microsoft Store: Digital content return/refund

[Asset 4049349]

 

Who should use this article?

  • Accounts & Billing
  • Office

What is this article about?

How to process a return of digital content using Membership support tool (MST). This procedure should be followed whenever a support call results in a return/refund based.

For Xbox Refund Request Solution available countries, see KB 4485105 Procedure: Xbox: Refund Request Solution.

1. Guidelines

See the following articles:

  • KB 3083551 Guideline: Microsoft Store: Return and Refund
  • KB 4338530 Guideline: Return/refund for non-gaming related digital orders

All refunds are applied to the payment instrument associated to the transaction and the customer’s Microsoft account. We will not refund or credit a different payment instrument.

Note: Do not issue a refund to a customer who only knows the payment instrument and cannot verify the account. See KB 2885545 Procedure: Handling a customer who is the payment instrument owner but not the account owner.

Return to top

2. Procedure

2.1 Verify ownership of the Microsoft account used for the purchase

  • Microsoft can only provide a refund to the account owner since we refund through the Microsoft account.
  • Verify the customer’s Microsoft account if you have not already. See KB 4506610 Procedure: Microsoft account verification.
  • To assist a customer who is the payment instrument owner but not the account owner, see KB 2885545 Procedure: Handling a customer who is the payment instrument owner but not the account owner.

Return to top

2.2 Confirm the payment instrument is still associated with the Microsoft account

Microsoft can only process refunds to the payment instrument that was used for the purchase. Review the customer’s Microsoft account to see if the payment instrument is still associated with the account.

When a direct debit purchase is made within the past seven days, the purchase should not be refunded until seven days have passed.

Confirm the following in MST:

  • Purchase date of product.
  • Method of payment was direct debit, as indicated only for the following countries of purchase:
    • SEPA Direct Debit: Germany, Spain, Austria, Netherlands, Italy, Ireland, Belgium, France, Portugal, and Luxemburg.
    • ACH Direct Debit: United States.
    • iDEAL BA: The Netherlands.
  • If it is within seven days, schedule a follow-up in ASD to process a refund after seven days. Let the customer know the refund will reflect in their account within 14 days.
  • If seven days have passed, proceed to section 2.3.
Return to top
 

2.3 Locate the purchase in question

  1. Check under the Orders section in MST.
  2. Review the refund exceptions and refund limitations listed in KB 4338530 Guideline: Return/refund for non-gaming related digital orders.
  3. Check for previous refunds:
    • All refunded orders and items will show in the orders section of MST. Select view all to view all transactions, and filter for the relevant period. Review the comment section of the refund to verify the reason for the refund.
    • See KB 3065764 TUG: MST: Orders section for guidance on reviewing comments.
  4. If return eligibility is met, proceed to section 2.4.

Return to top

2.4 Issue a refund

If return eligibility is met, select an appropriate reason code and select the appropriate item.

  • Return Line Item – Use this option for standard refund process. This will remove the entitlement of the product or service and refund the customer.
  • Refund Line Item – We should only use this option if the customer needs to keep the product or service, but they require a refund because of circumstances, such as the service is down, or the incorrect price was charged.

See the Orders section of KB 3065764 TUG: MST: Orders section for details on the refund process and guidance on reviewing comments.

Select the most appropriate reason code. In the comment field, enter the following information (example): <SR/case number>, <Rave ID/case number>, or <Opportunity ID/case number>|

  • Example: 1506210186|
    • Note: In case you need more comments space, please use the add comments feature.

The refund function is not offered by design for the following scenarios:

  • eGift card orders purchased more than 14 days ago or redeemed.
  • Order status is Canceled, Chargeback, or Refunded.
  • Order was not directly billed by Microsoft, such as in mobile billing.

If the refund function is not offered, and the scenario is not listed above, but the scenario is within guidelines to provide a refund, engage the A&B ERA Team (for Japan/Korea/China, engage ACA) or Xbox Ranger as a Refund Issue. See KB 3072553 Procedure: A&B: Tier 1 Billing Scenarios.

Return to top

2.5 Describe why you are refunding the customer in the service request

After processing the refund, update the SR case notes with (examples):

  • Total amount refunded (for example, $39.99 USD).
  • Time period of the line items, if it applies (for example, 17 Mar 2017 – 14 May 2017).
  • Refund procedure article number (for example, KB 3083551).
  • Title of the exception used (for example, Unauthorized purchase by a minor).

Return to top

2.6 Set correct expectations

Share with the customer that you have requested the refund for the purchase price of the item, typically financial institutions will receive the request from us within five business days, but the time it takes to appear on your payment instrument statement will depend on the financial institution. Set the customer’s expectations to the lead time based on the regional difference, and follow Case management framework (CMF) articles in closing the cases.

This probably will help to remove customer’s frustration for not knowing the lead time of the refund.

Return to top

3. Refund issue scenarios

There are instances when a refund will fail or will not process. Follow this section for guidance for different scenarios:

Scenario

Guidance

Customer in a non-LATAM region reports they have not received their refund, but MST Order status says Refunded.

If it has not been seven days since the refund was processed, ask the customer for their patience, and advise them that it can take some time for some financial institutions to post refunds. For more information, they can reach out to their financial institution.

If it has been over seven days since the refund was processed, engage the Account & Billing ERA Team (for Japan/Korea/China, engage ACA) or Xbox Rangers as Refund Issue and follow below process.

  1. Set expectations with the customer. Once you confirm that the money transferred to the customer’s bank, the customer may need to check periodically with their bank to see if the funds transferred. Transfers are usually completed within seven days, but it may take longer depending on the bank’s refund policy.
  2. Help the customer navigate their bank statement and ask them to look for the transaction within a 30-day date range from the refund on their statements. There are instances where customers do not understand/find the right expense line on their bank statements.
  3. PayOps will share a refund letter with banking details with the customer. Explain that the customer needs to show their bank the refund letter to track the refund.

PayPal refund not received.

If it has been less than 30 days since the refund was issued or payment was made.
  • Ask the customer to contact PayPal support (who will ask them to Verify Email Address, and bank account).
  • Ask the customer to confirm the email address and check the email account to claim the payment.
  • The funds may not be returned to the original source, the customer needs to check their PayPal Balance.

Note: For instructions on how to confirm an email address, refer the customer to PayPal Customer Service: https://www.paypal.com/smarthelp/contact-us or +1-888-221-1161.

  • The customer won’t receive the refund until they confirm the email address.
  • The email address must be confirmed before you can make it the primary email address on the PayPal account.

Note: Ensure that the email they sent the refund to is added to their PayPal account and is confirmed.
