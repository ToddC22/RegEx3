Use this KEY to determine who supports the issue:

Note: Look for the Line of Business (LOB) KEY to follow the guidance. If you don’t find your LOB listed, see All Others column.

PRIMARY YES NO
You support this scenario as main support LOB.

Expect to receive transfers from advocates who do not support the scenario.  

You support this scenario

Do not transfer customers to another group as you are expected to provide support.

 You can transfer the customer to “PRIMARY” LOB.

 If the row has no PRIMARY then there is no owning team. See the Reference/Notes column for guidance.  

 

Transfer reference:

See KB 4100081 Guideline: Microsoft and the Australian and New Zealand Consumer Law for more information. Where a consumer has that right, you must follow the ACL guideline in all scenarios listed in this article.
