Summary

Who should follow this procedure?
 
  • Frontline Support Engineer (SE)

What is this procedure about?
  • This procedure provides directions on how Frontline SE verifies account ownership under certain security conditions.

When should this procedure be followed?
  • Frontline SE follows this procedure whenever a policy or procedure requires account ownership validation.

back to top
 

Procedure

This procedure is used when account ownership cannot be verified by the Frontline Service Engineers (SEs) and must be sent to the Technical Advisor (TA) team for validation by legal documents.

To engage the TA team, locate the customer’s scenario below and then send an email to O365DPTA@microsoft.com using the scenario’s template.

Make sure to provide the requested information, including the correct subject. Failure to submit the request correctly can result in delay or denial of the request.
