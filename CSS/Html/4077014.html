2. Policy

Agents from *any* frontline team will support partner with their question by using the content of this Quick Reference Guide (QRG).

Important: a partner-facing version of this QRG is published here and can be provided to the partner.

The partner-facing version is available in 10 languages: German, Spanish, French, Italian, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese

Note: the resource with an asterisk (*) are available to MPN members only. If a link includes “en-us” as the locale, then change it to the partner’s locale if needed (e.g. de-de for German, it-it for Italian, es-es for Spanish, fr-fr for French, ko-ko for Korean, pt-pt for Portuguese, ru-ru for Russian, tr-tr for Turkish, zh-hans for Simplified Chinese, zh-hant for Traditional Chinese).

3.2 "Find a Disti" questions

Where can I find a VL Disti?

  • Via Partner Center:  https://www.microsoft.com/en-US/solution-providers/search
    1. Type in “distributor” in the search field
    2. Select customer company size
    3. Select your country (or search by distance from your location)
    4. Click on the partner name or logo to read their profile and find their website URL (or click on the “Select Provider” button for one or more partners and then click the “contact selected providers” button)

Note: If there are no results, see the “Where can I find an OEM Disti?” question below – most disti’s do both VL and OEM.

Where can I find a CSP Disti (a.k.a. CSP Indirect Provider)?

  • Refer to https://partnercenter.microsoft.com/en-us/partner/find-a-provider
    (go to aka.ms/csp -> scroll down to “Grow your business with CSP” section -> click on button “Find providers” just above this section)

Where can I find an OEM Disti?

  • Refer to https://devicepartner.microsoft.com/connect/distributor

How do I become a reseller?

  • Refer to https://partner.microsoft.com/licensing/become-a-licensing-reseller
    Note: This page now only mentions CSP Indirect Reseller. If partner wants to become a new VL/OEM reseller, ask them to contact a VL/OEM Disti.

How do I get listed as a partner on Partner Center Search?

  • Refer to aka.ms/PCprofile. If partner needs further help, refer to aka.ms/rsc and start a chat.

What’s the difference between EA and Select Plus?

  • Refer to Disti

How can I change my VL agreement? (for example, change MPN ID linked)

    • Refer to www.explore.ms (Disti/LAR only) -> to receive support, submit a CLT ticket

Questions related to complex licensing situations involving new technologies or combinations of products

What are the downgrade rights of product xyz? How can I license Microsoft 365 Apps for enterprise subscription in VL? How do we correctly license server products in a virtual environment?

For VL licensing:

  • Refer to Disti
  • VERY IMPORTANT! Advise partner to look up in the current version of Microsoft Product Terms: http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?Mode=3&DocumentTypeId=53 (for on-prem products) or Microsoft Online Services Terms: http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?Mode=3&DocumentTypeId=46 (for Cloud products)
  • Refer to the Licensing Briefs: https://www.microsoft.com/licensing/learn-more/volume-licensing-briefs.aspx
  • If Disti is unable to help/partner still has doubts either recommend engaging with their MS contacts or if they don’t have an MS contact, refer to aka.ms/rsc and start a chat. The RSC agent will engage the LicenseQ team following the instructions described in section 3.5 How to engage the LicenseQ team and deliver the answer back to the partner.

For CSP:

  • If Indirect Reseller, refer to Disti (aka Indirect Provider)
  • VERY IMPORTANT! Advise partner to look up in the current version of Microsoft Online Services Terms: http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?Mode=3&DocumentTypeId=46
  • Refer to Partner Center: https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/partner-center/csp-documents-and-learning-resources

(aka.ms/pc -> Learn More in top bar -> Partner Center Help -> TOC section “Enroll in the Cloud Solution Provider program” -> sub-section “CSP agreements, price lists, and offers)

  • If Disti is unable to help/partner still has doubts, refer partner to the CSP team (use chat button on http://aka.ms/csp)
  • CSP team: please follow the instructions contained in article id 4293628 How to handle CSP licensing questions

For Partner IUR Benefit License:

  • VERY IMPORTANT! If the question is for an MPN IUR benefit license, advise partner to look up in the current version of the MPN-MAPS Product Usage Guide:

https://assets.microsoft.com/en-us/MPN-MAPS-Product-Usage-Guide.pdf

  • If Partner still has doubts, refer them to the MPN team (use chat button on http://aka.ms/rsc).

3.4 Product Licensing

TIP: the first place to look for licensing terms, conditions, and supplemental information relevant to the use of products and services licensed through Microsoft Volume Licensing programs is this:

http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?mode=1 → Look under Document Type

SKU-specific questions:

What is xyz SKU for? SKU xyz has been retired, what’s the replacement SKU? What’s the step-up SKU of this product? What’s the pre-requisite to purchase this SKU?

  1. Refer to Disti
  2. If partner is disti or did not get support from disti:
    1. Refer to LicenseWise (for Open, OV(S)): https://licensewise.microsoft.com/default.aspx*
    2. Refer to Partner Quotes (for EA and SCE): https://partner.microsoft.com/licensing/resources-and-tools*
  3. VERY IMPORTANT! Advise partner to look up in the current version of Microsoft Product Terms: http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?Mode=3&DocumentTypeId=53 (for on-prem products) or Microsoft Online Services Terms: http://www.microsoftvolumelicensing.com/DocumentSearch.aspx?Mode=3&DocumentTypeId=46 (for Cloud products)
  4. VERY IMPORTANT! If partner is CSP and his/her question is about Dynamics 365 purchased via the CSP channel, please follow the instructions contained in article id 4293628 How to handle CSP licensing questions.

If the Disti is unable to help/partner still has doubts either recommend engaging with MS contacts. Neither the RSC nor LicenseQ can help with SKU questions.

Product feature questions:

What products does the O365 E5 suite include? What’s the difference between EXO P1 and P2? Can product X do XYZ?

  • Research the public info available on the concerned product via www.microsoft.com (ex.: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/cloud-platform/windows-server-pricing)
  • Refer to https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/solutions
    Note: there may a link that can only be accessed by active MPN partners, if so advise partner to enroll (and use chat button if they need help with that)
  • Info on compatibility and features can be found at https://technet.microsoft.com/
  • Advise partner to submit an online case to Partner Technical Consultants (PTC) via https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/support/partner-advisory-hours.

For detailed instructions, advise partner to read this public article:

https://support.microsoft.com/kb/3121537*

3.5 How to engage the LicenseQ team

Important: Before engaging the LicenseQ team, please review the LicenseQ Scope table of scenarios.

Submit a licensing question using the new “Ask LicenseQ” form.

Note: SLA is 2 business days.

Read the instructions on screen and click on the GetHelp submission form hyperlink:

[Asset 4576785]

In the next screen, insert Partner’s case number and click Next:

[Asset 4576787]

More mandatory details will be requested in the following screen, so include them accurately and click on Next:

[Asset 4576788]

Very Important: In the last part of the form, please complete the requested details accurately and in order to ensure routing to LicenseQ team is done, make sure to select as the Issue/Problem Area -> Program and in the search box for Programs -> LincenseQ - <Support Language> (e.g. LicenseQ – English)

[Asset 4576789]

Complete the remainder of the form and click Submit.

3.6 New escalation path

As an alternative to the LicenseQ team – or if the LicenseQ team could not help – agents can escalate to MPN Global support.

Agents using CAP should escalate via Child SR to the MPN Help Escalations queue, after making sure they have selected the most appropriate issue code (the correct issue code is Presales -> Product Questions -> Licensing Questions).
