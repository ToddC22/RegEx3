1. General information

What is Microsoft 365?

New name. More benefits. Same Price.

Microsoft 365 is Microsoft’s collection of powerful productivity apps and services, designed to empower people and organizations to accomplish their goals in both work and life. Microsoft 365 is available for consumers, small businesses, enterprise, governments, and education. It includes both free experiences and paid subscriptions. Paid subscriptions unlock premium features and benefits.

For consumers, Microsoft 365 is an evolution of Office 365, expanding the scope of productivity to include apps with new features and benefits, all under one subscription. In late April, Microsoft 365 replaces Office 365 with new features, apps, and benefits. One of the big changes is the rebranding to Microsoft 365. See the table below:

Note: Former service subscribers should only renew using renewal notifications in-app, or by email. Once the customer selects a renewal link from one of these notifications, the customer is directed to a product detail page (PDP) with the discount. This page is not accessible by the public. If the former subscriber purchases or redeems from the regular Microsoft 365 Personal page, they are changing their subscription SKU and will no longer be on the former services subscriber SKU (which enables the discount).

If the customer stays active, with recurring billing turned on, they will continue to receive the annual discount (6,384 JPY/year). Their subscription, although renamed to Microsoft 365 Personal, will still have backend identifiers confirming they should receive the renewal pricing every year. If at some point, they turn off recurring billing, and the subscription expires, or they cancel the subscription they no longer will get the renewal discount.

2.2 Teams

The power to organize and connect with friends and family, all in one app. A marketing webpage will communicate that Teams is coming soon and that customers can sign up for notification once the preview becomes available. See here for the link to the preview.

This will be for a mobile app.

[Asset 4555263]

Stay organized and connected with family and friends all in one place with the Microsoft Teams mobile app, now in preview with personal features coming soon. This all-in-one communication hub makes it easy to communicate, stay connected, and plan together with family and friends, no matter where you are. With Teams you can easily find people, files, photos, conversations, tasks, and calendars in one convenient and secure place.  With a simple, centralized view, you and your groups can stay on top of important personal events, tasks, and shared locations. Plus, all those great ideas, documents and conversations are readily accessible and saved securely in the cloud.

  • Get more out of chats: Microsoft Teams is your mobile messaging hub for all your personal chats. Start a video chat, assign tasks to people, edit calendars, send a GIF, and access cloud storage, right from your group chats, so you can get the most out of your conversations. Teams is available on iOS and Android making it easy to communicate with family and friends.
  • Keep everyone on the same page: Teams gives you and your groups a centralized view to help everyone stay on top of multiple calendars, activities, and shared tasks. Important shared events, files, documents, photos, and videos stay within your group chat, making them easily accessible in one place.
  • Collaborate more efficiently: With the Teams mobile app, you can easily share documents, tackle projects together, do a family budget or help kids with their homework using Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Your ideas, documents, and conversations saved in the cloud and are easily accessible across devices.
  • Easily share location and information: Let your family know you are running late with location sharing so they can rest easy knowing where you are. Plus, with Safe, a secured place in Teams, you can confidently store and share important information like passwords, rewards numbers, or login information. You may never need to remember your passwords or important data again.

2.3 Business or Personal accounts

Consumers who use the personal features in Teams will sign in using their Microsoft account by selecting Personal. If they have Teams, say for work or school, they choose to sign in using the Business option. 

Once Teams is available for preview, users have the option to sign in with their Microsoft account, or to sign up for a new Microsoft account.

If the user doesn’t have a Microsoft account and they select Sign up for free, they see an option to select Personal or Business. Personal gives the option to sign up for personal use, while Business is the free version out now.

3.1 Money in Excel

Money in Excel is a dynamic, smart template and add-in for Excel. It will help users feel aware of where their money is going, empowered to make progress on financial goals, and in control of their financial well-being. Because it’s an Excel spreadsheet, users can use existing Excel features such as filtering, soring, copy/past, and formulas.

With Money in Excel, users can:

  • Securely connect their bank and credit card accounts to import transaction data into an Excel spreadsheet.
  • Categorize transactions.
  • View personalized insights.

3.9 OneDrive Personal Vault

[Asset 4555272]

OneDrive Personal Vault is a place to store your most important and sensitive files and photos. It is a folder which requires additional security (two-step verification or strong authentication) to access. Files in this folder are private and secured while the Personal Vault is in lock mode.

Subscribers can store as many folders as they want (up to their storage limit).

Q: What is the difference between Office 365 and Microsoft 365?

A: Microsoft 365 includes everything customer’s loved about Office 365 and much more. With Microsoft 365, customers receive additional information including Microsoft Family Safety app (coming soon), Microsoft Editor, Money in Excel premium creative content in Office, and Windows 10 support.

Q: Why did Microsoft change the name from Office 365 to Microsoft 365?

A: To be more reflective of the range of benefits within the subscription, which now extends beyond the premium Office apps to include Microsoft Family Safety app (coming soon), Microsoft Editor, and more.

Q: What does this mean to existing Office 365 subscribers?

A: Their Office 365 subscription will automatically update to Microsoft 365.

[Asset 4555273]

Q: Will there be changes to Office perpetual products?

A: No, the Office perpetual products will remain the same.

Q: Will there be a price increase?

A: No, subscription costs will not change.

  • Note: Microsoft periodically assesses its pricing to ensure reasonable alignment with the needs of customers, partners, and the marketplace, and may make changes in response to that assessment and feedback.

Q: Who handles the support sign-up for partner programs?

A: The partner.

Q: Do customers have to sign-up and login to partners programs using the Microsoft account used to activate the subscription?

A: The authentication process uses the customer’s Microsoft account to redeem offers, but it’s not required use the Microsoft account for account creation. 

Q: I see partners mentioned – is this the same as partner billing?

A: No, partner billing refers to partners who sell and manage a customer’s Microsoft 365 purchase, like Apple, Best Buy, or Telenor.

  • Note: Partner programs are companies who are partnering with Microsoft 365 to offer their services at a discounted price.

Q: If a customer cancels their Microsoft 365 subscription, how does this impact any of the partner program benefits?

A: There is no impact. Once you sign up, the partner terms and conditions apply.

Q: Are partner program benefits available during a Microsoft 365 trial?

A: No.

Q: For Microsoft 365 Family customers, does the partner program extend to shared members?

A: Yes. Shared members must sign in with their own personal Microsoft account and create their own log-in at the partner program site.

Q: What if an existing subscriber already has an account with the partner? Will they get any benefits from being a Microsoft 365 customer?

A: Customers login with their existing account.

5.4 Family Safety app

Q: Do you need a Microsoft 365 to use the Family Safety app?

A: Customers do not need to have Microsoft 365, but feature releases within the app might be only available with a Microsoft 365 subscription.

Q: Does the Family Safety app work well with the Family features in Microsoft launcher on Android?

A: Yes, the Family Safety app will give a full view of your family member’s settings and access to features only available within the app. There is no conflict to using both Microsoft Launcher and the Family Safety app together on Android.

Q: Why doesn’t a child’s device appear on the managed devices or “activities being tracked”?

A: The app may be missing permissions or the child’s Microsoft account isn’t signed in. Advise the family member to open the Family Safety app on that child’s device. Once opened, the app detects if any permissions are missing for full functionality. Also check to ensure the child’s Microsoft account sign in.

Q: Why aren’t my child’s websites I set as block, not blocked from the device?

A: Family Safety uses Microsoft Edge. If they’d like to prevent the child from using a different browser, you can set limits or block usage of other browsers via the app limits features.

Q: Where can I sign up for the preview?

A: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/family-safety

See KB 4553544 Informational: Family Safety App.
