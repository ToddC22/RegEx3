What is this article about?

This Tool User Guide familiarizes you with the layout and functionality of the Product Activation Customer Service Representative (PACSR) tool. It also provides you with an opportunity to view two simulated types of activations. Rely on your online resources and the recorded demonstrations of the tool to gather a complete understanding of the tool and its requirements.

To find out what information agents/advocates can document and with what approved tools, including what information can be shared with other teams when transferring, follow KB 4051312 Policy: MPA: Product Activation Information Capturing & Sharing
