2. Payment on account (POA) - Overpayment amount visibility

Customers sometimes make overpayments to their account for various reasons, but afterwards they don’t have a way to see the amount that was overpaid.

Microsoft has decided to provide a way for them to see the overpaid amount by using Ibiza and/or Admin Center. CST/CMAT will have to provide a similar capability to Support Engineers so that they can address customers query around overpayments.

CMAT:

  • Overpayment details in CMAT will be displayed in Payment On Account, which is within the Billing section:[Asset 4535415]

    • Since Partners can have payments made in multiple currencies, we cannot show the total overpayment amount in CMAT but can show the payment and currency details, so if needed, the total overpayment per currency can be calculated offline.

      • The tab will display all payments made on the account.

CST:

  • Total overpayment will show as Unallocated amount.
  • CST will show overpayment details under Billing Profile + Payment Instruments as well as Legacy dashboard view. under Billing Profile à Payment on Account Legacy dashboard view

3. Refund on Returned Item

There will be times when a customer uses the self-serve return/cancel feature to return service (for example, Reservations). Self-service cancellation will provide pro-rated refund that the customer may contest and request for full refund. Previously, there was no ability to provide on returned item and support agent will have to escalate such cases to engineering team.

Now, CST provides the ability to provide a refund (remaining amount only) on a returned item. To provide a refund:

  1. Go to the Purchases blade and look up the item and then select the Refund/Credit option. The user will be able to refund up to remaining value of the service.
    [Asset 4535420]

    The rest of the flows remains unchanged once the user selects the Refund/Credit option.

4. Disable Override for Databricks Returns

Databricks reservations is transaction-based service meaning if customer cancels the service, they will get a pro-rated refund based on the remaining transactions. Overriding a refund to provide more than system calculated refund will be a loss situation for Microsoft.

CST has now blocked the override refund option for Databricks reservations. The “Override” link will be grayed out and will not be clickable.
[Asset 4535421]
