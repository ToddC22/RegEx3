2.2 FAQ

Question

Answer

How to tell when the customer’s Office support start date begins
  1. In MST look under Managed Subscription.
  2. Look at the Start Date of the Office product.
  3. If it is within 90 days of Start Date you can offer free Technical Support for perpetual products.

What are the system requirements for Office 2019?
  • Office 2019 is compatible with Windows 10 or the three most recent versions of macOS. See system requirements.

What if customers don’t meet Office 2019 system requirements?
  • Recommend upgrading to Microsoft 365 or upgrading their operating system.

Do we offer a Windows and Office 2019 incentive bundle?
  • No. There is currently no Windows + Office incentive bundle.

What should I do if a customer wants to upgrade their OS to Windows 10?
  • Transfer to a Windows support advocate or team.

3. Get Current offers and Get Current process

Important Note:

Get Current is:

  • limited to 1 license per 1 Microsoft Account. Any additional license upgrades must be purchased or POBO at full price. For example:
  • If a customer seeks to upgrade 2 or more licenses, the Get Current offering only applies to 1 of them.
  • If a customer or small business owns multiple Microsoft accounts seeking multiple license upgrades against each of them, Get Current only applies to 1 license for 1 of the Microsoft accounts.
  • Support cases may be subject to CAAs If multiple licenses are found discounted during an interaction.
  • Not valid with any other discount or promotions. Follow the Purchase on Behalf of (POBO) procedure while helping customers complete orders.
    • Important: See KB 4578369 Procedure: Purchase on behalf of (POBO) Consent and KB 4053527 TUG: MST: Purchase on behalf of (POBO) for information on POBO and mandatory consent actions.
  • No Get Current offers are available for Russia. Effective October 2nd, 2018, Get Current offers, including Preferred Payment Instrument (PPI), are not available in Russia.

Post Install support for Office perpetual products are not free after the first 90 days, and the customer may have a better Office experience on Microsoft 365 Subscription, which is always up to date with latest features and comes with unlimited free support.

When you are providing Get current options to a customer, always lead with Microsoft 365 subscription products. 

Below are the Get Current options:

  • Microsoft 365 Family or Personal:  On purchase of Microsoft 365 Family or Microsoft 365 Personal 1 year subscription, with recurring billing set to on, we will provide the customer with 3-months free additional time on the subscription.
  • Office 2019 SKUs: Purchase the latest perpetual version of Office 2019 standalone, Home and Student 2019 or Home and Business 2019 at a discount.
  • Prices vary by market.
  • The $40 SKU makes it challenging to take a clean 20% off the product, especially across all markets. In some cases, the discount may be too advantageous. In others, it’s disadvantageous. You need to calculate the Get Current price to not exceed 25%. If you cannot make it work to benefit the customer within 25%, do not offer the Get Current discount.

Note:

5. Case coding

Coding product category: The product category selected is the product the customer is contacting us about. For example: If the customer has an issue downloading Office 2013 but agrees to Get Current to Microsoft 365, use the product category for Office 2013.
