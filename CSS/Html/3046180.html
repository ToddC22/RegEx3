Who should use this article?

What is this article about?

This article is a side navigation that provides tool guidance for Assisted Support Desktop (ASD).
ASD is used by all advocates during customer support sessions, including locating data, history and documenting service requests. In addition, ASD provides access to support tools specific to your profile and the line of business. 

Please use the Side Navigation pane on the left-hand side to find the information you're looking for.
