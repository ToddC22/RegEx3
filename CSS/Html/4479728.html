1. Procedure

1.1 About the “Forgot my username” feature

The Forgot my username feature is a process that customers can use to recover their account directly from the web or their Xbox One console, if they forgot their username and can’t sign in. It will provide them with a wizard that will help them through the recovering process, using their security contact information to send a hint of their associated username.

When following the Forgot my username flow, any Microsoft accounts associated with the email or phone number they filled in will be displayed with masked characters.

Return to top

1.2 When to use the "Forgot my username" flow

Use this flow when customers can’t sign into their account because they forgot their username.

The list of usernames will give them hints on the account they are trying to log in. If the hint allows customers to recognize or remember one of their usernames, they'll need to select Sign in to start the sign in process flow.

  • If they receive an error stating the username does not exist, it may mean customers did not properly fill in the masked characters. Recommend they try again.
  • If the username is accepted but the password is failing, it means that they forgot their password or are trying to sign in to an existing account which they may not own.
  • If they are confident enough they are using the right username, and their password is still not accepted, guide them to the Reset Password flow. KB 2984712 Procedure: Microsoft account: Password reset

Note: This feature relies on remembering one of the following:

  • Alternate email address associated with their Microsoft account.
  • Phone number associated with their Microsoft account.

2.1 Web experience

  1. On the Sign in page, select the Sign-in options, and Forgot my username option to enter either an alternate email or phone number you believe is associated with the account.

4. FAQ

Q: Is the feature available on Xbox.com?

A: Yes, as part of the Web experience.

Q: Can I use this if I have Two Step Verification or Two Factor Authentication turned on?

A: Yes.

Q: Will customers see the error for ‘Too Many Attempts’ to send a code in one day?

A: Yes, this is normal and could happen.

Q: Can proofs in the 30-day waiting period be used?

A: Yes, but not to access security pages or purchase.

Q: Can evicted accounts (active = false) be looked up?

A: No.

Q: Are new users being added to the database during sign up?

A: Not specifically during account creation. They are added to the database when proofs are added.
