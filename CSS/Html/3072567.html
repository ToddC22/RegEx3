1. Overview

1.1 Consulting on cases, issues, requests

Often advocates need to engage with a higher tier or group to help resolve issues for customers. Most customer-based issues can be resolved by Tier 1/Team Leads, or through engagement with an Advanced Capability Advocate (ACA). For issues not based on a specific customer issue, but more around our services to customers, this require a CSS Command Center (CSS CC) escalation. The following table outlines which scenarios require what escalation and how to best support that escalation:

Consultation Group

Scenarios

Process

CSS Command Center (CSS CC)
  • Events that are impacting greater than 10% of advocates
  • Tools fully down
  • Purchase flow fully down – via GC or Store website, customer, or advocate
  • Sudden increase in order issues outside of normal volume (for example, spike in MS Fraud Hold, Cancelled Orders, Submit Terminal Orders)
  • Telecom issues

KB 3038858 Procedure: How and when to engage CSS Command Center (CSS CC) all regions 

Team Leads
  • Product pricing
  • Product inventory concerns
  • Promotion issues – setup and redemption
  • Problem with website content
  • Problem with product availability after purchase
  • Process documentation/guidance requests
  • Tool enablement/improvement requests
  • Undocumented issues
  • I’m not sure

Social Media

Escalations from the Social Media team will be accepted if the customer provides the previous SR for the support experience that they are encountering issues with. If the customer has not previously contacted Microsoft support, the Social Media advocate will ask the customer to contact support to receive assistance with their issue instead of escalating.

Customer requests free Gamertag change due to their personal data being part of their current Gamertag

Offline Team/Rangers

The customer’s current Gamertag contains any information that can be used to identify or contact a customer. This information includes:

  • The customer’s real name
  • The customer’s telephone number
  • The customer’s email address

Do not request free Gamertag change from Offline team or Ranger for any reason other than personal data Gamertag.

Offline advocates should refer to the Advanced Capability Empowerment Handbook OneNote for instructions on how to apply free Gamertag change flag on the account.
