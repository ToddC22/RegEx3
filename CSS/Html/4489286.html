1. Overview

⇒ What is Cloud Enablement Desk (CED)?

CED provides a guided, personalized experience to help partners better leverage MPN resources, while building and advancing their Cloud skills, as well as, Go to Market capabilities.

The team consists of Cloud Program Specialists worldwide, currently providing support in 12 languages.

⇒ How will a CED specialist help partners?

A CED specialist will help partners learn about the MPN program, understand the available benefits, and drive towards the milestones and necessary results that are important for the partner’s business.

[Asset 4489289]

⇒ What are the requirements for a partner to engage the CED specialists?

1. non-PDM managed partners

2. Have an MPN ID (sign up here)

3. Develop repeatable and scalable application or solution that can become go-to-market or co-sell ready

Note: for partners to meet one-on-one with technical experts, you must at least have an Action Pack Subscription.

⇒ How do I engage with a CED specialist?

  1. Digital experience promotion: partners who qualify for the CED program will be promoted through the digital experience.
  2. Field-nomination: field can bulk nominate partners for the CED program by filling this form.
    Important note: CED should engage directly with the field. For more information on the field engagement guidelines, please click here.
  3. Self-nomination: High-potential partners can self-nominate by filling this form. 
    Important note: The self-nomination form should be shared only with high-potential partners.
  4. OCP programmatic referral: Partners who qualify for the CED program flagged by the Frontline Agents, Red Carpet, SAM teams, GTM Resource Desk, etc.

The minimum required collaboration with a CED specialist is a monthly call. The partner can scale up this collaboration based on their business needs.

2. Additional background

Cloud Enablement Desk specialists can help partners with:

  • Assisted expertise from Microsoft to help partners start, expand, and co-sell in the cloud space based on the partner type
  • Assisted expertise to guide partners through the MPN benefits, competency attainment process, and co-sell process based on partner type needs
  • Assisted expertise to help partners understand and efficiently use the Gold cloud competency benefits, and stay up to date about MPN benefits updates

Cloud Enablement Desk (CED) led engagement


*two service categories for qualifying non-PDM managed partners

Accelerate to Go-to-Market (GTM) ready

*Tele-assisted practice building acceleration for high-potential unmanaged partners

Onboard to Co-Sell Ready

*CED specialists will act as temporary PDM to onboard unmanaged partners into the Co-Sell tools and program.
Assist partners to build their marketing capabilities and direct them to the best available resources. Introduce Co-Sell program and advise partners of Co-Sell/IP Co-Sell requirements based on their solution area/type.
Guide partners to achieve a Silver or Gold competency and activate their benefits. Help partners build and validate Co-Sell BOM.
Connect partners with Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) for deep technical skills and pre-sales consultations once they have a MAPs or a competency (partners receive unlimited access) Attach Co-Sell solutions to OCP CRM profile.
Assist partners with Marketplace Onboarding and connect them with GTM Desk/Marketplace Rewards Desk If the Co-Sell option is available in Partner Center for their offer type, guide partner with Co-Sell Onboarding in Partner Center.
Guide partners on how to set up business profile and complete regular verification of the lead sharing status Process exemptions and request approval from Co-Sell Desk once all requirements are completed. 
Which partners qualify?
Partners must have an MPN ID and be a non-PDM managed partner. They must also purchase at least MAPs to engage PTCs or attain a Silver/Gold competency to engage GTM Desk. 		 Which partners qualify?
Partner must meet the Co-Sell/IP Co-Sell Ready requirements that aligns with their solution area and type.

4. CED process to refer partners to GTM team

Scenario 1: Partner needs support to complete BOM – Please note this service is available only for partners that obtain/renew a competency.

CED has already sent to the partner the BOM guides and templates BOM Link.

  1. Partner has already started to work on BOM and has a draft version. The partner needs help to complete/improve the BOM.
  2. CED can offer GTM support to accelerate the BOM completion.
  3. Before referring the partner to GTM team, CED needs to make sure:
    1. Partner has the GTM benefit activated. For the process, follow article 4471278.
    2. Partner activates the specific benefits(s) in PC they need help with:
  • Co-Branded Marketing Assets: Presentation
  • Co-Branded Marketing Assets: Case Study
  • Co-Branded Marketing Assets: One-Pager

Note: If the partner does not see the benefits they are eligible for, or have any trouble activating, the partner needs to open a new support request directly from the Partner Center Dashboard.

GTM SLA: 2 business days. The partner will receive an email with instructions on next steps once they successfully activate the benefits in Partner.

6. CED completes the form here: https://aka.ms/GTM_activation_desks.

7. GTM will keep CED posted on the BOM progress provided we select this option and add our alias in the form.

8. In case we have issues: the partner is not contacted, CED has no visibility, please email GTM@microsof.com and copy your supervisor for visibility.

Scenario 2: Partner completes the Marketing benefits

If the partner wants to continue to accelerate time to market, generate demand, and expand their business after completing the CED Marketing consultation, please complete items 5 and 6 above to connect the partner with the GTM desk.

Scenario 3: Partner is Co-Sell Ready/IP-Co-Sell Ready

  1. Before referring to GTM for Business Profile Optimization and Referral Management, and How to market with MS, CED makes sure the partner:
    • Is Co-Sell, IP-Co-Sell Ready
    • Partner has the GTM benefit activated. For the process, follow article 4471278
    • Partner activates the specific benefits(s) in PC
      • For Co-Sell:
        • Co-Branded Social Marketing Assets
        • Partner Success Story
        • Press Release with Microsoft Executive Quote
      • For IP-Co-Sell:
        • Co-Branded Social Marketing Assets
        • Partner Success Story
        • Press Release with Microsoft Executive Quote

Note: If the partner does not see the benefits they are eligible for, or have any trouble activating, the partner needs to open a new support request directly from the Partner Center Dashboard.

GTM SLA: 2 business days. The partner will receive an email with instructions on next steps once they successfully activate the benefits in Partner.

2. CED completes the form here: https://aka.ms/GTM_activation_desks.

3. In case we have issues: the partner is not contacted, CED has no visibility, please email GTM@microsof.com and copy your supervisor for visibility.
