Procedure: VLS/DEV/WWL: Transfer to O365 Commerce/Billing & O365 Data Protection (Photocopy)

[Asset 4489629]

Description

This article outlines the procedure of transferring ownership of a Service Desk case (via Photocopy) to the following teams when the customer's issue is out of scope for the VL/DEV/WWL teams:

  • Office 365 Commerce/Billing
  • Data Protection team (for issues such as password reset or IW elevation when no admin exists on the tenant)

 

Applies to

  • Region: All (Global) 
  • Role: 
    • Volume License (VL), Developer Program (DEV) and World-Wide Learning (WWL) Advocates (commercial LOB’s)
  • Service: Partner, Unified Support
  • Product: Cloud products (commercial billing issues & data protection issues)
  • Country: All
  • Language: All

Additional Info on Service Desk & Rave Cases

 

Check if the caller is a customer of a Partner

Some customers who purchased their subscription through a partner are required to contact the partner for support. Follow the instructions below to determine call handling.

  1. Refer to the Partner Information section
  2. Search for the following entries:
    1. A Partner name appears:
      1. Search for the partner type listed on the right to the partner name, such as:
        • Syndicated
        • Reseller
        • Support Partner
      2. Select the right arrow to expand the Partner Details
      3. Process the call according to the partner type:
        • SyndicatedPartnerTenant: (NOAM only):
          • Refer to the customer to support.godaddy.com or 480-505-8877.
        • ResellerPartnerTenant:
          • Refer the caller to their partner. If the customer does not know their Partner, this information can be found in the Admin Center at admin.microsoft.com, under the support area.
        • Support PartnerTenant:
          • Proceed with case creation.
    2. No Partner name appears:
      1. This entry indicates that the tenant is “not” a customer of a partner
      2. Proceed to the case creation

IMPORTANT

Before transferring any case, beyond the specifics outlined below for this scenario, you must ensure you are also following all requirements outlined in the General Guidelines for all Transfers above.

While the specific steps vary by scenario, they always include:

  • Update the case internal title to reflect it was a misroute
  • Including in the case title why you are transferring the case to another team
  • Tenant name, and issue description

Important:

  • Do not include any PII in the title
  • Make titles as concise as possible, while Service Desk allows very long titles, Rave does not
  • Service Desk cases’ notes are not visible in the rave case

 

  1. Before transferring a case from Service Desk to the Office 365 Commerce/Billing team or Office 365 Data Protection, perform the following tasks:
 

O365 Commerce/Billing

O365 Data Protection

Verify that the case hasn't previously been transferred to Rave

Important

Previous transfers strictly within Service Desk do not impact this, only a previous transfer to Rave (which creates a duplicate Photocopy case) prevents transferring to a Rave queue.

 

Each Service Desk case can only be photocopied to Rave ONCE, either at case creation time or case transfer time.

  1. If the Service Desk case was transferred to you from elsewhere, look to see if the Assigned To field shows offagent@microsoft.com.
  2. If so this indicates that the case was already transferred to Rave previously and cannot be transferred again. Create a new case for the customers issue and transfer it to the O365 Commerce/Billing team or O365 Data Protection team.

Do Not Close Service Desk Case

Do not close the Service Desk case manually. Closing the Rave case will close the linked Service Desk case. However, closing the Service Desk case will not automatically close the photocopied Rave case.

Scope is not Clear

If a webform case customer has not provide enough information to accurately define the scope, please call the customer and collect the required information.

If the issue is determined to be in scope for O365 Commerce/Billing, inform the customer that you will transfer the case and provide the Rave case # during the phone call – no additional email needs to be sent.

Scope is Clearly O365 Commerce

If the scope is clearly an issue for O365 Commerce send an email from Service Desk using the template below.

Tip

Even after the Service Desk case has been transferred to Rave, you can still send email from Service Desk to the customer.

New SR number xxxx – your case is transferred to the Office 365 commerce team

Automatic Routing and Case Closure

Photocopied Rave case will automatically be assigned to the proper Rave queue based on the related SAP information from Service Desk.

Don't close the Service Desk case manually. Closing the Rave case will close the linked Service Desk case. However, closing the Service Desk case will not automatically close the photocopied Rave case.
