1. Overview

Offline payment refers to bank wire transfer for MAPS or Competency orders created in Partner Center for migrated partners. The partner has the option to either choose credit card or bank wire transfer. The Offline PI tool can be accessed via https://offlinepitool.azurewebsites.net/list.

2.1 Electronic bank transfer payment process

Partners can select their payment preference between credit card and electronic bank transfer (EBT) when they purchase subscriptions on either MAPS/Competency.

[Asset 4509749]

[Asset 4516457]

If the partner’s preferred method of payment is Electronic bank transfer, in the next screen the partner will be prompted to review the TAX ID, the purchase details, read the Agreement and then Submit the request.  

[Asset 4516458]

[Asset 4516459]

After the request is submitted partner will be asked to upload the payment proof: Payment date, the account where payment is sent and attach the payment proof document.

[Asset 4516461]

After completing all these steps, a green message will be displayed letting partner know that the request was completed successfully. Partner can always go back to this screen and upload payment proof in the Membership Offers left menu tab, under Purchase Offers section.

[Asset 4516462]

All this information uploaded will be visible in Offline PI under a new column added named: Payment proof uploaded.

[Asset 4516463]

After the payment is successfully completed, the partner’s purchase will appear as active 48-72 hours after it reaches Microsoft’s bank account.

Partner can switch from Electronic bank transfer payment preference to Credit card payment method.

If partners have decided that they would rather pay using Credit Card and they already have an unactioned electronic bank transfer order created in the system, they can cancel the order and repurchase using the preferred payment method.

In order to cancel an existing unactioned electronic bank transfer partners must first access the Membership Offers tab in PC Dashboard and click on the Cancel order and confirm the Cancel order option

3. India flow in Partner Center for Wire Transfer - unique payment method

Microsoft India has digitally signed all invoices and credit notes since July 17, 2020. The digitally signed pdf copies will be created by MPN Billing team and will be sent from SAP tool to partners directly, over the e-mail, based on the e-mail address of the user who created the order in PC.

Partners have been informed about this change through a message posted in PC:

[Asset 4469666]

If partners come to Frontline team claiming that they didn’t receive the invoice, Frontline team will escalate the case to MPN Billing, asking them to create a soft copy and attach it in the case for visibility purpose.  

For TDS, we allow partner to deduct the 10% and pay us 90% first. But we will have to continue to chase partner to send us back the TDS cert.

For TDS:

  1. Partner submits order in Partner Center, they will be provided with the total amount and tax amount to pay. (see screenshot)
  2. Partner submits payment at 90% of the order amount to MSFT bank account
  3. Billing team confirmed payment received of 90%
  4. Billing team activate orders
  5. India warehouse team get final tax invoice/receipt and send to partners
  6. Once final tax invoice is received partner goes to tax department for TDS filing - TDS tax cert is generated by India tax authority
  7. Partner sends the soft TDS tax cert to Varun
  8. Frontline team is responsible for making sure partner send us all the document for TDS exemption

For Tax exemption:

  1. Partner submits the order in Partner center, they will settle full amount
  2. Partner provides the tax exemption document
  3. We escalate the case to Tax team for approval
  4. Once this is approved, we will refund the amount
  5. Again, if partner insists to send the amount after tax, we might have to get tax team approval and then escalate to billing team

FAQs:

How the system or Billing team knows the partner requested TDS as there is nowhere in PC indicates the partner choose to deduct TDS amount? Is it the default if Billing team check the payment on membership or MAPS is 90% of the total amount?

Billing team process is to validate if the payment is 90% - if yes, activate the order and assumed that partner had deducted TDS. 

Will partner’s membership been hold off if partner did not submit TDS cert? What criteria and what is the business rule to hold off the membership?

Once we received 90% of the payment, benefit will be activated. Then invoice will be generated. Then invoice gets sent to the partners. Partners then use the invoice to submit TDS.

Can I check how my team can check which partner had paid but require to submit TDS cert?

We can expose this information to you via Billing team.

Does this mean my team has to conduct outbound call in asking partner to provide TDS cert as this is for outbound with additional funding required? If this is approved by OCP team, are there any communication can be shared?

Partner’s membership activation will be on hold if partner did not provide 2 past TDS cert, e.g. 2016 no TDS cert, 2017 no TDS cert, 2018 order placed and apocpay will have to on hold the membership. Apocpay will inform RSC why the membership is on hold, RSC will follow up with partner until partner submit back the TDS cert for 2016 and TDS cert for 2017. If partner is not willing to submit the document, partner will have the pay back the TDS amount deducted for the past 2 years.

If my team requires to conduct outbound, what happens if we try at least 3 attempts but we are not able to reach to partner?

After we try 3 fail attempts, partner will contact via inbound inquiry as they did not see the membership is being activated.

5. MPN Offer - upgrade discount from Silver to Gold level

Starting October 15, a new feature has been implemented for partners who want to upgrade their level from Silver to Gold. This means that Silver active partners get a 25% discount on Gold offer’s unit price while purchasing Gold membership.

Once you meet Gold eligibility criteria, you can purchase Gold membership by paying the Gold fee, calculated after 25% discount is applied automatically by the system. The new Gold Anniversary Date will be scheduled to one year from the date of purchase and you will get full Gold benefits.
