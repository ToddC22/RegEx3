What is this article about? 

This article describes the details of the various service subscriptions available to customers which are built on the modern platform, or “modern subscriptions”. The article also compares the difference between Modern and Legacy subscriptions.

All Xbox Live and Xbox Game Pass have been migrated to the modern platform including existing customers.

For Office, all customers (except customers from the United States) who purchase or redeem a new subscription are already on modern platform, and we are currently in migration phase for existing Microsoft 365 customers.

1. Dunning period 

The dunning period is the time where the customer can pay their renewal via console or on account.microsoft.com before the subscription is automatically expired. After the subscription expires, the customer will have to renew or repurchase the subscription.

3. Identifying modern vs legacy subscription in MST

  • Select Microsoft 365 Xbox Live, or Other Services to identify the subscription.
  • Under Manage subscriptions select Details to identify the subscription type or ID field.
    • Modern subscriptions will have mdr in ID and will be listed under Subscription type as Modern.
      [Asset 4092712]
    • Legacy subscriptions will not include mdr in subscription ID.
