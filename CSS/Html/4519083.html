3.2 Courier Pickup (CSC)

After the order is submitted, SingPost will contact the customer the next business day to arrange pickup.

Before pickup, the customer is expected to package the device into a sturdy shipping box, and using bubble wrap, newspapers, or other recyclable materials to securely pack the device.

The courier will arrive with appropriate paperwork for the pickup. Pickups can occur Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

SingPost will schedule a three-hour pick-up window. For missed pickups, or if customers are not contacted by SingPost within the above timeframe, customers will need to contact SingPost by calling 6845 6485 during business hours to arrange a pickup.

Return to top

3.3 Prepaid Box with Label (SST)

Surface Studio only. Once the box has been delivered, the customer should contact SingPost Monday - Friday during normal business hours by calling 6845 6485 to arrange collection.

Return to top

3.4 Advanced Exchange (SAD)

The service center will ship a replacement first. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, they should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the return instructions included to arrange collection. The customer should contact SingPost Monday - Friday during normal business hours by calling 6845 6485 to arrange collection.

Return to top

3.5 No Action Required (NCA)

The service center will ship a replacement and the customer is not required to return the non-functioning product.

Return to top

3.6 Overnight Shipping (NBD)

Firebird/White Label customers only. The service center will ship a replacement first with delivery the next business day if placed before 12 PM local time. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, they should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the return instructions included to arrange collection. For more details see KB 4034889 Procedure: Surface: "Firebird" White Label Call Handling – Onsite & Next Business Day Shipping.
