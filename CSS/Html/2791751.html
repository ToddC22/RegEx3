2. Guidance

The Microsoft verification and entitlement guidance protects customers and Microsoft against the fraudulent or unauthorized activity. Due to the sensitive nature of the support model, entitlement is limited to:

  • Organizational (Tenant) accounts, profiles, & assets: Global (Company/Tenant), Billing, BSP Administrator(s) ONLY;
  • Individual (Employee) accounts, profiles, & assets: The individual listed as the account / profile owner, or purchaser of the asset(s);
  • For VL customer: Activation email recipient/Online Service Manager (OSM) associated in VLSC and those in the list of OSMs in explore.ms;
  • For Advisor Partners on behalf of their customers: Admin advocate on the customer tenant or Global (Company) admin on the partner tenant and a DAP relationship with the customer tenant. For detail, see ﻿POBO Partner validation﻿ section (within a. Baseline verification expandable section from this article);
  • For cases transferred from Service Desk, validate in CMAT/CST that the customer has valid product and service in the admin center and meet entitlement requirement.

There are no exceptions to this guidance, regardless of the contact’s title, stature, employer, or contract.

It is your responsibility to verify the contact as an authenticated administrator on the account, subscription, or tenant by using the information provided through approved support tools (i.e., CMAT, Rave, CST, Explore.ms, VLSC, etc.).

DO NOT PROVIDE ANY BILLING, PAYMENT, OR OTHER SENSITIVE INFORMATION TO AN UNENTITLED CONTACT.

Only a general inquiry such as a portal walk through or how do I questions, links to public content etc. do not require entitlement. Any account specific information requires entitlement. For more details, see b.2. PII scenarios guidance (by clicking “down arrow” of b. Personally identifiable information (PII) verification﻿ to check expandable section).

3. Procedure

a. ﻿Baseline verification﻿

1. Prequalifying an existing case in the Commerce support area (Online Submission or cases created and dispatched by CSS Tech Router/TR) 

Review the case and validate below info in the ticketing system and CMAT/CST before contacting a customer.

Required entitlement information Example
Caller's first and last name Jeff Price
Initial Domain Contoso.onmicrosoft.com
Email address jeff@contoso.com or jeff@contoso.onmicrosoft.com
Telephone number or mobile phone number (if listed) Country Code + phone number
Admin role (details can be found in step a.3 validate role) Billing Admin, Global Admin, BSP administrator (follow support for Global (Company) administrator), BSP Purchaser (follow support for Billing administrator), Self-service purchaser in CST
Alternate email address (This is optional if ALL above validation passed. However, this is REQUIRED if one of the above validations failed.) jeff@fabrikam.com


2. Phone cases from a CSS Tech Router (TR)

  1. CSS TRs will create and dispatch cases directly to Commerce support if entitlement can be verified. However, for calls that cannot be entitled (such as self-service purchase scenario or any other non-admin scenarios), TR will transfer the call to the Commerce phone line and announce that they are from CSS by using the “whisper” feature of the telephone system and by displaying on the telephone the routing source of the caller.
  2. When you receive a call, the following script is suggested to greet the customer:

    “Thanks for calling. My name is <insert name>. May I have your first and last name? Thanks. May I have the case number? Thanks."

    •  If the customer is able to provide a case number, open the case and take ownership.
    •  If the customer has a case number but does not remember it, search for the customer in the ticketing system to find the appropriate case.
     

    Note: Searching for an individual user (e.g. self-service purchaser) in RAVE will only pull up the tenant associated to the domain in the user’s email address. In order find a case for a particular user, the advocate will need to click through each and every case in the tenant’s case history.

    For all incoming phone calls, you need to follow PII verification process to entitle the customer.

    •  If the customer does not have a case, and is a Company or Billing Admin on the tenant, follow the standard case-creation process. Otherwise, follow b. 2. scenario 2 for how to locate, verify, entitle non-admin caller in CST and to create non-admin cases in RAVE.   
    •  For calls received as warm transfers from other advocates or cold transfers without a case number, you need to follow the PII entitlement and verification process. This applies whether other advocates have entitled the customer or not, because other advocates entitlement procedure may differ.

  3. If the customer was routed to Commerce Support incorrectly, redirect the customer to the appropriate team by using 2500396 Informational: Office 365 redirect resource sheet.
  4. Follow standard call-handling processes to assist the customer.

b. Personally identifiable information (PII) verification﻿

If a phone case comes through as an anonymous (un-entitled) case, proceed to PII verification process to complete entitlement by communicating with the customer via the phone only. If a customer is not reachable via the phone, leave a message to ask the customer to call support at their convenience. Web cases opened from the Microsoft 365 admin center by listed admins are considered entitled (see role verification for details in section a. 3.), no further PII verification is needed.

Note:

1.There are Generic Admin accounts that can be identified by username only and may be used by multiple people. An account/caller is a generic admin account if the display name shows “Admin” or "Administrator”, matches the email address 'admin@' or 'administrator@'. All other admin accounts are considered specific to a given user. If a generic admin account has a different username after clicking the email address, and if the customer can access to the account, advocates should walk the customer through on how to update the information in the portal.

  • An ambassador may consider a generic admin regardless of the name, e.g: it@company.onmicrosoft.com, company@company.onmicrosoft.com etc., as long as he/she can provide the user id and confirm that they have access on the account. In this way we are sure that the customer calling is part of the company.
  • If in case the caller is not able to provide this, we will proceed in calling the listed admin or the number listed on this user id to make sure that we will provide information to an authorized person.

2.Customers can sign up commercial subscriptions with consumer email account such as Microsoft account or other types of consumer account. If customers are unable to provide tenant domain or tenant ID, search consumer email account in CMAT. Proceed with standard entitlement process when finding commercial subscriptions in CMAT.

Before the verification, provide the customer with a Preparatory Speech. Suggested script:
“To help you, I’ll need to perform a security check. This security check will be based around the information you have in your Microsoft 365 account. I’ll need exact answers matching the information in your Microsoft 365 admin center. If you can log in to your Admin account, it’ll be easier for you to answer these questions. Are you able to log in at this time?”

If the customer cannot find the Office 365 admin portal, navigate the customer there. See section d. PII help using MAC and Partner Center. Then use the following five questions to verify the identity of the contact.

Remember: Do not provide any guidance (hints or otherwise) to the customer during the verification process.

1. Customer Details:

Go to the CMAT Account page of the customer to see the customer details.

Ask the following five PII verification questions and verify the customer’s responses against the information displayed in the customer details. If the customer provides an incomplete but accurate answer, you can ask the customer to be more specific.  

Note: Do not miss to ask the FIRST 3 questions before jumping to ask the 4th and the 5th questions.

CMAT PII information Sample Questions
Company Name "What is the Organization Name of your company as it displays on your Microsoft Online Services account?"
Company Address "What is the full company address?”
Technical contact email “What is the Technical Contact email address listed on your account?”
Telephone number or mobile phone number (if listed) “Can you provide me with the phone number on your account?”
Company (Global) administrators (details can be found in step a.3 validate role) This question is to be used only if the caller failed all above questions.
Go to the EMPLOYEES section of the Account page.
“Please tell me the name of any of the administrators on your account.”


If you are not able to entitle the customer in CMAT, try to find the customer in CST.

  1. Open CST (https://cst.portal.azure.com/ServiceDesk/#/customer/commercial).
  2. Enter the customers ORG id into the search bar, press enter.

The customer is global admin but does not know the tenant name. Can advocate provide tenant name to global admin? For example, the customer gives us information like invoice ID, Order ID, etc.

Scenario 3: How to handle Azure Billing and Subscription support request and questions from Azure Pay As You Go Customers

If the caller is calling about their billing and subscription related to Azure, follow below instructions:

  • If a Rave case has already been created by customer asking about Azure subscription,
    • Update Resolution to “Transferred” on the Summary tab
    • Fill out below template in “Resolution steps” on the Summary tab before choosing the direct transfer path to transfer the case to Azure Billing and Subscription (Service Desk). See Rave to Service Desk Case in article 4456157 Procedure: Misroute for additional details.

Resolution steps

#Transferred# Azure Billing

Customer issue: <insert customer issue>
  • If Commerce support needs to create a new case for Azure support, fill out Customer Problem (problem description) section with customer appropriate language and above template in the Resolution steps, before transfer the case to Azure Billing and Subscription support area.
    • Information entered in this section is customer viewable, avoid internal languages and acronyms.
    • Provide detailed description of the issue, and any additional information customer provided such as contact info, subscription details.
    • Indicating Premier if this is a premier customer and case severity. Sample description: Sev B, Premier Customer with Azure pay as you go billing question, support needed from Azure support.
  • If above two options failed (entitlement failed), direct caller to create an Azure case online from https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/supportforbusiness/productselection, advocate may walk the caller through the online case creation process, but should NOT create a case on behalf of the customer.

For the non-admin caller, we will follow the same process stated in Scenario 2: How to handle non-admin caller for NOAM advocates.

c. EMEA Caller ID and PUID verification


For inbound calls, we are piloting a simplified PII process. Please find the steps below.

  1. Please check if you can see the customers' phone number that they are calling from (Caller ID) on the Avaya display. If yes, please record the number in ticketing system and go to Step i. If you do not see the number, please go to Section 2.

    i. Ask the customer for his MosID.

    ii. To identify the customer, please check the phone number against the MosID (email address) the customer has provided in CMAT.

    The phone number has to match a phone number under the user in CMAT or the phone number on the Account Page of CMAT for the tenant.

    iii. If the number matches, you are done verifying PII. The customer still has to be eligible (i.e. has to be a Global or Billing Admin).

    iv.If the number is not a match, please go to Section 2.
  2. You can either use the legacy PII process from the section of Personally Identifiable Information (PII) verification, or if the customer is logged in or can easily log into the portal, you can use the single step PUID method below.

    i. Ask the customer to go to this URL Aka.ms/PUID.

    ii. Or ask the customer to go to the portal.

    [Asset 4091944]

    iii. Ask the customer to provide the last 6 digits of the User ID and Match that against the PUID you see in CMAT under the users account.

    [Asset 4091945]

    iv. If it matches, you are done verifying PII. The customer still has to be eligible (i.e. has to be a Global or Billing Admin).

    v. If it does not match, double check all the info (tenant name, user name, last 6 digit), if no match, the verification has failed.
     

Note: The quick link Aka.ms/PUID behaves differently depending if the customer is logged in or not.

If the customer is logged in, the link takes the customer directly to here.

[Asset 4034795]



If the customer is not logged in, the customer will go here and has to select the “Subscriptions” tab.

[Asset 4034796]

 d.  ﻿PII help using MAC and Partner Center﻿

 
There are 2 user interfaces available for the office admin center.
Partners using POBO would normally use the Help - Company profile, Microsoft Admin Center.

If the caller cannot find the Office 365 admin portal, navigate the partner to the Microsoft Admin Center (MAC) and click the Admin tile.
Admin preview: If you are on the home page, click on your Company name in the top right or via the menu click Settings and select the Organization profile.
4013165 

For the old portal: Click Company profile.
4013166 

Partner Center (CSP Partners)

Partners using POBO may be have chosen to use Partner center and therefore ask you how to open the customer from Partner center. In 2017, advisor partners will start to use Partner center as they become indirect resellers and later in the year partners will start to use Partner center the way they use the MPN portal today.

Select Customers (menu on the left), expand the subscription, and select Office 365 to open the Admin center as the customer.
