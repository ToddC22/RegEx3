1. Overview

The Microsoft Azure Bulk Credit / Azure Sponsorship offer is for a limited sponsored Microsoft Azure accounts. The offer is available via e-mail invitation only for limited partners selected by Microsoft. If you are entitled to the Microsoft Azure Sponsorship offer, you would have received an e-mail invitation to accept this offer for your Account ID.

Special Pricing The pricing for your Microsoft Azure usage under this offer will be 100% off standard published Pay-As-You-Go rates for the term specified below.
Term & Conversion to Paid Account

Unless prohibited by applicable law or otherwise specified in writing, the special pricing will terminate and your subscription(s) under the Microsoft Azure Sponsorship offer will be converted automatically to the Pay-As-You-Go offer upon the earlier occurrence of:
(1) When your total cumulative usage reaches your Usage Cap (as specified in your invitation e-mail) at standard Pay-As-You-Go rates prior to application of any discount.
(2) When you reach your End Date (as specified in your invitation e-mail).

Your special pricing will also terminate if your Account ID (as specified in your invitation e-mail) is associated with an applicable Enterprise Agreement enrollment, in which case you will be subject to the pricing and terms of your Enterprise Agreement. You will be responsible for all usage charges after the special pricing expires.
Exclusions

Special pricing does not apply to Microsoft Azure support plans, third-party branded products, products sold through Microsoft Azure Marketplace, or products otherwise sold separately from Microsoft Azure (for example, Active Directory Premium). You will be responsible for all charges incurred for these services and products.

The Sponsorship offer can’t be combined with other licensing, prepaid, or credit offers (i.e. Microsoft Enterprise Agreement, Cloud Solution Partner, Azure in Open, Azure Pass, Action Pack, etc.).
Payment Options

Credit or debit cards only, unless previously approved for invoice.
Note: we do not accept prepaid and virtual credit cards. There may be a delay before discounts are reflected on your bill. You are solely responsible for any credit card interest or other credit card charges that may result.

More details on pricing and billing can be found on our Pricing page.
Cancellation Policy You may cancel this subscription at any time. Payment is required for any outstanding fees incurred.
Microsoft Online Service Agreement Your subscription remains subject to the Microsoft Online Service Agreement to which you agreed (current version available here).
Offer Availability This offer is available only to limited partner invited by Microsoft to receive a limited sponsored Microsoft Azure account. Find here the complete list of countries/regions and the currency that will be used for billing.
Limits, Quotas, and Constraints Please refer to this document for a list of common Microsoft Azure limits, quotas and constraints.
Service Level Agreements More details on each of our SLAs can be found on our SLA page. Service level agreements are not provided for services during preview.
Resource Commitment

All increases in usage levels of service resources (e.g. adding to the number of compute instances running or increasing the amount of storage in use) are subject to the availability of these service resources. Any quota described in the Limits, Quotas and Constraints section is not a commitment that there will be enough resources available for you to reach the quota.

For any subscriptions of this offer that are not incurring charges and are inactive for more than 90 days, we reserve the right to remove any compute resources including hosted service(s), virtual machines and websites. Please note that no data would be deleted, and partners can redeploy at any time. We will also provide notice prior to removing any compute resources.

You can also avoid having your computer resources removed by doing either of the following every 90 days:

1. Log into either the Azure portal or the new Microsoft Azure portal.

2. Access your resources via our Azure APIs.

2. Azure Experts MSP Partner (Center) Dashboard Flow

On the main page of partner.microsoft.com, anyone interested in finding more about the Azure Expert MSP has the ability to learn more about the program and its advantages by clicking on the Learn more >  link in the dedicated tile of the program:

[Asset 4488311]

 

Azure Expert MSP program is now available and offered for top Partners in Azure, in Partner Center.

On the Partner (Center) Dashboard, under the left navigation pane of Membership > Compare Programs > Expert MSP programs tab is available for Partners. To access the Expert MSP programs tab, the partner user must have MPN Partner Admin or Global Admin role in Partner Center.

The steps for Partners to apply for Azure Expert MSP program:

1. Partners should meet all pre-qualification requirements.

The pre-qualifications are:

A. Partner must demonstrate US $200,000 Azure consumption, monthly run-rate for the most recent 3 months. The partner can achieve this by becoming their customer’s Partner of Record, selling as a direct provider, through indirect resellers or by using the Partner Admin Link (PAL), selling solutions through Microsoft marketplaces, or by your usage-driven SaaS solution(s). Of this amount, US $100,000 should be achieved through selling as a direct provider, through indirect resellers or PAL. The offer types Trial, Benefits programs, Support, and Internal sales are excluded from your demonstrated consumption amount.

You can view the last 3 months Azure consumption details in Prose:

[Asset 4522842]

Note: If the partner reports discrepancies with their Azure consumption for AEMSP, please document the case with the same details that you’re using to troubleshoot Cloud Platform Azure consumption issues (see article 4505016) and escalate to Global Support as described in article 2865698.

B. Partner must have 15 full-time employees passing the exams specified in Partner Center.

C. Partner should have either ASfP or PSfP support programs.

Note: If the partner reports that they have an active contract which is not reflecting in the AEMSP requirements, please raise a New Parenting request using the MSSales ID (TPID) as described in article 4520665. If this process fails or the mapping request is rejected, please document the case with the contract number and the TPID (MSSales ID) and escalate to Global Support as described in article 2865698.

D. Partner must be Gold in Cloud Platform competency.

E. Partner must have at least one application published on App Source or Azure Marketplace with the below characteristics:

  • Published under Consulting Services
  • Azure focused solution
  • Managed service offer
    • Note: For partners where the AppSource or Marketplace is not available because of country limitations, ask the partners to work with their Microsoft contact (PDM) to publish a solution in the OCP Catalogue following these steps. If the requirement is still showing not met by the partner, after publishing the OCP Catalogue solution, please escalate a case to MPNGS and make sure to provide the OCP Catalogue solution proofs (link and screenshots).

F. Partner’s organization must be enrolled in either Direct CSP or Indirect CSP programs.

[Asset 4505569]

2. When pre-qualification requirements are met, Partner will be eligible for the second step of the application to the Azure Experts MSP program which is Apply. This step consists of the Partner filling out the Application form.

[Asset 4488317]

3. In the Application Form area, Partner would have to:

- fill in the URL for Azure MSP offerings

- fill in the Partner contact name

- fill in the Partner contact e-mail address

- submit one case study

- submit three-win wires

Note: Case study template and win wires template are available for Partners to view and follow. Field validation is also enabled in the form.

[Asset 4488319]

After everything is correctly completed by the Partner, he can click on Submit application.

[Asset 4488320]

4. Partner will also be prompted to confirm the submission of the application form. Once the application is submitted, a notification will go out to the Azure Expert MSP team that will start the reviewing process of the application.

[Asset 4488321]

In the same time, the status will change under step 2, in Partner (Center) Dashboard to let the Partner know that his application to join the Azure Expert MSP program is being reviewed.

[Asset 4488324]

5. Once the Azure Expert MSP team admins approve the application, the application step will be marked as completed in the Partner’s account and will reflect accordingly in his Dashboard, under the Programs tab, Azure Expert MSP option.

[Asset 4488325]

6. The next step for the Partner is to Get Audited. This is run by a third-party audit, outside of Partner Center. A notification will be sent to the Partner and the auditor. The auditor will contact the Partner for the audit.

[Asset 4488326]

7. Once the audit is completed (outside of Partner Center), the Enrollment status of the Partner becomes active on Partner Center as part of the Azure Expert MSP program and will reflect as such in his account.

[Asset 4488327]

[Asset 4488328]

Rejection on the application or audit:

If there’s rejection on the either the application or the audit steps, the Partner will have to wait for a minimum of 6 months before re-submitting the application form if they meet the submission criteria. The later date at which the Partner can re-apply is displayed in the Azure Experts MSP tab in Partner Center.

(a) Rejection on the application:

Partner is offered with a direct link to contact the Azure Expert MSP team if they have any queries regarding the rejection of their application.

[Asset 4488329]

[Asset 4488330]

(b) Rejection on the audit:

Partner is offered with a direct link to contact the Auditor team if they have any queries regarding the rejection of their application.

[Asset 4488331]

Azure Expert MSP re-enrollment

For re-enrollment, when the partner’s Azure Expert MSP program status is in AD-45 to AD+30, the application process will show as not started.

[Asset 4505573]

For re-enrollment, once the pre-qualification requirements are met, the partner can submit the application, and this will reflect in the process flow.

5. FAQ

Q1: The review of the pre-qualifications will be done by Azure Expert MSP Team, and they will change the status once they complete the review for the Partner’s Application. Is that correct?

A1: Yes.

Q2: Are there any benefits associated with this program?

A2: Yes. Now the benefits of this program are handled manually by Azure Expert MSP team, like get incentives and get discount for support team. Partners will receive a notification after they are approved for enrollment and pass all the steps and will also have a direct link to see the benefits associated to their company from their Azure Expert MSP tab. The benefits are not reflecting for now in the benefits area of Partner (Center) Dashboard, but will go automatic in Partner Center in the future.

Q3: Where can the partners get more information of this program if they have questions?

A3: There will be a portal site available for partners to know all the details - https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/business-opportunities/managed-services-provider.

Q4: Do partners need to re-apply when the program is due?

A4: Yes. Since this is a one-year program, partners need to re-apply 30 days before the anniversary date.

Q5: How can I check if a partner is qualified for Azure Expert MSP through PAL?

A5: You must go to OCP Insights tool, under Cloud Product Performance, choose Microsoft Azure and then select from the Partner Attach Type dropdown menu, Partner Admin Link.

[Asset 4516786]

In case the association was done through Partner Admin Link, in the blue box you will be able to see the number of subscriptions. More information will be displayed on the second half of the page under Azure Subscription Details if you select Partner and then check the information displayed under Partner Attach Type.

Note: If you cannot access this data in OCP Insights, please document the case with the same details that you’re using to troubleshoot Cloud Platform Azure consumption issues (see article 4505016) and escalate to Global Support as described in article 2865698.

Q6: How can I find out if I’m available for the Azure Bulk Credit benefit via Gold Cloud Platform competency?

A6: ALL Gold Cloud Platform competency partners are eligible for the Azure Bulk Credit offer.

Q7: How can I reach out for support about the activation of the Azure Bulk Credit?

A7: There are two types of support for activating Azure Bulk Credit:

1. Use the information provided in the e-mail and contact Azure Subscription Management Support by going into your Azure Portal and create a 'New support request'.

  • Issue Type: 'Subscription management'
  • Select the Subscription you want to have converted
  • Let the Support Plan auto-populate and click 'Next'
  • Severity: 'C - Minimal Impact'
  • Problem Type: 'Switch to another offer'
  • Provide any additional details and your contact information to submit

2. Begin Chat/Message through https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/support/options/.
