Procedure: M365 Commerce: Applying hashtags in Rave cases

[Asset 4462485]

Who should use this article?

M365 Commerce advocates

What is this article about?

This article provides guidance on integrating Hashtags into the Rave case based on the dedicated Commerce scenarios, according to the issue investigated on the case.

The purpose of using hashtags is to:

  • Streamline and standardize how delivery partners are capturing scenarios.
  • Consistently help Supportability to track and improve the accuracy of scenarios, and drive conversations with Engineering.
  • Use the insights in support for tracking KPI drivers, Readiness opportunities and Backlog management.

Bill and Payments

#BILLINGFREQ

Change billing frequency/commitment

Customer wants to change their billing frequency from Annual to Monthly, or vice-versa.

#SLA

Credit Request for SLA outages

Customer is requesting a credit cause on an issue with the service.

#CCDECLINE

Credit Card Decline

A customer with a direct subscription, receives an email notification that their renewal payment was declined.

#PMCHANGE

Change Payment Method

Customer wants to change the payment method from Invoice Pay to Credit/Debit Card ...or vice versa.

#PIADD

Add payment instrument

Customer needs help adding a new payment instrument.

#PICHANGE

Change Payment Instrument

Customer wants to change their Payment Instrument (CC) associated to their subscription.

#INVOICEREFUND

Invoice Pay customer insists on refund

Customer wants to either get a refund or inquire about an outstanding refund owed.

#PROOFPAY

Payment sent but not received; proof of payment

Customer would like to know if they have an outstanding balance.

#BILLINGINFO

Help understanding the bill

Customer needs help understanding their invoice.

#ONLINESTATEMENT

How to locate online statement

Customer is unable to find where they can view and download their statement in the portal.

#TAXEXEMPTION

TAX Exemption (Non-VAT)

Customer asks about the tax exemption in their region. This is for Non-VAT B2B 0% tax offered by MS.

#VAT

VAT Related issues

Customer asks for help adding or updating their VATID in the portal.

#DAF

Hardcopy invoice, DAF

Customer is requesting a hardcopy invoice or delivery acceptance form from Microsoft.

#SOA

Statement of Account (SOA) ; Account Reconciliation Request

A customer with an Invoice Pay order requests a Statement of Account (SOA), account reconciliation, or their order balance.

#BILLINGDISPUTE

Billing Dispute due to incorrect balance, price or tax, DNC, PTP

Customer disputing his invoice due to incorrect charges. May result as well in Do Not Contact (DNC) or Promise to Pay (PTP) requests.

#WHT

Withholding tax, TDS

Customer requests Withholding Tax.

#BILLINGCYCLE

Billing Cycle - Invoice

Customer requests to understand when invoice generates.

 

Subscriptions and Licenses Activation

#VLACTIVATION

VL Activation

Customer needs help with volume license activation.

#PREPAIDMOVE

Incorrectly activated Pre-paid token on the wrong tenant

Customer or partner has activated a prepaid token on the wrong account.

#PREPAIDREDEEM

Guidance need to redeem product key

Customer has a product key and need help to redeem it.

#LICENSEDISCREPANCY

License reduction or disappearing from the portal

Customer has purchase licenses and they are not viewable in the M365 portal or assignable to users.

#VLLINK

Resend VL activation link

The customer needs help to locate or resend the tenant discovery email, for volume licenses activation.

 

 

Subscriptions and Licenses Management

#NOTIFICATIONS

Notifications e-mails issues

Issue and inquiries about Email notifications.

#SUBCANCEL

Subscription cancellation

Customer wants or has issues to cancel a subscription.

#REMAP

VL Tenant Remap

Request to move Volume Licensing subscriptions from current tenant to another tenant.

#TRIALEXTENSION

End Date Extension Request for trial subscriptions

Customer asks to extend their trial.

#PAIDEXTENSION

End Date Extension Request for paid subscriptions

Extension requests for Volume Licensing, Prepaid, any subscription that can be extended and is non-trial.

#SUBREACTIVE

Reactivate paid subscription

Customer contacts support to get a subscription reactivated.

#PREPAIDRENEW

Renewing a pre-paid token

Customer wants or has issues to renew their prepaid subscription.

#PREPAIDREPLACEMENT

Pre-paid token: changed seat count instead extending subscription

Customer added seats instead of extending their subscription.

 

 

Subscriptions and Licenses Purchase

#PORTALERROR

Portal errors during purchase

Customer experiences a M365 portal error during the purchase flow.

#PURCHASEISSUE

Issues while creating new purchases

Existing paid customer requests help navigating the portal to make a purchase

#CREDITCHECK

Credit Check status request

Customer is experiencing credit check when purchase licenses with invoice.

#NOOFFER

Offer not available

Customer can't purchase commercial offers.

#EDUTAG

Add/Remove EDU tag

Customer is trying to purchase an EDU offer and/or requesting to be tagged as EDU (includes EDU verification requests).

#CHARITY

Purchase Non-profit offers

Customer can't purchase or doesn't have access to nonprofit offer from M365 admin portal.
