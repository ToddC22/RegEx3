Key Notes

When a Rave case has been stamped ‘Premier Customer’, this customer case will be routed to the Premier Support queue automatically. [Asset 4560542]

If for some reason, a Concierge or Enterprise engineer (non-premier engineer) receives a Premier stamped case like this, their team manager can transfer the case to the Premier General queue directly without the need of going through the process described in this article. See an example:

[Asset 4560544]

Procedure

The procedure in this article describes three possible scenarios where cases are not stamped as Premier (meaning we cannot see the Premier customer tag in Rave) and cannot be directly transferred to Premier General:

  1. Customer states they have a Premier contract at first contact with the engineer.
  2. IM flags the customer as Premier after engineer receives the case.
  3. After engineer works on the case for some time, either the customer or the Account Team (IM, AM, and so on) states the customer is Premier.

Follow below procedure to transfer the case to Premier support (​All transfers are done via email):

  • Enterprise/Concierge SE should meet the Initial Response (IR) and proceed to contact with the customer as usual.
  • In the interim, if the case owner is notified that the customer is Premier, depending on where the claim is coming from, support engineer must inform TM about the situation:
    • If SE is informed by the customer that the account is Premier, but in RAVE, there is no reference to the Premier contract (no info on who the CSAM is). Prior to inform TM about the situation, SE should confirm with customer on who’s their allocated CSAM (TAM).
    • If SE is informed directly by the CSAM about the Premier contract in place, then SE should move forward with next step by informing the TM.
Enterprise/Concierge TM should reach out to aligned POD TA for verification/confirmation.
  • Aligned POD TA should contact CSAM in charge of customer’s account and request customer’s tenant to be matched as Premier (transfer can be initiated in the meantime). If CSAM states this task was already completed, POD TA must confirm Premier contract type with CSAM. If it is one of below contracts, case belongs to Premier Escalation support. Transfer the case to Premier Escalation support by engaging Duty Managers.

  • After the transfer request has been sent, the Premier Queue trackers/DMs within CKPIRDM@microsoft.com will act upon each case depending on the Region. The Premier Queue tracker/DMs will:
    • Acknowledge the Transfer request.
    • Identify an available Premier resource to handle the pending case.
    • Skill the Premier engineer with the correspondent Concierge/Enterprise support area, so the case (that will still appear as Concierge/Enterprise in RAVE) can be properly assigned.
    • After case has been assigned, the Premier Queue trackers/DMs will proceed to immediately remove the Concierge/Enterprise skill given to the Premier engineer, so its RAVE profile remains the same.

Exceptions:

  • Enterprise/Concierge SE should reach out to its POD aligned TA if the case is not reassigned within the first business day. POD TA can directly engage with the General Premier TA - M365 Global Premier TAs to follow up the progress.
  • All properly tagged Premier cases and IM/Customer requested Premier case transfers must be initiated within 48 hours of creation.
  • After Enterprise/Concierge SE worked on a case for more than 2 days and Customer/IM requests the case to be assigned to a General Premier SE, aligned POD TA should:
    • Follow the verification path above to confirm if the Customer is, indeed, Premier.
    • If Customer is now matched as Premier in Rave (within the Support Program), POD TA/TM should create a new case with the up-to-date summary (note the New Case number before proceeding) and redirect the case to DAO/Queue. Aligned POD TA or TM should close the original Enterprise/Concierge Case as duplicate.
    • If Customer is still showing as Concierge/Enterprise in RAVE, POD TA/TM can use the Premier Toggle switch option to directly create a new case in Premier Support (with the up-to-date summary) which will directly be rerouted to Premier DAO/Queue. Aligned POD TA or TM should close the original Enterprise/Concierge Case as duplicate. [Asset 4565779]
  •  In situations where the Customer signed a Premier contact after the original case was created, the aligned POD TA ((after confirming customer’s Premier contract with CSAM) should create a new case with the up-to-date summary (note the New Case number before proceeding) and redirect the case to DAO/Queue (since the Customer will now be matched as Premier, case will automatically go to the Premier queue). Aligned POD TA or TM should close the original Enterprise/Concierge Case as duplicate.
  • If customer/CSAM/IM informs that they are entitled for Premier, but Rave does not reflect the same, the  POD aligned TA can reach out to General Premier TA - M365 Global Premier TAs.
  • If, at a certain point after the transfer, a wrongly stamped tenant becomes a CritSit, FLE/TM should immediately notify the POD aligned TA to request an urgent re-stamping of the ticket through CSS Amplify TAs CSS-Amplify-TA@microsoft.com. After the case has been stamped as Premier, the severity should be set accordingly. 
  • In situations where the case ends up being an Advisory/RCA/OED or any other scenario, that, as per established procedure, will require direct transfer to Premier Escalation. After POD aligned TA has confirmed the validity of Customer’s Premier contract, TA should request the re-stamping of the case through CSS Amplify TAs (CSS-Amplify-TA@microsoft.com). After the case has been stamped as Premier, it should be routed to the Premier Escalation queue.
