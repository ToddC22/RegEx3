TUG: MST: Starting MST

[Asset 4049349]

Who should use this article?

  • All Consumer

What is this article about?

This article is part of the MST TUG and details the steps for starting MST.

 
Step Screenshot
1. Click on the ASD icon on your computer.
[Asset 4051900]
 
2. Enter your credentials, and then click OK. [Asset 4051901]
 
3. Enter your Partner credentials, and then click OK. [Asset 4051902]

 
4. Usually, the customer contacts you by phone or chat.
When a new case is started, you begin the process of authenticating your customer.		 [Asset 4051903]

 
5. Make sure you are viewing the Customer pane. [Asset 4051904]

 
6. Search for the customer account. You can search for your customer by:

- Email
- SR#
- GamerTag
- Phone number
- Serial Number
- CP Billing Account
- Guest order ID and email

Note: You may have to choose from multiple customer records.
[Asset 4051905]

 
7. Authenticate the customer by sending them a verification code.
Once the customer has given you the code they received, select Verify and this will automatically launch MST. 
Depending on how the customer came to you, they may already be authenticated.
[Asset 4051906]

 
8. The MST homepage auto-launches with the customer’s information specific to their Microsoft account.

Note: When you first open MST a number of the sections are open for you to review.
[Asset 4051907]
