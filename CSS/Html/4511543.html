Procedure: Surface: Expanded/Swollen battery or bulging screen: Creating an expanded battery exchange order

[Asset 4049349]

Who should use this article?

  • Surface
  • Surface Repair Specialists (or site equivalent)

What is this article about?

  • This article describes how to identify and how to handle Surface devices with a possible expanded battery. This article should be followed when the customer mentions that their Surface has a possible expanded, swollen or exploded battery (or also described as raised, lifted, separated, or bulging screen).

Note:

If the customer, at any time during this process, states that the Surface has an expanded battery AND reports another RRT (product safety) issue as well (smoke, fire, melting, sparks, injury, etc.), then you must follow the RRT process located here instead:

  • Consumer: KB 2764515 Procedure: Surface & Xbox: Handling Product Safety (RRT) Contacts
  • Commercial: KB 2971041 Procedure: Surface: Product Safety (RRT) Issues (Surface, Hub, HoloLens)

Note: Do not start here. Be sure to first read KB 4508455 Procedure: Surface: Expanded/Swollen battery or bulging screen: Overview.

  • Expanded battery orders are to be used only for expanded battery situations where the customer has provided videos of the device showing the issue and the serial number and should never be used for any other issue. If the issue has not been confirmed through videos, an expanded battery order should not be used.
  • Expanded batteries will be covered at no cost for three years from the original purchase date. After three years there will be a charge for the exchange if the device still has out of warranty (OOW) support. For more information on this, see the FAQ section in KB 4508455 Procedure: Surface: Expanded/Swollen battery or bulging screen overview.
  • There is no grace period for the expanded battery policy. If the customer is one day over the three years, the device is OOW and no longer qualifies for the expanded battery policy.
  • Do not use the “Escalation (Swollen Battery)” offer at any time. This is for escalations to T3 Redmond only as it requires special handling due to the unique nature of the order.

Note: Follow only the exact steps for the customer’s regional device location. If the steps are not followed exactly this will cause issues with the order and delays for the customer as the steps for exchange of an expanded battery do differ based on the customers location.

Table of contents

1. Continental US (lower 48 US States)

2. Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, and all other supported US territories

3. Canada, Japan, Australia

4. China

5. All other supported countries (where a device has been launched)

6. Unsupported countries (where a device has not been launched or is not supported)

 

1. Continental US (lower 48 US States)

  1. Launch your Service Order Management (SOM) tool and start the order process.
  2. Select Lifted, separated, or bulging screen as the problem type.
  3. Select either:
    • In Warranty/Out of Warranty - Standard Exchange (Swollen Battery) (available for consumer and commercial)
    • In Warranty - Advanced Exchange (Swollen Battery) (available for qualifying commercial customers only)
      • This option is not available for consumer customers in this region and will only show for commercial customers that have a qualifying device and/or warranty. If this option does not appear then it is not something that can be provided to the customer.

Notes:

  • If the customer declines the OOW offer let the customer know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process. If they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. Close the case.
  • If the customer disputes the warranty and indicates they should qualify for a free replacement, follow standard warranty dispute process and return here once completed.
  1. Select Get a Box & Label By Mail as the shipping method.
    • Do not select any other options for customers located in the continental US (lower 48 states). If any other shipping method is selected the original device will not be collected and resolution for the customer will be delayed.
  2. Complete the order.
  3. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to backup the original Surface and how to ship the original Surface in.
    They will be provided with a special return box that must be used for shipping the damaged Surface and that return box will have a prepaid UPS shipping label attached to the box. Once the customer receives the box they will need to follow the instructions that come with it to package the Surface inside and then take it to a UPS shipping location.
    • For commercial expanded battery AE orders, the replacement device and the return box will ship separate from each other. The return box may arrive 1-2 business days AFTER the replacement device is received by the customer.
    • For all expanded battery SE orders, once the original Surface is received at the service center, a replacement will be shipped back to the customer.

2. Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, and all other supported US territories

Note: For customers located in the Virgin Islands we can only create orders for customers that have a US Military address. If they do not have an US Military address, then we are unable to provide an exchange option.

  1. Launch your SOM tool and start the order process.
  2. Select Lifted, separated, or bulging screen as the Problem Type.
  3. Select either:
    • In Warranty/Out of Warranty - Standard Exchange (Swollen Battery)
    • In Warranty - Advanced Exchange (Swollen Battery) Note: This option is ONLY available for qualifying commercial customers located in Alaska, Hawaii, or Puerto Rico or other supported US territories.

Notes:

  • If the customer declines the OOW offer let them know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process. If they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. Close the case.
  • If the customer disputes the warranty and indicates they should qualify for a free replacement, follow standard warranty dispute process and return here once completed.
  1. Select Get a Box Mail & Schedule Courier Pickup as the shipping method.
    • Do not select any other shipping options for customers located in Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, or other supported US territories. If any other shipping method is selected the original device will be unable to ship as it is restricted by the courier.
  2. Complete the order.
  3. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to back up the original Surface and how to ship the original Surface in.

The customer will be provided with a special return box that must be used for shipping the damaged Surface, there will be no return label provided. Once the customer receives the box they will need to follow the instructions that come with it to package the Surface inside and then need to follow the instructions located in the order email to contact the courier, Blu Logistics, to arrange collection. Refer to the order email for those instructions as needed.

  • For expanded battery AE orders: the replacement device and the return box will ship separate from each other. The return box may arrive one to two business days AFTER the replacement device is received by the customer.
  • For all expanded battery SE orders: Once Blu Logistics has collected the Surface, they will deliver it to the service center and the replacement will be sent.
    • For Blu Logistics, inbound tracking may not be available. If the customer has any questions about the return shipping, please refer them back to Blu Logistics who can assist the customer with any shipping questions about the damaged Surface.
    • The customer WILL receive the standard order emails once the device is received at the service center and the replacement is sent.

3. Canada, Japan, and Australia

Support options in these countries for expanded battery will vary based on the model of Surface the customer has. It is very important to confirm the customer’s serial number and device model and follow the exact steps below to ensure the customer understands what will be required.

Depending on the model (in these countries only) we may not collect the original from the customer.

  • If the customer declines any offer listed below let them know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process. If they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. Close the case.
  • If the customer disputes the warranty on the device and indicates they should qualify for a free replacement, follow standard warranty dispute process and return here once completed.
  1. Launch your SOM tool and start the order process.
  2. Select Lifted, separated, or bulging screen as the Problem Type.

What device does the customer have:

If the customer is reporting multiple devices follow the instructions below for each model, ensuring the customer is provided correct next steps for each model. The customer may need to handle the devices differently and must be provided the correct next steps for each device.

  • Surface Pro 4, Surface Pro (5th gen) Surface Book 1, or Surface Laptop 1
  • Surface Pro 3, Surface Pro 6, Surface Book 2, Surface Laptop 2 or newer

Surface Pro 4, Surface Pro (5th gen), Surface Book 1, or Surface Laptop 1:

  1. Select In Warranty/Out of Warranty – Keep Your Own Device (Swollen Battery).
  2. Select No Action Required as the shipping method.
  3. Complete the order.
  4. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to back up the original Surface and how to dispose of the original Surface themselves. Per the instructions the customer should have the original Surface recycled per their local e-waste process. If they need help with locating a recycler, they need to contact their local municipality for assistance. The original Surface will be flagged as ineligible for future support and repair as well.

Note: We do not have contact numbers for the customer’s municipality if they need help locating a recycler. The customer will need to locate that information on their own.

  • If the customer insists that Microsoft needs to collect the original and recycle inform them that this is not an option and reiterate self-disposal information.

Surface Pro 3, Surface Pro 6, Surface Book 2, Surface Laptop 2 or newer:

  1. Select either:
    1. In Warranty/Out of Warranty - Standard Exchange (Swollen Battery)
    2. In Warranty - Advanced Exchange (Swollen Battery)
      • The AE option will only appear if the customer qualifies.
  2. Select Get a Box Mail & Schedule Courier Pickup as the shipping method.
  3. Complete the order.
  4. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to back up the original Surface and how to ship the original Surface in.

The customer will be provided with a special return box that must be used for shipping the damaged Surface. There will be no return label provided. Once the customer receives the box they will need to follow the instructions that come with it to package the Surface inside and then follow the instructions located in the order email to contact the courier, Blu Logistics, to arrange collection. Refer to the order email for those instructions as needed.

  • For expanded battery AE orders: The replacement device and the return box will ship separate from each other. The return box may arrive one to two business days AFTER the replacement device is received by the customer.
  • For all expanded battery SE orders: Once Blu Logistics has collected the Surface, they will deliver it to the service center and the replacement will be sent.
    • For Blu Logistics, inbound tracking may not be available. If the customer has any questions about the return shipping, please refer them back to Blu Logistics who can assist the customer with any shipping questions about the damaged Surface.
    • The customer WILL receive the standard order emails once the device is received at the service center and the replacement is sent.

4. China

If the customer lives in an area covered by a walk-in center:

  • Inform them that they will need to go to a walk-in center to have the issue resolved, and provide the customer with the nearest walk-in center information
  • The walk-in center will move forward with the expanded battery process and create the appropriate order to resolve the issue

If the customer does not live in an area covered by a walk-in center:

  1. Launch your SOM tool and start the order process.
  2. Select Lifted, separated, or bulging screen as the Problem Type.
  3. Select In Warranty/Out of Warranty - Same Unit Repair (Swollen Battery).
    • If the customer qualifies for a free same unit repair there will be no price (or $0) listed next to the offer.
    • If the customer does not qualify for free same unit repair, then the OOW price will be listed next to the offer.
      • If the customer declines the OOW offer let the customer know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process. If they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. Close the case.
      • If the customer disputes the warranty and indicates they should qualify for a free replacement, follow standard warranty dispute process and return here once completed.
  4. Select Get a Box Mail & Schedule Courier Pickup as the shipping method.
  5. Complete the order.
  6. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to backup the original Surface and how to ship the original Surface in. The customer will be provided with a special return box that must be used for shipping the damaged Surface, there will be no return label provided. Once the customer receives the box, they will need to follow the instructions that come with it to package the Surface inside. Then they need to follow the instructions located in the order email to contact the courier, Blu Logistics, to arrange collection. Once Blu has collected the Surface, they will deliver it to the service center and the replacement will be sent. Refer to the order email sent to the customer for those instructions as needed.
    • For Blu Logistics, in bound tracking may not be available. If the customer has any questions about the return shipping, please refer them back to Blu Logistics who can assist the customer with any shipping questions about the damaged Surface.

5. All other supported countries (where a device has been launched)

If the customer is in any other country that is not listed in one of the above processes and it is a supported country for the Surface model the customer has, then follow these steps. For these other countries we do not collect the original Surface once the expanded battery has been confirmed, instead the customer is responsible for properly disposing of the original device.

  1. Launch your SOM tool and start the order process.
  2. Select Lifted, separated, or bulging screen as the Problem Type.
  3. Select In Warranty/Out of Warranty – Keep Your Own Device (Swollen Battery).
    • If the customer qualifies for a free same unit repair there will be no price (or $0) listed next to the offer.
    • If the customer does not qualify for free replacement, then the OOW price will be listed next to the offer.
      • If the customer declines the OOW offer let the customer know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process and if they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. Close the case.
      • If the customer disputes the warranty and indicates they should qualify for a free replacement, follow standard warranty dispute process and return here once completed.
  4. Select No Action Required as the shipping method.
  5. Complete the order.
  6. Inform the customer that they will receive an order email with instructions on how to back up the original Surface and how to dispose of the original Surface themselves. Per the instructions the customer should have the original Surface recycled per their local e-waste process, if they need help with locating a recycler, they need to contact their local municipality for assistance. The original Surface will be flagged as ineligible for future support and repair as well.

Note: We do not have contact numbers for the customer’s municipality if they need help locating a recycler. The customer will need to locate that information on their own.

  • If the customer insists that Microsoft needs to collect the original and recycle inform them that this is not an option and reiterate self-disposal information.

6. Unsupported/non-launch countries

If the customer is in an unsupported country or a non-launch country, provide normal unsupported messaging to them. Let the customer know that Microsoft advises to discontinue using the original Surface and to have it properly disposed of following their local e-waste process and if they need help locating a local recycler, they can contact their local municipality who can assist. For proper messaging about disposal of the Surface refer to the FAQ section in KB 4508455 Procedure: Surface: Expanded/Swollen battery or bulging screen overview.
