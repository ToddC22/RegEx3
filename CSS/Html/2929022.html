ASMS – TA/PTA and Manager Engagement Process

Submit a ModTrack request to update this TSG.

Overview

This TSG provides guidance on how and when to escalate internally to the various PODs, Technical Advisors (TAs), Partner Technical Advisors (PTAs), and Managers.

Policy

  • In instances where it is unclear who to engage or how to engage them, work with your TA for guidance.
  • Teams who are not in the Pod model should escalate tasks to the following queues for escalation assistance:
    •  APAC - ASMS Mandarin, ASMS Japanese, or ASMS Korean queue - ASMS APJ TA Consult – Internal queue - Email alias ASMSAP3 as needed.
    • Suppliers - ASMS Billing and PI WP, ASMS Quota WP, or ASMS GQA WP queue - ASMS POD PPP queue - Email alias ASMSPPPAP for account escalations and ASMSPPPBPI for billing escalations as needed.
    • POC POD/TA queue - ASMS Internal Team Engagement. For guidance on escalating to this queue, please refer to the below TA/PTA Internal Queue Procedure section of this TSG.
  • US Gov SEs should send their TA consult tasks for USGov cases to the ASMS POD USG queue.
  • FTE SEs should send their TA consult tasks to the ASMS PPP TA Consult - Internal queue.
  • TAs should follow the Manager Recovery Process TSG (3137642) when needing to engage a manager for recovery. For further TA instructions on troubleshooting tasks, see TSG 4474330 (Azure Cost Management)
  • When sending a task or a case to your TA, update the title (case/task) to the following:
    •  Reason for engaging TA | Brief description of Issue | Date 
    • Note: For Sev A tasks to the TA/PTA, please do a warm hand-off. 

Engagement and Escalation Path

Use the general TA template and provide all the relevant data for the TA to assist, when the following need to occur:

  • Advisories
  • De-provisioning a subscription
  • LCA related tasks
  • Adding bulk co-admin
  • Attaining TA approval for JIT operations that need to be executed by ASEs (before sending the CRI to ASE)
  • Approval for re-enabling a sub that was cancelled for chargeback
  • Prioritization/expediting with partner teams.
  • Force SOT requests
  • Before submitting a Sev 1 SR to PayOps
  • Before submitting an ICM to WASU
  • TA-only write actions
    • Subscription de-provisioning/deleting requests
    • Subscription enabling/disabling in RDFE/ARM
    • Bulk add/remove co-admins (for internal subscriptions only)
    • Cloud Service VIPs Quota Increase (RDFE only)
  • Credit or Refund related issues, where TA approval is required. Leverag the TA Credit/Refund Approval template below. 

Note: If you need assistance finding the appropriate TA or PTA, leverage TSG 3189895.

Unresponsive Internal Team Engagement (i.e Payops)
Use the template below to engage your TA, when you are not receiving the traction needed on a Internal Team engagement.

  • Partner team name:
  • Partner ticket number:
  • Summary of the issue:
  • Severity of the ticket:
  • Is the ticket assigned to a resource?
  • Is the SLA missed?
    • If Yes, have you followed up on the ticket and sent an email to the ticket owner?
    • If Yes, have you attached the email thread in customer files and what is its title?
    • If No, what is the reason?
    • If No, why is this urgent?
 

Note: All bug/ticket/issues submitted to a partner team must be followed up upon on a regular basis. This will ensure the partner team is working on the issue appropriately. Ensure you have done all of the following before engaging your TA. 

  1. Followed-up on the TFS/Partner SR for updates.
  2. E-mailed the TFS/Partner SR owner for update if SLA is missed.
  3. Attached a copy of the email thread with the partner team under Customer files folder of case and send a task to POD TA for traction.

Once you have engaged your TA, they will review the request and add justification for expediting the request  The TA aligned to the specific partner will pick up the request and reach out to the partner team’s support contact.

Note: Following up over email is not applicable for the Enterprise Agreement (EA) team. Please escalate a task to your TA for all EA-related follow-ups.

TA/PTA Internal Queue Procedure

Note: All internal Team Network TA/PTAs share the responsibility of monitoring the ASMS Internal Team Engagement queue and picking up items sent for their network.

When a TA/PTA needs assistance from another Aligned TA Network to collaborate with one of our internal Teams, the following should occur.

  1. Create a task. The title of the task should include the following:
    1. Name of aligned Internal team you need assistance from.
    2. Action required.
    3. Title of the issue.
    4. Ticket number of the issue.

Note: Your task title should reflect the following nomenclature: Internal Team Name | Action Needed | Issue Title | Ticket #

Example:  EA | Traction | Credit Request| VSO # 12345678

  1. Include in the task any details of what the issue, what is stuck, what help is needed, etc.
  2. Review and check for any malformed submissions from the relevant Ticket type.
