1. Creating a connection with your customers using the Feature – Benefit - Advantage approach

Feature: Make sure that you are trained on the all the new features of the new Microsoft Edge.

Example: One of the features of the New Microsoft Edge is enhanced productivity features such as linking your browsing across all your devices.

Benefit: Familiarize yourself with the benefits of the features of Edge.

Example: With that feature, one of the benefits is that your favorites, addresses, and payment information sync in the background, so you can jump from device to device without skipping a beat.

Advantage: Finally, connect that benefit with your customer’s needs.
Example: You mentioned that you do a lot of traveling and are likely using different devices to get your work done. This means that you could browse from one device to the next, pick up where you left off, wherever you left off.

1.1 Create your own recommendation using the features and benefits below

Remember that you can combine some of these recommendations based on what’s relevant. These are ideas to help you connect the right solutions to meet your customer’s needs.

If your customer wants to learn more about Privacy and Security, actively listen for cues where your customer may be concerned about their privacy while browsing or may have had a negative experience in the past with privacy and security.

  • Safer surfing from start-up to shut down. The new Edge Chromium prevents unauthorized access of your browsing data and it comes with Microsoft Defender protecting you against phishing or malware websites, and from downloading potentially malicious files.
  • Take control of your data. Microsoft Edge and Bing give you more control over your data, and more transparency into what information is being collected, while you browse with new features like Tracking Prevention and InPrivate mode.

If your customer wants to learn more about Performance, actively listen for cues where your customer may be using their browser heavily for streaming games or shows.

  • The New Edge is optimized for Windows 10. Microsoft Edge is now available on all your devices, and automatically syncs your passwords, favorites, and settings.
  • Microsoft Edge streams in 4K. Microsoft Edge is the only browser where you can stream Netflix in 4K Ultra HD resolution.

If your customer wants to learn more about Productivity, actively listen for cues where your customer could benefit from features that help them be more organized and find what they’re looking for quickly so they can get back to doing what matters most.

  • Browse from one device to the next. Phone, tablet, this computer or that one, pick up where you left off, wherever you left off. With Microsoft Edge, your favorites, addresses, and payment information sync in the background, so you can jump from device to device without skipping a beat.
  • Organize your thoughts and workspace. Don't let too many tabs take over. With Microsoft Edge, you can easily save and set them aside. If you get an idea or want to comment on a PDF, write it down directly with a digital pen. You can even sign a form if you need to.
  • Fewer obstacles to learning. With Microsoft Edge and Bing, grammar helpers break down words into highlighted syllables. Reading view allows you to block distractions. You can also have text spoken to you.
  • Browse anywhere. The new Microsoft Edge is now available on all supported versions of Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.

If your customer wants to learn more about Personalization, actively listen for cues where your customer is pulling together information from the web from many sources. They could be a teacher or student doing research or someone planning a trip looking at different sites or comparing items across shopping sites online.

  • Collect and organize the content your browse. The new Microsoft Edge Collections feature lets you organize the content you browse on the web. You can gather different forms of text, images, and other content and organize them into collections. You can then share your collections and export them to Office apps like Word.
  • Customize with extensions. The new Microsoft Edge is now compatible with your favorite extensions, so it’s easy to personalize your browsing experience.
  • Choose your style. News, images, search—choose what you want to see when you open a new tab. Select a layout best for you: Focused, Inspirational, or Informational.

Customer pitch example:

“Have you tried the New Edge browser? Whether you're searching, streaming, or just need to get some stuff done, Microsoft Edge and Bing are the speedy and secure way to work on the web. Featuring built-in learning tools, 4K streaming, and advanced cyber protections—all optimized for Windows 10.​ Click here to try it, https://aka.ms/EdgeLearnMore.”

1.2 Is the customer interested?

Unless the customer explicitly says they are not interested, provide the following information:

  • Chat: “Click here to learn how to use Edge https://aka.ms/EdgeLearnMore.”
  • Phone: “You can open Edge by clicking on the Start menu and typing “Edge.” Then select “Microsoft Edge” from the list. In addition, I’ll send you more information about using Edge in the case email.”

If the customer is not interested, include the links in your support email in case they want to learn about the feature later.

2. Email content (required)

Add this content to your case closure email.

Following up our conversation today, here’s more information about the Microsoft Edge browser:

  • Binge Netflix in 4K. See seasons of your favorite show in ultra HD resolution. Only on Microsoft Edge.
  • Safer web sessions. Microsoft Defender SmartScreen analyzes sites and downloads, warning you of any malicious intent.
  • Keep a better tab on all your tabs. Pin, preview, save them for later and more. Microsoft Edge makes it easy to manage your open tabs.
  • Discover your way. No matter how you learn, Microsoft Edge adapts with tools like grammar helpers and text-to-speech readers.

Click here to try Microsoft Edge, https://aka.ms/EdgeLearnMore.”

3. ASD coding

Under Reason for Contact in the ASD form:

  1. Under Reason for Contact > Achieve More Conversation > Yes
  2. Achieve More Product > Windows
  3. Achieve More Campaign > select Edge
  4. Customer Interested > select Yes or No

Details on where to code an Advise Me conversation in ASD can be found in KB 3082289 Informational: Advise Me Educate Program, section Tools - Universal Achieve More campaigns coded in ASD.
