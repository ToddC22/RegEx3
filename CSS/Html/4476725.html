1. Overview

If you are unsure whether a scenario is covered under the standard manufacturer's warranty, or how it may be handled by the service center, refer to the below table.

Note: This information is provided as a reference only. Do not proactively disclose details of what is or is not covered.

Also reference KB 2867160 Procedure: Customer induced damaged and return unrepaired.

Note: Surface Hub devices are not eligible for Microsoft Complete and Accidental Damage Protection (ADP).  Any Microsoft Complete or ADP offerings found in the below scenarios are not applicable to Surface Hubs.

3. Notes and additional information

Microsoft Complete does not provide warranty extension or ADP coverage for covers.

  • Both severe physical damage and liquid damage are not covered by warranty and does not present repair options regardless of warranty status.
  • If the customer declares conditions that match severe physical damage or water immersion, an order should not be created.
  • If the customer is unsure if the damage declared is severe physical damage, create an order for the actual issue the customer is facing (for example; Won’t power on from battery), and then set the expectation that the service center may return the device back ‘as is’ to the customer if damage is considered severe physical damage.
    • Even if the device is considered within the warranty period, these damage types require the customer to pay an out of warranty exchange fee. However, paying the OOW exchange fee does not guarantee a device exchange.
  • If the customer has other failures not listed, the order should be created against the actual symptom.
  • MST will display both the IW and OOW offers. Only select IW if the damages explained fit the examples provided and were confirmed by the customer out of the box. If these damages occurred as part of regular usage, select the OOW offer.

Upon receipt of the customer's device, the exchange center will assess the damage.

  • If an in warranty order is written for one of the symptoms listed but the service center finds conditions listed that voids the warranty, the service center will return the device back to the customer "as is."
  • If the service center finds conditions listed under severe physical damage or liquid damage, the service center will return the device back to the customer "as is" regardless of the order type written.
  • The service center will replace devices with any normal cosmetic wear found at time of exchange.
  • Internal failures are device functional issues covered by the warranty.
  • Normal cosmetic wear is not repairable if this is the primary reason for repair and there are no other functional failures.
