Business profiles are a benefit for partners, which they can create on Partner Center to showcase their business to customers, as well as Microsoft inside sales teams, who are looking for their unique solutions and expertise to address the customer’s business needs.

Partners create profiles on Partner Center > Dashboard > Referrals > Business Profiles. Partner guidance is available on Create a business profile to get sales leads from Microsoft.

Once the profile is active, we'll be able to direct customers to their business, through:

  • Microsoft marketing activities
  • Internal Microsoft sales and service agents
  • Customer search, using the solution provider search tool
How to find existing business profile:  

Search for the partner by name only, use the following link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/solution-providers/search?skipCheck (NOTE: this link is to be used internally.)

NEW: The Referral Tour is now available on the right side of the page, which describes the business profile, leads, Co-sell Opportunities, Deal registration and Analytics.

This tour will automatically show once a user is logging in into the Referrals section and they can also launch it by clicking in the “Take referrals tour” anytime they want.

IMPORTANT NOTE: If partners still have issues with Partner Finder ranking, do not escalate to MGS/engineering, as we cannot provide the search algorithms, please see below guidance for partners:

Recommended guidance

  • Business profile search results are derived based on filtered profiles, which are then ranked
  • Only 24 profiles are fetched for each customer query
  • If your profile is not ranked higher, it may not surface in the search results, even though it matches all the filter criteria
  • To view if your business profile is properly published, click on the view link in the business profile section

Criteria for Best match category

Below data from business profiles is used to fetch a filtered list of profiles

  • Location - based on radius.
  • Exact match is required with the customer org size and any one of the other fields in your business profile
  • Industry expertise
  • Product
  • Service type
  • Skills and capability
  • Solutions
  • After fetching the filtered profiles, ranks are assigned to the profiles based on the below earned attributes.
  • Azure expert MSP
  • Advanced specialization
  • Endorsements
  • Competencies
  • Past referrals conversion data
  • Criteria for Most responsive category This section takes into account, the past performance with respect to the number of referrals received, number of referrals accepted among them, time to accept, number of referrals marked as won etc.
  • Criteria for Nearest to you category This is based on the address that is entered in the search. If a city name is entered in the search, the city center latitude and longitude as determined by Bing maps is considered for measuring the distance from the partners location in their profile to the city center.

Customer contact partner / generate referral leads

If the partner’s expertise meets customer’s demand and the customer is interested in the partner, the customer clicks Contact, and fills out a form with his/her business needs and clicks Send.

[Asset 4558164]

[Asset 4569922]

An email will be generated and sent to the contact listed in the partner’s business profile notifying the partner a referral lead has been generated for the partner business.

Customers often choose to send the same RFI to multiple companies at the same time. To increase your chance of success, we recommend accepting or declining new leads within 24 hours or less.

Note: Make sure that the partner knows who the Referral Admin is in their organization, and if this will be the same person to receive the referrals (the email address entered in the business profile). Advise them to check spam filters for missed referral notifications. This person can create distribution list to allow other people from his organization to also receive the notification email and respond as soon as possible to the referral. We recommend that an alias or distribution list is used that will allow multiple people to receive/access the email notifications to ensure that the referral can be responded to within the timeframe.

All the requests that originate from the profile listing on the Microsoft solution provider page are available in the Leads section under the REFERRALS tab in the left navigation pane. Please note that all the customer engagements through Partner Finder are now called LEADS and not Engagements as before.

Types of leads

  • Customer enquires – These are leads that are directly originating from the customers. A prospect customer company’s employee found partner’s company profile and filled the enquiry form stating the need and the contact details.
  • Marketing qualified – These are leads that are sent to partner company by Microsoft employees who received the customer request through one of the various marketing channels.
  • Sales qualified – These are leads that are sent to partners by Microsoft sellers who gather the requirements from a customer and are helping them find the right partner to solve a customer problem. These leads are not be confused with Co-sell opportunities where a Microsoft seller is actively engaged with the partner and customer till the deal closure.
Navigating the Leads section

There are three top level tabs in the leads section. The functionality of the same is described below.

  • Marketplace leads – All the leads originating from Microsoft solution provider page which are of the type customer enquiries fall are available in this tab.
  • Qualified leads – All the leads of type Marketing qualified and Sales qualified are available in this tab.
  • Favorites – Any leads that are marked as favorites in the Marketplace leads or Qualified leads tab will be shown in this tab. You can mark any lead as a favorite by clicking on the heart icon on the lead in the list view. You can remove the lead from favorites by just clicking on the same icon again.

Responding to a lead

Important disclaimer: Please note that Microsoft DOES NOT verify the identity of referrals or whether the interests of those referrals are legitimate. Microsoft has only verified that partners may contact the individual or company being referred in accordance with the details provided. This referral should not be construed as a Microsoft endorsement of the company or individual being referred. It is partner’s responsibility to take any actions necessary and appropriate to safeguard their company from fraud in connection with these referrals.

Each lead moves through a life cycle of its own and this section will help partners understand the different stages and the actions that they can take in each stage.

[Asset 4569926]

Received stage: In this stage partners receive a new lead either directly from the customer or from a Microsoft employee. Partners should review the details, and feel free to contact the customer if they want to learn more about their business needs. They can take two actions in this stage. Either accept or decline the referral:

  • Accept: Partners should enter a name for the deal, the estimated deal value, and the estimated purchase timeframe. They should also provide info in the Notes field to explain more about what the customer is looking for. They can optionally enter their CRM ID (for their reference only), the marketing campaign ID which resulted in the respective lead and add additional contacts from their company. When they're finished, they should select Next. We'll move the referral to the next stage, which means that the partner plans to actively engage with the customer to address their need. We'll also use this information to help partners find similar deals in the future. 

[Asset 4569927]

[Asset 4569928]

  • Decline: Partners need to select the reason they’re declining the deal and add any notes they would like to include, then select Close deal. We'll archive it as Declined and notify the customer to choose a different partner.

If partners don't respond within the allotted time (currently 14 days), we'll archive it as Expired and notify the customer to choose a different partner.

[Asset 4569929]

Tip - A customer has the option to explicitly request that interested partners contact them directly. If this is the case, they'll see an alert at the top of the page with a flame icon. We strongly recommend partners to reach out to contact the customer as quickly as possible to improve their chances of winning the lead. After 72 hours, the lead will continue to be active, but the icon and message will change. Partners should still contact the customer if they're interested in pursuing the lead.
