Summary: Ad Copy Creation

Through the Advertiser Enablement Services (AES) program, Back Office Support (BOS) advocates execute a creation of Expanded Text Ads on behalf of the Microsoft Advertising Account Management Team and Frontline MAST Services & Support advocates.

The BOS team processes the following ad copy services:

  • Convert standard text ads to expanded text ads
  • Create expanded text ads with dynamic text using param insertion
  • Create new expanded text ads for new and existing campaigns and ad groups
  • Update/Revise existing expanded text ads

BOS will deliver an output file containing ads with both a clear call-to-action and keywords from the account. The ads are more engaging for the advertiser’s potential customers, giving the opportunity to increase clicks. These ads are written in a compelling way that use advertisers’ language and highlight the benefits your advertisers are looking for when searching online.
