1. Summary

Go-to Market with Microsoft offers are marketing offers offered as part of the MPN membership to our Partners. Microsoft provides exceptional marketing resources to help our partners accelerate time to market, generate leads, and grow cloud business.

What we intend to offer Partners, according to their membership level, is to:

  • Get to market faster: Microsoft Partners need to get to market as fast as possible. This means training teams, enabling sellers, and creating great marketing content that resonates with customers. By learning from our experts, leveraging industry research, and assessing new opportunities, our marketing resources help you do it all, and do it fast.
  • Generate leads: To find more customers, partners must reach a bigger audience. We’ve made it simple and easy to create campaigns and enable sellers to connect with more customers through multiple channels, like social media and resource tools. Partners make a big splash when they take advantage of co-branding opportunities with Microsoft.
  • Grow cloud business: Find customers faster when you partner with Microsoft. Gain visibility with both Microsoft sellers and our partner ecosystem. Expand your marketing footprint. Maximize the use of every selling channel.

2. Go-To-Market with Microsoft Resources and Offers

ISV Go-To-Market with Microsoft offers have been made available in Partner Center to activate as of June 18, 2020

This Go-To-Market with Microsoft resources and offers framework guides Partners through marketing practice building and give access to resources, offers and programs that support successfully improving their direct channel, co-marketing and co-selling with Microsoft to help Partners grow their business.

Offers will not be outlined in the license table, they will be shared with frontline support only for awareness. Based on the info outlined in this field readiness guide, agents can offer more info to Partners.

Network members

  • Digital Marketing Content OnDemand delivers always-on digital marketing content in fresh weekly campaigns. Through an intelligent tool partners are empowered to start generating demand and connecting with customers online through social media, company blogs, and email.
  • Smart Partner Marketing is a marketing how-to hub, filled with marketing essentials including the latest industry research, market trends, partner best practices, free tools, templates, and more.
  • Sales Enablement Platform allows partners to access and customize Azure, Dynamics 365, and Microsoft 365 content within their Office applications. Track your customer’s engagement with your documents to gain insights into how customers interact with your proposals.
  • Partner Marketing Center provides access to thousands of sales and marketing assets in this comprehensive content library, including sales presentations, infographics, web images, and more!
  • Geo Expansion Readiness Assessment helps partners break into new geographies to initiate growth in new markets and drive scale of strategic solutions.
  • Geo Expansion Webinar Series, partners will gain insight and best practices for growing your business and expanding into new markets when you sign up for this series of webinars. Guest presenters will include market expansion field experts, Microsoft leadership, and successful program partners.

  • Profile Optimization and Referral Management help partners increase their visibility with customers and capture their attention when their profile listing is optimized. Partners will get customized feedback and recommendations to ensure their profile listing showcases their solution’s features and benefits. 4. Partner fulfillment in Solution Workspace

For Silver and/ ISV Partners with a co-sell ready solution and Gold Partners and/or ISV partners with an IP co-sell ready solution, the partner will fulfill and track their GTM offers in Solution Workspace. This is where partners can start their offer, track its progress, and perform partner activities needed to complete the offer service. 

The steps below will cover the actions taken by a partner in Solution Workspace when working on their offer or reward. Although we encourage partners to use Solution Workspace as a self-serve tool and lessen their reliance on engagement managers, it is important for engagement managers to understand the steps which partners will take when working on their offers and rewards in Solution Workspace to help guide and assist when necessary. Engagement managers WILL NOT work in Solution Workspace but continue to accomplish their daily tasks in OCP GTM.

Accessing go-to-market offers and rewards

[Asset 4593533]

Partners with a Microsoft competency, ISV partners with a co-sell ready solution, ISV Connect partners, and partners with a published solution in the Microsoft commercial marketplace will have access to start working on Go-To-Market Offers and Marketplace Rewards. To access their go-to-market offers and rewards, they can go to the My Solutions landing page and sign in. Here, they will find their solutions and applicable go-to-market offers and rewards. If they have more than four Go-To-Market Offers or Marketplace Rewards, they can view them by selecting the View all option to go to a gallery.

Their offers and rewards will be listed in the gallery in the following order:

  • In progress:
    • Action required
    • Most recent
  • Available
  • Completed
  • Expired

They can search for offers or rewards in a few ways. Use the Filter option on the left side to search by the status of offers or rewards. Use the search bar to enter key terms to look for a particular offer or reward by either title or name. They can also use the Sort by drop-down list on the right side to arrange the offers and rewards by either status or latest.

When they find the offer or reward that they’re looking for, select it to view the details of that page.

[Asset 4593534]

Learning about available offers and rewards

The Go-To-Market Offers and Marketplace Rewards that appear on the page are those that are available to their company. To learn more details of an offer or reward and the value that it can bring to their solution or company, select its card.
Selecting the card will take they to a page with the details of the Go-To-Market Offer or Marketplace Reward. They can learn about the main benefits of using this offer or reward on the Details tab. Some offers and rewards have an example of what they would receive if they were to enact it. If an offer or reward has this example, a thumbnail of it will appear. To view a full example, select the Download example option to download an example that they can open and review.

Starting their go-to-market offers and rewards

[Asset 4593535]

When they select a card, whether to learn more about the offer or reward or to start acting on it, they will land on its Details page. If they’re ready to start acting on their offer or reward, select the Start now button, after which they will land on the Workflow section of the page.

Here, they will find a progress bar, Get started instructions, a Work area section, and a Latest activity section.

The progress bar will show they, at a glance, where their offer or reward is in the process. As they move through the steps to act on the offer or reward, their position on the progress bar will also move.

The Get started section can have templates or helpful resources to provide a guide for their information or give they more context about what they have to provide. This section will always provide the steps for they to progress through the offer or reward.

In the Work area section, they can complete fields, upload files, or add a comment to their Partner Engagement Manager or Marketplace Rewards Engagement Manager. The fields can change, based on their current stage. Comments that they enter in the Comment section will go to their Engagement Manager. Upon submission, upload fields will change to read-only. They will always be able to enter comments.

[Asset 4593548]
 

After they complete an activity in the Work area section, it will appear in the Latest activity section of the page. When they’re first starting, they will see the text “No activity yet” in this section. They will also find comments from their Engagement Manager in the Latest activity section.

Keeping track of their progress with go-to-market offers and rewards

They can track their progress through Go-To-Market Offers and Marketplace Rewards in several ways in Solution Workspace. The first way is the colored status found on the card itself. They can find this indicator on the card, either on the My Solutions landing page or in the gallery. It has a few states:

  • In progress
  • Available
  • Completed
  • Expired

[Asset 4593549]

When an offer or reward is in progress, the step that the offer or reward is in will appear under the status itself. This step is identical to the status on the progress bar that appears in the Workflow section. Also, if they’re interested in the details about the progress of an individual offer or reward, go to the page by selecting the card and going to the Workflow section. Here, they will find the progress bar and the Latest activity section.

If they have questions, they can use the Comment section or send a message to the email address that is listed on each offer or reward.

[Asset 4593550]

Adding contacts to their offers and rewards

Any individual at their company can view, start, or take action on a Go-To-Market Offer or Marketplace Reward. However, they might want to call attention to one or more offers or rewards to specific individuals at their company. If they want a particular person to view a Go-To-Market Offer or Marketplace Reward, they can invite them.

To invite a teammate, go to the Contacts section of the offer or reward, enter their email address, and select the +Invite button. This action will open a dialog box to confirm that they want to add their teammate. After they select the Add and send invite option, their default email application will open with a prepopulated email. After they send the email, and the recipient has accepted the invitation by selecting the access link within it, their teammate will appear in the Contacts table.

Adding a teammate will list them as a contact in the Contacts section, or, if that person is an individual outside their company, it will give them access to the Go-To-Market Offer or Marketplace Reward within Solution Workspace. Contacts who are added here are granted permission only to that Go-To-Market Offer or Marketplace Reward.

8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is gtm@microsoft.com a support alias for Partners?

A: No. gtm@microsoft.com should only be contacted when you encounter one of the scenarios from section 5 of the article. The rest of the scenarios should be escalated to MPN Global Support.

Q: Are GTM offers competency-specific?

A: No, As a member of the Microsoft Network partners have access to resources and offers depending on where they are with their partnership with Microsoft. All partners have access to self-service digital marketing resources. Service partners with a Silver/Gold competency and/or ISV partners with a IP/co-sell ready solution can unlock access to a marketing specialist and additional marketing offers.

Q: What Go-To-Market with Microsoft partner ready resources are available to partners?
  • Go-To-Market with Microsoft website: External website
  • Go-To-Market with Microsoft Partner Overview (ppt): Learn about marketing resources, offers, and programs available to all members of the Microsoft Partner Network to help you increase visibility with and connection to customers.

Q&A around ISV Go-To-Market with Microsoft offers that have been made available in Partner Center on June 18, 2020:

Q: When will ISV partners be eligible for Go-To-Market with Microsoft Offers?

A: If an ISV partner has a co-sell ready/IP co-sell ready solution when the feature releases GTM Offers will be available to the partner within their Partner Center Dashboard. If an ISV partner does not have a co-sell ready/IP co-sell ready solution available at the time of launch they will not have GTM offers available however when their solution does become co-sell ready/IP co-sell GTM offers will them be available.

Q: How long will ISV partners have access to their GTM offers?

A: ISV partners will have access to their GTM Offers within their dashboard for a year upon achievement of co-sell ready status if they don’t have a silver/gold competency.

Q: What happens if an ISV partners solution is no longer co-sell ready?

A: The ISV partner will continue to have access to the GTM offers within the partner center dashboard for the year.

Q: When will the GTM offers expire?

A: GTM Offers that have not been activated will expire in a year. Once a GTM offer is activated, partners have 3 months to complete the activity with the GTM Desk.

Q: When will ISV partners with a co-sell ready solution will have access to new GTM offers?

A: ISV partners will have access to new GTM offers at the change of the fiscal year (July 1, 2021).

Q: What happens if a partner has a competency and a co-sell ready solution?

A: The partner will receive only one set up GTM offers, dependent on the partner scenarios below:

  • Scenario 1: An ISV Partner has a silver competency + an IP cosell ready solution partner will receive all offers available in the “silver column + the “new” offers available in the gold column – receiving all GTM offers
  • Scenario 2: An ISV Partner has a gold competency + an IP cosell ready solution – partner will receive GTM offers aligned to the Gold competency
  • Scenario 3: A partner has a Gold Competency and ISV with a cosell solution – partner will receive GTM offers aligned to the Gold competency
Q: What happens if the ISV partner does not renew their competency?

A: If the competency is not renewed however the partner has a co-sell ready/IP co-sell ready solution the partner will continue to receive the GTM offers aligned to that status.

Q: Are GTM offers awarded per solution?

A: No only one set of GTM offers are awarded per partner.

Q&A around Co-branded marketing assets and request submission form:

Q: Partner submitted a form to request a co-branded asset but I haven’t heard from anyone. When will they get the asset?

A: Partners will be contacted by their Partner Engagement Manager within 3 business days of submitting a request for a co-branded asset. The PEM will work with the partner to finalize the asset. If it has been more than 3 business days, please follow the steps in this document.

Q: Partner clicked on the link in Solution Workspace to get to the asset submission form but is being told they need “admin consent” to access the form. What does that mean?

A: Admin consent means that the IT admin at the partners company needs to allow the Ansira Partner Go-To-Market Toolbox to access their AAD tenant. Without admin consent, some partners will not be able to access the Toolbox. This is the partner’s internal policy setting decided on by the partner’s IT department and cannot be changed by Microsoft.

Additional information about the Solution Workspace experience is available in article Guideline: Partner: Solution Workspace.

Q: Partner clicked on the link in Solution Workspace to get to the asset submission form but is being asked to consent to the application accessing some of their data. Is this safe to do?

A: This is safe for the partner to do. This Microsoft application is built using another company’s software so we have to ask for each user’s permission to access name, email and partner information to provide the best user experience.

Additional information about the Solution Workspace experience is available in article Guideline: Partner: Solution Workspace.

Q: Partner clicked on the link in Solution Workspace to get to the asset submission form but is being told they do not have permissions to access the form. Why don’t they have access?

A: The Toolbox queries Partner Center to validate partner status. If no partner status is returned, or the partner status returns as inactive, the user will not be granted access. Please ensure that partner status is active and the partner is in good standing. If the partner membership is up to date and the partner should have access, please escalate to the Toolbox team for review.

Additional information about the Solution Workspace experience is available in article Guideline: Partner: Solution Workspace.

Q: Partner needs to go back into the form and make some edits to what they submitted previously. Why can’t they make changes?

A: Partners can make changes to their answers in the form until the Microsoft team has assigned the asset request to one of their team members for review. While the asset is in review by the Microsoft team, partners cannot make edits. If further information is required from the partner those details will be requested by the PEM. If there are edits or additional details a partner would like to make while the asset is in review they can work with their assigned PEM to make those updates.

Q: Partner requested an asset from Microsoft and received the final version but now can’t access it using the link they received previously. What happened to their asset?

A: The links to the final versions of assets shared with the partners are only good for 30 days. After 30 days, the link shared with the partner will expire. If the partner still needs access to the document, they should contact their PEM to request a new link. The partner should be encouraged to download the asset and save it to their files as the new link will also expire in 30 days.
