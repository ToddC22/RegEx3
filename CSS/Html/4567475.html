ASMS – Applying Credits and Refunds in CST

Submit a ModTrack request to update this TSG.

 

Overview

This TSG provides guidance on how to credit and refund customers via the Customer Support Tool (CST).

Note: If you are looking for guidance on how to credit or refund a customer via CMAT, see TSG 3084129.

Policy

  • In CST, every invoice is settled on its own.

    • For example: if a customer receives an invoice for $12 USD and a refund for $5 USD, they will need to pay the full $12 USD on the invoice, as opposed to just paying the difference of $7 USD. The $5 USD refund will be deducted from their next invoice.

  • When the Refund action is applied to an invoice in CST, a negative adjustment will be posted on the affected account.

    • The invoice on which the Refund was processed must be paid in full.

    • This adjustment takes effect on the next invoice that is generated. Be sure to notify customers of this.

    • Importantly, this is not a refund to the customer’s credit card. There is no operation in CST that directly triggers a refund to the credit card.

If the final amount on an invoice generated after a Refund is negative, the customer’s credit card is automatically refunded. Alternatively, if the final amount is positive, the amount is debited from the customer’s credit card.

 

Support Scenarios

Credit or Refund

For guidance on how to credit or refund a customer via CST, follow the Issuing Refunds section of the TUG TSG 4487934.

Amend an Invoice

In addition to a customer receiving a credit or refund, customers may ask to have their invoice amended. In these situations, you should note the following:

  • Not every processed refund requires an amended invoice.

  • When an invoice paid with a credit card is amended, the earlier overpaid amount is issued as a refund to the credit card on the 10th day after the generation of the new, amended invoice. 

  • When an invoice is paid via wired transfer or check, do not amend the invoice. The credits (processed refund) will flow to the next invoice.

  • If a customer has not yet paid an invoice, it can be amended regardless of the payment method.

Important: Before amending an invoice, please be sure that a minimum of two hours have elapsed since the last operation on the invoice.

To have an invoice amended, do the following:

  1. Send a collaboration task to your TA/PTA following the normal engagement process outlined in TSG 2929022.

  2. Your TA will review the request and follow the TA-only actions below to amend the invoice:

    1. In CST, click Amend.

    2. For Amendment Type, select Recalculate Invoice.

    3. For Reason, select Refund.

    4. Enter any relevant comments, and then click Next.

    5. Click Submit.

      • This will amend the current invoice, taking the Refund into account. The system will expect the new amount post-adjustment, and that amount will be charged to the credit card.
