1. Office 2019 FAQ and features

1.1 How do I update/upgrade to Office 2019?

If you have a Microsoft 365 for home subscription, you already have the most current version of Office at no additional cost. As a subscriber, you regularly get new and improved Office features and you'll always have the latest fixes and security updates. In other words, there is no need to get a one-time purchase of Office 2019.

If you have a one-time purchase of an older version of Office, such as Office Home & Student 2016, you don't receive an automatic update or upgrade to Office 2019 for free. You can purchase an Microsoft 365 subscription or Office 2019 as a one-time license. We recommend purchasing Microsoft 365 as your best option.

For more information about Office upgrades, see How do I upgrade Office?.

1.3 What new features will I have after update or upgrade to Office 2019?

If you have a Microsoft 365 for home subscription, you already have the most current version of Office at no additional cost. As a subscriber, you regularly get new and improved Office features and you'll always have the latest fixes and security updates. To view the new features available to Office 365 subscribers, see What's new in Microsoft 365.

If you purchased a one-time license of Office 2019 to replace an older version of Office, see What's new in Office 2019.

Return to top

1.4 Why should I buy Office 2019?

If you have an older version of Office, such as Office Home and Student 2016 or 2013, purchasing a one-time license of Office 2019 will provide benefit of newer features over older one-time purchase versions of Office. For a list of these new features, see What's new in Office 2019.

If you have an Microsoft 365 subscription, you already have access to those new features. There is no need to get a one-time purchase of Office 2019.

Return to top

1.5 Is there anything I need to do as an active Microsoft 365 subscriber?

No, If you have an Microsoft 365 for home subscription, you already have the most current version of Office at no additional cost on your current Windows operating system. As a subscriber, you regularly get new and improved Office features and you'll always have the latest fixes and security updates. For more information, see What's new in Office 365.
