Procedure: Partner: Global Email Template

[Asset 4090831]

Summary

Who should use this article?
This applies to all MPN and CSP frontline advocates from all regions.

What’s the article about?
This article presents the email templates and methodology that defines how frontline support uses these templates when communicating to partner via email regarding a case issue, resolution provided, and next steps.

When should use this article?
MPN and CSP frontline advocates when completing web submission cases via email and following the attempt process when performing follow-up emails to partners.

The goal of this procedure is to standardize email templates for all MPN and CSP frontline support. The templates were structured to convey appropriate messaging and provide partners and frontline support guidance on next steps of the interaction.

Additional Information needed to move on with the investigation / Providing possible resolution and waiting for partner’s confirmation before closing the case in CAP/Service Desk tools.

  1. 1st attempt -> Call Customer -> Customer is Unresponsive -> Set Disposition whichever is applicable -> Send Email
  2. 48H later -> 2nd attempt -> Call Customer -> Customer is Unresponsive -> Set Disposition whichever is applicable -> Send Email
  3. 48H later -> 3rd attempt -> Call Customer -> Customer is Unresponsive -> Contact QA to verify Case -> If QA approves the resolution/Case handling -> set disposition whichever is applicable -> Send Email and Close Case
  4. If solution is NOT achieved and partner will be OOO for more than 2 weeks, offer to archive the case temporarily and they can create a new ticket when they come back to continue with resolution referencing the old SR.
  5. Is mandatory for advocates to send an e-mail after each phone call, for responsive partners, with a summary related to the information provided/ topics discussed over the phone.

Here’s some information that should help:

We have noticed that while renewing the IUR <add product name> subscription, you have added <mention number of seats added> additional seats onto your existing license count and currently have a total of <add total seat count>, decreasing your expiration date which is not until <Add licenses end date>.

Please note that the Microsoft Partner Network is not a licensing program, it's a suite of services aimed to help you grow your business. Therefore, it is the partner’s responsibility to manage their MPN account and benefits and we don’t offer the same level of service for MPN product licenses as for licenses purchased through commercial channels.

Unfortunately, since the action was performed on the <add date of last activation of key>, our team cannot provide a new token for replacement. We can work on providing replacements if the request comes to us within 30 days from the product activation date.

You can solve this issue on your next renewal, by removing the extra licenses/seats from users to have same number of seats or less than the new token received. This action must be performed before activating the key. In case you are not able to perform this action, please reach out to us.

We are sorry for the inconvenience that this caused to you and your business as we understand the importance of your Microsoft products. 

For the background process, please refer to this public documentation regarding adding IUR seats instead of renewing.

We will go ahead and archive the request, however, please feel free to reach out in case you need further assistance. You can open a ticket in your Partner Center here.
