3.2 Courier Pickup (CSO)

The service center will contact the customer directly via email within two business days with a link to arrange collection of the non-working product by Quantium Solutions.

Customers should pack the non-functioning product into a sturdy box that they must provide before the courier arrives at their location to pick up the parcel.

Pick-up occurs only after agreement of a pickup date is reached.

Return to top

3.3 Email Print Label (SEI)

The service center will contact the customer directly via email within two business days with a link to arrange collection of the non-working product.

Customers should pack the non-functioning product into a sturdy box that they must provide before they drop-off the non-functioning item to the nearest Australia Post location.

Return to top

3.4 Prepaid Box with Label (SST)

Surface Studio only. The service center will ship a prepaid empty box to the customer within two business days. The empty box will contain the return instructions for collection.

Return to top

3.5 Advanced Exchange (SAD)

The service center will ship a replacement first. Once the replacement has been received by the customer, the customer should package the non-functioning product into the same box and use the return instructions included to arrange collection.

Return to top

3.6 No Action Required (NCA)

The service center will ship a replacement and the customer is not required to return the non-functioning product.

Return to top

5. Australia consumer law

Australia consumers have the right to repair, replace, or refund in certain situations. Please refer to KB 4100081 Guideline: Microsoft and the Australian and New Zealand Consumer Law. Where a consumer has that right to repair, replace, or refund, you must follow the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) policy and you must not charge or collect any fee for out of warranty (OOW) service.

If a customer has processed more than two device exchanges, see KB 4502115: Procedure: Assisting customers from Australia or New Zealand who have had two or more device exchange orders.
