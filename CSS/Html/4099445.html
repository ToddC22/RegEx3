Advocates need to create a new case with contact details (email and phone number) in Service Desk (+Case) when a Phone call is received. E-mail cases are created automatically in SD through the Webform – You can view those cases in the Unassigned Cases section.

IMPORTANT HUAWEI EXCEPTION

The only exception is that Advocates must not currently create cases for Huawei (the company, not e.g. end users of Huawei phones).  The full details of this Huawei exception is documented in:

Supporting Huawei, and Huawei Partners and Customers: https://internal.support.services.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4507208?nochrome

Prior to creating a new case, Advocates must check if the customer already has an existing case created in Service Desk:

  • Ask if the customer is contacting for a new or existing case.
  • You can also view all customer´ cases using the E-mail address to search for a customer.
  • If a customer is found:
    • If a case is already created, is in Status Open and the customer would like to continue working on the existing case, Advocates must continue working on the existing case.
    • If a case is already created and is in Status Closed, Advocates must check that the customer´s issue is the same AND that the case was closed within the last 14 days.  If both are true, re-open the case; otherwise,  create a new case for the issue.

Note

Every time you click +Case, a new case is created, even when you select to continue working on an existing case.

  • To avoid this behavior, it´s recommended to-keep one case open for the entire day just to search for customers
  • When you create a case and you don’t search for the contact first and you click on active case – it is important to know that only on the search field you can create a contact. On the active case tab there is no + contact field until no contact is listed.

New Cases

The Manager, Supervisor or SME will be assigning the Unassigned Cases to the Advocates. In some regions, Advocates will assign cases according to their workload volume. Cases will be divided equally and/or as per business need.

Misroutes coming from other teams:

Note: cases coming from other teams – should have Misroute in the title and should be picked up first.

EMEA has also agreed process with GetHelp and ROC please escalate the case to tier 2 if you cannot solve the issue in the first contact.

Note: It is also possible that cases are coming from Rave – (transferred from External)

Out of scope:

If you identify that the case is not in scope for your team, transfer it to right team immediately using this SDK article: 4503246 How to Transfer to Other Teams

 

NOAM: Advocates should reply and continue working on the case through E-mail unless the customer requests a phone call to continue working on the case.

EMEA-Specific Guidance

MPSA:

Beginning November 2019 NGVL cases are handled by Tier 2 only.

If Frontline teams get a phone call then create the case, set the right expectation that customer will receive a call back, and create the case with correct SAP the case will be automatically routed to the VL_EMEA_MPSA

Webform cases are automatically transferred VL_EMEA_MPSA

VIP:

VIP customer should get assigned with priority – see VIP article: https://internal.support.services.microsoft.com/en-US/help/4527228

 
 
  1. For every VL, OLM and eAgreements customer email reaching the VL EMEA support team where the customer has provided a phone number in the email, you will first try to contact the customer via phone. 
  2. You should try the phone numbers provided by the customer (e.g. in the signature). Also look for an alternative phone number in any of the Microsoft tools available (Explore.ms, CMAT…)

Exceptions

  • Thank you e-mails do not require a call-back. 
  • Misroutes: any e-mail contact not concerning VL, NGVL, OLM or eAgreements (i.e. not our support scope) and need to be referred to other departments, do not require a call-back. 
  • Customer provided further data: After we requested the customer some additional information in order to resolve the case, if we are not able to resolve the case by calling the customer back (e.g. the case needs to be escalated), then you can simply answer the email acknowledging the information and informing the customer that we will contact him/ her back as soon as we have received a response from the team. 
  • Customer or partner says explicitly they want to have the case handled via email.

Existing Cases

Advocates will keep ownership of cases so long as they are opened and they´ve worked on the case.

  • Advocates are responsible of their list of open cases and must ensure the case is updated (Follow up E-mail, reply, escalate, etc.) according to the business rules and existing processes.

Noam specific: Initiate a phone call with the customer only when the case is too complex to handle through E-mail.

Case Ownership Process

If an agent finds a closed case pertaining to the customer’s current issue, created by another agent

Agent reopens the case, continues assisting the customer seeking the promptest resolution. If the case must be followed-up on, the agent who reopened the case keeps ownership of the case, unless the customer proactively requests to speak with the original case owner or it is an escalated case.

If an agent finds an opened case pertaining the customer’s issue, created by another agent
  • Agent continues assisting the customer, seeking to resolve the customer’s case within the call as a priority. If the agent is unable to resolve the case, and it must be followed-up on, the last agent to touch the case keeps ownership of the case moving forward, unless the customer proactively requests to speak with the original case owner or it is an escalated case.

More Information

In the event that the owner of a case is out of the office, the supervisor, SME or manager will assign case to the next available agent. Upon return, the case will be re-assigned to the original owner (If needed).

If a case was routed to an agent who is not the owner, the agent can send an email to the SME or Supervisor to re-assign the case. Agent should include screenshot on how he/she was able to view that the case was owned by another person. This is to lessen the time needed by the SME/Supervisor in verifying the case. 

For escalated cases, SME and/or Supervisor will leave a note about the status of the case or resolution. After which, case will be re-assigned back to the agent to notify the customer.

Special Scenarios per LOB

For MCT, automated (MCT Application Submitted) email will be routed to the Advocates via Outlook. Agent will send the outbound email to the candidate through Service Desk

Advocates should close the case in Service Desk according to the definitions below. Check with your Lead / Supervisor if there are questions related.

Status

Usage Policy & Examples

Resolved

  1. The customer solution provided to the customer:
  • customer confirmed resolution or
  • Support provided solution but no response from customer after following ‘the unresponsive case handling\strike process’
  1. The customer went unresponsive and advocate followed the ‘unresponsive case handling\strike process’. All unresponsive customers will be closed as resolved also when no solution was provided.

Exception: webform cases where the scope was not determined and the unresponsive case handling\strike process was followed but customer never replied should be closed as Disconnect hang up  – see also Disconnect/hang-up section

Resolved External

The customer was redirected to a website, forum or 3rd party (Non Admin or Reseller) for a known and confirmed resolution.

Resolved by Customer

The customer notifies Support that they have resolved the issue on their own.

Un-Resolved

  1. Transfer and redirects:
  • Transfers or redirect to non-Service Desk teams/resources (either Microsoft or 3rd Party)
  •  Redirect to Primary Admin and Reseller
  • Note: For MS teams which are in SD transfer and process is agreed use the transfer option - case should not be closed unresolved as redirect see article: https://internal.support.services.microsoft.com/en-US/help/4488085
  1. Offline work -with no engagement with customers.
  2. Case that receive approval from Team Manager

Disconnect/hang-up
  1. Connection with the customer is lost
  2. Contact info is not available - Anonymous case logging

Note If advocate capture the phone number or email address – advocate should re – contacting the customer and case should not be closed as disconnect

Root Cause - Commercial Communications\Channel\Phone\Disconnect | Ghost

Undetermined Scope:

webform cases where the scope was not determined and the unresponsive case handling\strike process was followed but customer never replied should be closed as Disconnect hang up  Choose the following Agent identified RCA: Root Cause - Commercial Communications\Channel\Email\Duplicate | Undetermined Scope | Bounce

Closed Duplicate
  1. Customers or advocates are creating for the same issue multiple requests.
  • Change the Case Title to reference the case # of which this case is a duplicate (e.g. change the title to "Dupe of <Case_Number>).
  • The case in which the resolution is actually tracked should not be closed as Duplicate.
  1. Cases created to search for a customer record (+case for searching)
  2. Test cases
  3. Customer remains blocked and get the final negative message (714, trade etc.)

  • you are not able to capture the email address because the call was disconnected, and you were not able to call back the customer

Redirects

I´ve sent an E-mail to the wrong alias, what should I do?

Email: If you have sent email to wrong alias there is no way to undo and the email is sent. For example if you have reached out to the wrong escalation team – just sent a follow up email to ignore the email before.

Notes: If you saved the notes there is also no way to delete the notes

Important if a wrong email is sent to customer contact your team lead to open ticket with data protection team!

How can I find the notes I have put into the case especially the notes and content from previous days?

After around 1 day the notes will be visible under History/TimeLine.

[Asset 4338022]

What I´ve copied and pasted the data from another source in SD but is not clear / easy to read, what should I do?

Paste the content into a Notepad and copy again to paste in SD.

What if I´ve pasted the Case Number in the SD case notes, but is not visible due to font being white?

Copy and paste the Case number from the URL.

[Asset 4338025]

I would like to provide feedback (Issue, new feature), how can I do that?

We are open to feedback directly from Advocates. If you would like to submit a feedback, open Service Desk and click Feedback on the bottom right
You will land in the home page.
Recommended to first search for a similar feedback item and vote since it would increase the visibility (Feedback with more votes will have higher visibility). Otherwise, proceed to create a new feedback item.
