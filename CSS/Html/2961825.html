Procedure: Xbox: Handling hardware refund requests

[Asset 4049349]

Who should use this article?

  • All Consumer

What is this article about?

This article provides guidance on how to handle Xbox hardware refund requests.

1. Misdiagnosed or no fault found (NFF)

If the customer has followed online or agent-assisted troubleshooting steps and paid for an Out of Warranty (OOW) console repair or accessory replacement and requests a refund, follow the steps below:

  1. Verify the customer.
  2. Complete case handling for the customer’s issue and identify the root cause of the issue.
    • If the same root cause is identified, refer to KB 2229086 Procedure: Xbox: Problems with a delivered product.
    • If a different root cause is identified, proceed with a repair or fulfillment as indicated.
  3. If the customer requests a refund for the original accessory purchased, the customer must proceed with a corrective fulfillment order before the case can be transferred.
    • Advise the customer they may not be required to return the original item (This will be determined by your Offline Team).
  4. Please utilize P-Chat, suppliers IM tool or your sites specific method to contact your Offline Team for assistance, if requested by your Offline Team then transfer the customer’s SR to your specific variance Queue. For transferring, refer to KB 817557 Guidelines: Advanced Capability Advocate engagement Tier 1 Variance Queues:
    • Repair/ Fulfilment Order SR number
    • Justification for Refund
    • New Repair and Fulfilment Order SR number (If different from the transferred SR number)

2. Console returned to customer unrepaired due to modification

If the customer calls requesting a refund after receiving their console back unrepaired and received a letter indicating the console has been modified, follow the steps below:

  1. Fortify the customer.
  2. Check the order information and warranty on the console.
  3. If it is <6 weeks from the date the console was processed, advise them of the time frame for refunds. DO NOT contact your Offline Team or process a refund via MST.
  4. If it is >6 weeks from the date that the console was processed, please process a refund for the transaction. Refer to KB 840536 Procedure: Microsoft Store: Digital content return/refund.

    Note: for issues with processing refund, please contact your Offline Team for assistance.

3. Advance exchange orders where the customer has been charged and the product was received at the SCV

This applies for AE orders where the customer has returned either the ticketed or advance exchange product and their credit card was charged for a replacement product.

Examples:

  • We haven’t received the product from customer.
  • They sent us something that was incorrect.
  1. Fortify the customer.
  2. Select the device in MST and select the repair order in question.
  3. Identify if the order is an AE and the date that the replacement product was sent.
  4. Verify outbound tracking information in MST (AE product sent to Customer); usually received between 3-4 days.
  5. Verify the inbound tracking information from customer (Customer returned product sent to Service Center).

    • If the agent verifies that there is no charge for the AE in MST, If it is <40 days (Step 4), advise the customer that their card has a hold for replacement amount but has not been charged. Once the ticketed device has been received at service center and processed (Step 5), the hold will be removed from the payment instrument. Do Not contact your Offline Team or process a refund in MST.

    • If the agent verifies the charge in MST, please process a refund for the transaction. Refer to KB 840536 Procedure: Microsoft Store: Digital content return/refund.

      Note: For issues with processing refund, please contact your Offline Team for assistance.

4. Customer is unable to process repair order online

This only applies to customers who have attempted to place their repair order online and are unable to complete it and contact support to create an order with an agent.

  1. Verify the customer.
  2. Complete troubleshooting and process the repair or fulfillment order as indicated.
  3. If the customer indicates they were unable to complete the order online, and requests a refund of the difference in cost, please utilize P-Chat, suppliers IM tool or your sites specific method to contact your Offline Team for assistance, if requested by your Offline Team then transfer the customer’s SR to your specific variance Queue. For transferring, refer to KB 817557 Guidelines: Advanced Capability Advocate engagement and ensure the following information is captured:
    • Repair Order SR number
    • Justification for refund

5. Refunds of new hardware purchased from the Microsoft Store

  1. Verify the customer.
  2. Locate the Order in MST.
  3. Confirm the return is within warranty.
  4. Launch the line item tools (…) and select Refund Item.
  5. On the form indicate the reason for the refund.
  6. Process the refund.
  7. Inform the customer they will receive further information via email on how to process the return and once Microsoft receives the unit the refund will be issued.
