1. Applies to

The following services: All services
The following advocate roles: The appropriate first tier VL support advocate in each region
The following markets: All markets (For detailed information about product-specific availability, see 2748142 Informational: M365 Commerce: Markets where Microsoft Online Services are available.)
 

Scenario
 

Volume Licensing (VL) customers provisioned their VL licenses on one tenant but had intended to provision them on another.

Back to the top

2. Guidance

Follow the steps in Process to support VL customers when they provisioned their VL licenses on one tenant but had intended to provision them on another.

Important: Do not use the Public Customer Number (PCN) tenant remap process for activations blocked by the EA tag.

For other VL scenarios, refer to the article 2820242 Informational: M365 Commerce: Supporting Volume Licensing customers.
About which admin roles from each tool are entitled to support, please refer to Guidance section in article 2820242 Informational: M365 Commerce: Supporting Volume Licensing customers.

For customer who asks for VL remap due to migration and is part of the tenant migration list, the customer should be provided an additional 90 day extension on the grace period to give 180 days automatically.

Note: Treat remapping requests with highest priority as the customer is blocked and not able to use the service. Cases should be resolved if possible, within 24 hours. Escalate as soon as possible after completing the steps, to Tier 2/POD SME.

3. Procedure

  1. Enter the Commerce Management Agent Tool (CMAT) and select Quick Access > Lookup VL Payloads. Input customer's VL Agreement Number, and select Search.

    [Asset 4557476]
    [Asset 4557477]
  2. On the VL Payloads page, expand the details of the Payload in question. The Error Message field will display “EA tag found, Provisioning skipped”.

    [Asset 4557478]
  3. The tenant the payload was attempting to activate against will have an ea tag with the value approved.

    [Asset 4557479]
  4. This tag indicates the tenant has VL licenses purchased through the QuickStart (QS) Program, which is not compatible with other purchase channels.
  5. If either or both of the above conditions are true, do not perform a PCN tenant remap as this will have a negative impact on the customer's service. Refer the customer to their reseller to purchase from VLSC.
  6. If neither condition exists, proceed to the next step to begin the process of remapping the tenant.
  7. If the customer asks for VL remap due to migration, verify if the customer is part of the tenant migration list. If yes, provide an additional 90-day extension on the grace period to give 180 days automatically when proceeding Step b,2.
Note: If public switched telephone network (PSTN) conferencing is currently provisioned on a tenant with the same currency as the agreement, moving this agreement to a tenant with a different country (different currency) will disable the ability to use this as a payment option. In case the issues are identified, the cases should be escalated to the VL Hub team.
a. Steps 1. Explain to the customer how the PCN tenant remaps process works, call out the following points:
  • We need to receive confirmation from the global admin of the source tenant or VL admin. Only one written confirmation is required. If the customer doesn’t know who they are, please proceed as described in the below sections Specific to remapping request for EMEA when VL contact does not know the tenant name and the global admins and Specific to remapping request for EMEA when a Global admin does not know the VL admin/contact.
  • We cannot split subscriptions found under 1 EA between multiple tenants.
  • The desired target tenant needs to be existing before we can transfer the licenses.
  • No data will be transferred to the desired target tenant.
  • Licenses on the incorrect/source tenant will go In Grace period for 90 days or 180 days for customers on the tenant migration list, so that customers can back up their data.

2. Entitle the customer only on the current tenant, following 2791751 Procedure: M365 Commerce: How to verify and entitle a customer (including POBO).
Specific to remapping request for EMEA when VL contact does not know the tenant name and the global admins
If the OSM/VL admin or VL contact does not know the tenant name and the global admin of the tenant where the licenses are activated.

Tier 1 advocate responsibility:
  1. Ask OSM/VL admin or VL contact for the company name, address and customer email domain e.g. contoso.com.
  2. Verify in CMAT if the tenant Global/Company/Billing admin belongs to the same company (e.g. same email address).
    • If yes, offer the customer that you will contact the Company/Global/Billing admin via phone and then you will send an email to and add the OSM/VL admin or VL contact to the email. If the tenant has multiple Company/Global/Billing admins, work through the list until you find someone that can assist.
    • If no, request assistance from the POD SME via Yammer. The POD SME will then review the case with the POD Lead.
  3. Call the current Company/Global/Billing admin and explain the situation and inform the admin that you will add the OSM/VL admin or VL contact to your email.
  4. Send an email to the Company/Global/Billing admin and cc the OSM/VL admin and VL Contact. Here's the template:

    Subject: Courtesy Inquiry: Office 365 tenant name xx.onmicrosoft.com


    Hi <customer first name>:

    Thanks for contacting Microsoft 365 support. As we discussed via <phone, email, voicemail> I’m  following up to let you know that <colleague name> (<email>) has requested to speak with you about your Office 365 account. They would like to transfer the EA licenses from:

    Active tenant xx.onmicrosoft.com
    to
    New tenant xyz.onmicrosoft.com

    I need your approval to move the licenses. Please let me know how you’d like to proceed.

    If you need more information you can reply to let me know.

    Your service request number is %%servicerequestid%% in case you need it.

    Thanks again,

    First and Last Name
    Microsoft Customer Service and Support
    Phone Number
    Email address


Specific to remapping request for EMEA when Global admin does not know the VL admin/contact
If the global admin does not know the OSM/VL admin or VL contact where the agreements/licenses are activated.
  • The advocate is allowed to provide the VL admin/contact to the global admin.
  • The advocate should inform the global admin that we will reach out to the VL contact (if not available via call we will write an email and cc the global admin) asking to get the VL template filled out.

3. Entitle the customer on the agreement, following 2801563 Procedure: M365 Commerce: How to verify and entitle Volume Licensing customer(s).

4. Proceed to step b.
b. Suggested script “This change will result in all subscriptions under the Public Customer Number (PCN) being moved to the new tenant. No information or users will be transferred in this process – only licenses will be transferred.”

Note: If the customer wishes to split their subscriptions between domains, they will need to follow up with their licensing specialist or partner to create a new PCN. Please instruct partners to follow the same process to create a PCN with their licensing specialist.
b. Steps 1. Request permission to place the subscription into InGracePeriod. Users will have access to their service while the subscription is InGracePeriod. Make sure to verify if the customer is part of the tenant migration list. If so, provide 180 days for grace period.

If the customer is concerned about losing access to their service, check the Current Status End Date in CMAT after the subscription is put into InGracePeriod to determine when the subscription will move into Disabled status. This is the date access to the service will be lost.
[Asset 4557492]

2. Once permission is obtained, put the subscription into InGracePeriod. If the subscription is already Disabled or InGracePeriod, no further action is needed before proceeding to the next step.

3. Once the customer has been entitled on the source tenant and the agreement, and the remap process was presented, use the following email template to email the contact requesting their authorization for the remap.

Hi < customer first name >,

Thanks for contacting Microsoft 365 support. The remap will move all  subscriptions under the Public Customer Number (PCN) xxx from the source domain xx.onmicrosoft.com to the new tenant xyz.onmicrosoft.com. Here’s the information I need to continue with the remap:

Enrollment number

 
Subscriptions moving to new tenant <Subscription type and seat count>
Subscriptions to remain on existing tenant <Subscription type and seat count>
Incorrect/Current tenant Domain: <*.onmicrosoft.com domain the subscription(s) is/are currently provisioned on>
Correct/Destination tenant Domain <*.onmicrosoft.com domain the customer wants the subscription(s) provisioned under.>
 
Reason for remap request  


Please double check the form for accuracy before replying. When I’ve received the completed form, I’ll continue processing the request.

Your service request number is %%servicerequestid%% in case you need it.

Thanks again,

First and Last Name
Microsoft Customer Service and Support
Phone Number
Email address

4. Proceed to step c. 
c. Suggested script "When I receive the email authorization, I will escalate your request to have the subscriptions activated under the new tenant. You will be contacted when the process is complete."
c. Steps

1. Create a work item for the escalation team or follow process per region.

2. Include the information:
a. The template filled by customer together.
b. A screenshot of the email message that includes the header. The email must be from Global admin from the source tenant or OSM/VL admin.
c. If the customer’s email is in a language other than English, send a Word file that contains a translation of the email.

3. Escalate.
After escalation, Tier 2 will begin handling the case as described in article 3165892 Procedure: M365 Commerce: How Tier 2/POD SME support VL customers when they provisioned their licenses on wrong tenant.

4. Email customer and inform him that you escalated the request to have the subscriptions activated under the new tenant. Let him know that he will be contacted when the process is complete.
