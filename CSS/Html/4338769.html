Who should use this article

All CSP advocates from all regions

What is this article about

This article describes the procedure and scenarios for escalation to the Global Credit Services (GCS) teams.

Note: WOCS has recently changed names to GFS and now to GCS.

1. Processes 

Global Credit Services (GCS), formerly known as Worldwide Online Credit Services (WOCS), is responsible for credit checks, credit limit, collections, dunning process and cash app functions for online services like Office 365, Azure etc.

Refunds for CSP Partners that are no longer transacting

WOCS Refund

MSaaS GCS Refund

If all required information is provided, WOCS will submit a request for the refund or will reject the refund request within 7 business days.

The entire process can take:

Check refund 2-3 weeks

Wire refund 6-8 weeks

CSP Partners will always receive any due credit as a deduction to next month's invoice. However, should the Partner leave the CSP program and stop transacting, they are entitled to receive a refund, if any credit is due.

If a work item assigned to WOCS is unresolved by their SLA, SME/ Escalation agent can send an email from the Service Request to one of the following aliases to request a status update (mark the email and the reply from the WOCS team as private):

  • For missing payment investigation for an account paid by invoice (Cash Applications team), contact wocscashleads@microsoft.com
  • For Credit Checks, contact riskescalation@microsoft.com
  • For all other issues for customers who pay by invoice (Refunds, Re-enablement, Account Reconciliation, Statements of Account, other issues), contact gcscatleads@microsoft.com 

WOCS Partner facing email addresses where we are not required to be involved (between Partner and WOCS teams):

  • WOCS Risk (ucmwrcsp@microsoft.com) – as part of the CSP Application process, Partners need to pass a credit check (see VKB 2981121 – section “Emails from the Credit Collections Team”). Therefore, the credit team will reach out to applicants requesting financial documents. Once the partner provides all the required documentation, WOCS SLA to complete the credit check is 48 hours. Additionally, WOCS Risk handles all questions related to CSP credit limit (see VKB 4132126 Credit Limit Enforcement in CMAT).
  • WOCS Security refunds (wocssec@microsoft.com):
    • Partners that are signing up and according to WOCS their financials are not good, they need to provide a security deposit as a guarantee (the security deposit can be refunded by WOCS if specific criteria are met)
    • If Partners are driving business more than the credit limit received from WOCS, they are requested a security deposit
