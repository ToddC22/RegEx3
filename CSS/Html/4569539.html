Overview

Commerce escalation that requires assistance from GFS will need to use the Assistance Request (AR) Feature in Rave. Below are the support areas that have been created and a description of what should be escalated per support area.

Description

Support Area

Responsibilities & Duties

Collections
  • Collect Payments for Invoices owed MSFT
  • Dunning letters (letter stating customer/partner are past due)
  • Invoice disputes by customer/partner
  • Works out 'Promise to Pay' agreements
  • Pre-billing checks to ensure accurate invoicing
  • Refund management
  • Retail deductions

CAT
  • Reviews account for accurate invoicing prior to invoicing (EA Direct accounts)
  • Managed non-collections activities such as:
    • Gatekeeping
    • Sending of Dunning Notification (Manual)
    • Refunds
    • Clearance
    • Escheatment and Writebacks
    • Bad Debt Write off
    • Write Off to GL
    • Vendor Form
    • Disabling (Cloud Direct)
    • Do not Contact tagging (Cloud Direct & CSP)
    • Account Reconciliation

Cash Applications
  • Apply payments received from customer/partner
  • Bank statements
  • Lockbox reports
  • SAP Auto Posting & Exceptions
  • Process bad debt write offs
  • Payment plans
  • Withholding Tax/FTC
  • Partial payment management/Residual payment management

Credit Risk
  • Account Mastering
  • Credit Reviews
  • Credit Analysis
  • Credit Limits/Scoring
  • Securities reviews
  • Credit Term Extensions (CTE)
  • Credit holds
  • Insurance
  • Bad Debt
