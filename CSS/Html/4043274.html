ASMS – Azure Free Account

Overview

This TSG provides details about the Azure Free Account offer (44P) and how it should be supported.
The Azure Free Account offer includes $200 of Azure credits (to be used within the first 30 days of sign-up) and 12 months of select free services.

Note: Modern Free Accounts have slightly different features compared to Legacy Free Accounts . In cases where they diverge in this TSG, the section will be broken out by noting Modern or Legacy as part of the header.

Policy

This offer is limited to one enrollment per eligible customer and cannot be combined with any other offer unless otherwise permitted by Microsoft.

Within 30 days of sign-up or upon exhaustion of the customer’s credits (whichever occurs first), the customer must upgrade to a Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG) account by removing the Spending Limit. This allows continued use of the Azure Free Account for the remaining 11 months. After the user has upgraded, usage outside the initial credits and select free services will be billed at PAYG rates. If the customer elects not to upgrade, the Azure Free Account subscription will be disabled.

[Asset 4043334]

The customer will be notified at least 30 days in advance of any increases to the PAYG rates. ​

Some customers may be able to sign up for Azure Free Trial subscriptions without phone verification or a credit card. To make things smoother for sign-up, some organizations have been designated Trusted Orgs.

Note the following about Trusted Orgs:

  • Users of these Trusted Orgs will pass through the Risk Evaluation mechanism without any phone verification or credit card.
  • Trusted Orgs are set at the top-level parent tenant the status applies to all users in the tenants under it.
  • Various criteria are used to identify a Trusted Org, which are a low risk for fraud.
  • Customers will sign up for the same Azure Free Trial offer (44P) as other new trial customers.

Note: This change is not advertised publicly and none of this information should be shared to any customer for any reason. However, SEs should expect to see Azure Free Trial subscriptions without credit cards.

Always engage ASE first when you are asked to engage ACE (unless there is an emergency). Do not use the ASE template as a way to open ICM tickets to any other team. ASE is not the same as ICM.

Modern Policy

Customers with an active Azure Free Trial subscription will be unable to create any other subscriptions, and are shown an error when attempting to add subscriptions to their Billing Profile. After the customer has upgraded to a PAYG account, they will no longer be blocked from creating subscriptions.

Overall Support Scenarios

Unable to activate Azure Free Account.

  1. Check the eligibility.
  2. If eligible, follow the usual troubleshooting steps outlined in TSG 3035847.
  3. If not eligible, follow the customer sign-up experience outlined in the Customer Experience and Provisioning section of this TSG.

Refund Request: Customer didn’t realize X service was not part of Free included Services.

  1. Explain what services are offered as Free Included Services.
  2. Provide the customer with the public facing documentation that explains which free services are included.
  3. Offer a one-time courtesy credit for the first time.

Is the Azure Free Account per "Account" or per "Customer"?

Per policy, the Azure Free Account is per customer. If a single customer uses multiple email addresses and multiple credit cards, it may be possible for the customer to create more than one Azure Free Account. However, checks by the Human Intelligence Team (HIT) are in place to try and prevent this.

Re-Enable Subscription: Subscription disabled after 30 days

  1. Re-enable and convert the subscription to PAYG. Because the conversion is from Azure Free Account to PAYG, Free Included Services should start from the time the subscription is enabled.
  2. If it’s not included, enable Free Included Services for 11 months

Converted Azure Free Account to X offer (other than PAYG) by mistake. Can I get Free Included Services back?

Free Includes Services are not removed upon offer conversion from Azure Free Account to any other offer. Customers will continue to receive the benefits.
 

Converted PAYG subscription with Free Included Services benefit to X offer by mistake. Can I get Free Included Services back?

Free Includes Services are not removed upon offer conversion from Free Account to any other offer. Customers will continue to receive the benefits.


Why am I billed for Azure Free Included Services? ​

  1. Look up usage details and explain why Free Included Services is ​​billed.
  2. If customer is billed as expected, explain the same.
  3. If customer is billed incorrectly, engage the engineering team to troubleshoot further.

How can I verify free services are active or when they will expire?

Navigate to Azure Portal​ > Subscription > Overview. Under Free services for 12 months, click the Information icon. This will display the date when Free services expire.

Jarvis Operations and Steps

Note: All Jarvis operations require JIT permission. Follow the standard process to get JIT access. (See TSG 3004901 (JIT Access) for additional information)

Show Azure Free Included Services with Start and End date

After you have recieved JIT access, perform the following steps to unlock this operation in Jarvis:

  1. Navigate to Jarvis > Billing > Provisioning Management > A- Get Subscription from Subscription Table.
  2. Click Get Access.
  3. Select the following:
    • Work-item Source: Other
    • Work-item Id: <Enter the Case Number>
    • Resource Type: Jarvis
    • ACIS Scope: Billing
    • Billing Access Level: Customer Service Operator
  4. Click Submit.

Edit Subscription Benefit Information

Note: This operation is used to Add Free Tier, Update Free Tier End date, and Remove Free Tier. This operation fails if the Start date and End date are set for more than 12 months.

After you have recieved JIT Access, perform the following steps to unlock this operation in Jarvis:

  1. Navigate to Jarvis > Billing > General Management > Edit Subscription Benefit Information.
  2. Click Get Access.
  3. Enter the following information:
  • Work-item Source: Other
  • Work-item Id: <Enter the Case Number>
  • Resource Type: Jarvis
  • Scope: Billing
  • Access Level: Customer Service Operator
  1. Add the following additional details:
  • Benefit Type
  • Benefit Start Date
  • Benefit End Date
  1. Click Submit.

Modern Support Scenarios

Monetary Cap Removal

There is no longer a concept of Monetary Cap Removal prior to conversion from Free Trial to PAYG. Upgrading from Free Trial to PAYG performs both the Monetary Cap Removal and Offer Conversion.
If a customer needs assistance with upgrading their Free Trial Subscription, Support should follow the Offer Conversion process outlined in the Modern Support Experience document.

Credits and Refunds

In the event a customer requests or is entitled to more free trial credits, those requests must be escalated to the MSaaS Azure Web Direct Escalations queue.
ASMS does not have permissions to provide Azure Free Trial credits or any other credit type beyond Refund on a purchase.

Engagement and Escalation Path

  • Refund: Use the existing process to engage Azure BG.
  • Purchase and Portal experience: Follow the existing process to engage ASE & ACE (TSG 309326).
  • Purchase failures due to PI, invoice issues, and other commerce-related issues: Follow the existing process to engage PayOps & Commerce Engineering (TSG 2651579)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I pay anything when I am on the Azure Free Account?
​You will be charged only if you deploy services that are not included in the Azure Free Account. ​Only after you deplete the $200 credit or reach the 30-day period (whichever comes first) will you begin to be billed at the PAYG rates.

How many Azure Free Account subscriptions am I allowed to use on my account?
There is a limit of one Azure Free Account subscription per account. ​

How does Azure Free Account work with Enterprise Agreement (EA) customers?
This offer is not available to EA customers. However, if a customer has a Free Account and converts to an EA tier later, the Free Account benefits will continue for 12 months from the time of the Free Account sign-up.

Will services offered in free tier consume from initial $200 credits?
No. Customers get a fixed count of Free Tier services and a $200 credit for the first 30 days. So, customer could use the $200 credit on other non-Free Tier services. ​
