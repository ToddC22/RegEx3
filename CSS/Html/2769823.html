What's this article about?

This article provides the process to follow for transferring a customer when they require help from another line of business.

Important notes:

  • Do not provide customers with internal transfer toll–free numbers located in this article. These numbers should not be provided as they are direct dial into the queue and bypass the automated system; which may result in the customer having to be reconnected to the correct department should they use the number again in the future. If a customer requests a phone number for the department you are connecting them to, provide them with the published toll–free number for that department or the general Microsoft number for their country (1–800–Microsoft for example) or direct them to support.microsoft.com.
  • You may provide customers with the listing of worldwide main contact numbers for Microsoft. The web address is:
    http://support.microsoft.com/gp/customer–service–phone–numbers/
  • Do not provide the IVR push button options to customers as these options might change and cause a poor customer experience.
  • For more help or information about transferring by phone or chat, see KB 3163896 Procedure: All–up landing page for Phone and Chat Transfer.

2.1.2.1 Requirements for transferring to Commercial for billing support if customer is only PI Owner

If customers inquire about charges and we search by credit card, we can locate a Microsoft account but it can’t be loaded to view transactions (example.@onmicrosoft.com). We should only transfer customers to Commercial Microsoft 365 Billing if they are the Global Admins or Billing Admins. Commerce advocates do not have the ability to search by PI and they are required to provide support. We need to advise customers (usually the accountant or book keeper is contacting us) to contact people in their company to see purchases of the Office Small Business services.

If the customer says they are the administrator, please have them log in to https://portal.office.com. If customers cannot log in, it is unlikely they are the administrator. For additional information, please refer to the scenarios below:

Customer states they are an administrator, however the log in is unsuccessful.

Explain that due to Personal Data concerns, you are not authorized to share any account information to validate if the customer is an administrator or not.

As a result, you will not be able to determine if the customer works or not for the company they are calling us on behalf of.

Explain to the customer that only their company billing administrator can access such information.

Note: Commerce support is not able to provide admin names to the customer. If the customer keeps on stating they are the administrator, transfer the customer to our Commercial support. The Commercial advocate can contact one of the company admins on the customer’s behalf to explain the situation and let them know what is being requested.

 

Customer can also raise a web incident via https://portal.office.com by navigation to the Admin Center and select Support on the left navigation pane.

 

[Asset 4072656]
Customer isn’t sure if they are an administrator or not.

Ask the customer to log in https://portal.office.com. If the log in is successful, then the customer is likely to be an administrator.

If the log in is unsuccessful, follow the steps above.

2.6.6 Skype Support

Skype provides chat and email support (but not phone support):
  • For Chat
    • Advocates from other lines of business should transfer the chat to a Skype support advocate via CES chat/Comms Console and include all relevant details for the Skype advocate to resolve.
  • For Phone
    • As Skype does not provide phone support for customers, advocates from other lines of business should direct customers to online support:
      • Suggested script: "I can help you to the Skype support site where there is a wealth of information that may help you. Please look here - www.support.skype.com."
    • If the customer demands they need assisted support or are unable to find what they’re looking for, the advocate should direct them to FA1170 How do I contact Skype Customer Service? 
      • If the customer can find what they are looking for, ask them to sign in and they will be taken to the getsupport flow where they can select their support issue and get through to the correct channel for support.
      • If they can’t sign in, then the customer should click on Problems signing in in the FAQ and they will be directed to recover their account online.
