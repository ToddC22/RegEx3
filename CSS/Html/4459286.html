These templates set the minimum expectation on what communications with customers and partners should include. While they can be used as is, verbatim, you can also modify and personalize them as needed:

  • Note: to help deliver a consistent and quality communication experience, if you choose to change the templates include the required information as shown in the guidance section at the bottom of each template. For instance, in a Standard Scope email ensure you include at minimum the Issue, Impact and Scope; these are marked as (Required). The bullets under the required sections are examples of what information could be included.

Additional Notes:

  • Only use a template if you speak and write the language it is written in.

  • Sections inside triangle brackets (< >) are placeholders and should be replaced.

  • To save yourself time copy the link to the language you use and save it to your favorites.

  • Internal templates will also be listed under your Lanugage for easy access, but are only available in English.
    For example: Summary, Transfer and U​nresponsive Customer Involve Incident Manager.
