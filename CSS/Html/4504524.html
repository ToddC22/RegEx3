Escalating MAK Exceptions to MAK ADD Agents

[Asset 4489629]

 Agents with VLKCSR permissions will need to escalate to agents with MAKADD permissions for the following scenarios

  • The VLK CSR Tool does not allow an increase or throws an error message
  • Request is to Block or Replace compromised / leaked keys.
  • Request is to assign additional keys to a customer’s agreement.

Note

MAK ADD agents will not provide any technical assistance or Phone support).

Under no circumstances should you set any expectation that MAK ADD / KMS agents will call the customer or transfer a customer on the phone to a KMS/MAK agent.

Maintain positioning that VLSC and KMS/MAK are two completely separate teams.

  1. Verify that the requestor is an authorized Admin, Key Administrator or Key Viewer in VLSC, that the products are associated to the agreement, and if the agreement is a subscription that it is in an active status.
  2. Complete the KMS MAK Increase Template – requesting missing required information from customer if necessary,

Note

Agents must use regular Escalation Idle Code when completing the template.

KMS / MAK Increase Template
  • Subject Line: VL Contact - MAK/KMS Increase Request
  • Case ID:
  • Company Name:
  • Agreement Number or License and Authorization Number:
  • Product Name:
  • 5x5 Product Key:
  • PKGroupID: (can be extracted from KIT)
  • Channel+Sequence: (can be extracted from KIT)
  • Key Type: (MAK or CSVLK/KMS)
  • New ROT/DMAK Limit: (Activation seats after increase)
  • Requested Increase: (how many activations being requested)
  • Business Justification: (this requires comprehensive or detailed reason)
  1. Inform the customer they will receive an email within the next 3 -5 business days once the increase has been processed.
     
    Increased MAK Count Email template:

    “Thank you for requesting additional activations for <<Microsoft Product>>. We are currently processing your request and it may take 3 to 5 business days to receive an update regarding this inquiry.

    The Case ID number associated to this request is <<<CASE ID>>>>. Please refer to this number for any future contacts regarding this matter. We appreciate your patience while we process your request.”

    Email Template for Additional Keys / Replacement Key / Blocking of Keys

    “We have received your request for << Product Key Replacement / Product Key Blocking >> and have assigned it to the appropriate team. Please be aware that it may take 7 to 10 business days to complete due to the approval and system processes involved.

    The Case ID number associated to this request is <<<CASE ID>>>>. Please refer to this number for any future contacts regarding this matter. We appreciate your patience while we process your request.”

     

  2. Add a Follow Up task in Service Desk for yourself for the next 2 business days, selecting “Support” as a Blocking Reason.
  3. Assign Collaboration task to the appropriate MAKADD regional Queue:

    • JAPAN

    • EMEA
      • EMEA_VL_KMSMAKADD,  MAK
  1. KMS MAKADD agent will complete the task in Service Desk and respond to the customer in English.
    1. EMEA

      2. Tier 1 Agent informs the customer of resolution and closes the case, unless customer indicates issue is not resolved.
