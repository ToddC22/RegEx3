What is this article about?

  • Details how to transfer Service Desk (SD) cases to the Partner Center team for issues determined to be ones that they support. 

 

This is not a phone transfer. 

The SD case is created, the case number is provided to the Partner, and the SD case is transferred to the Partner Center team.

IMPORTANT

Before transferring any case, beyond the specifics outlined below for this scenario, you must ensure you are also following all requirements outlined in the General Guidelines for all Transfers above.

While the specific steps vary by scenario, they always include:

  • Update the case title to reflect it was a misroute
  • Including in the Notes all details as to why you are transferring the case to another team


These steps should be followed for cases that customers submit a webform to VL/DEV/WWL teams, and for which the issue involves the Partner Center

  1. Agent enters the webform from unassigned cases
  2. Please complete following action prior to transferring the case:
    1. Click Edit
    2. Select Status: Identifying the issue
    3. Document in the notes that the question is outside the scope of the receiving VLS/DEV/WWL team, and therefore transferring the case to Partner Center team (in case of any doubts regarding support boundaries, please consult with Helpdesk)
    4. Change title misroute from your LOB like Developer, Student and Startup Programs, Learning for VL and keep existing title
    5. Don’t add any RCA into the case – leave it empty otherwise the case cannot be transferred
    6. Change the SAP to the new correct SAP:

Transfer by SAP

Product Family

Partner Center

Product

MPN topics: MPN Benefits and Competencies

CSP topics: Billing and Invoicing

Verify the Queue name – it should start with CSS PE

Product Version

not needed

Support Topic

not needed
  1. Click on Transfer Case
  2. Select Misroute, and then click Next
  3. Before clicking Submit please make sure that the queue name starting with CSS PE
  4. If the queue name is correct, click Submit. The case just has been transferred.

IMPORTANT

Before transferring any case, beyond the specifics outlined below for this scenario, you must ensure you are also following all requirements outlined in the General Guidelines for all Transfers above.

While the specific steps vary by scenario, they always include:

  • Update the case title to reflect it was a misroute
  • Including in the Notes all details as to why you are transferring the case to another team


These steps to be followed for cases in which the customer calls/chats with DEV/WWL/VLS support and reports an issue with the Partner Center.

While contacting the customer please remember to:

Confirm with the customer his contact details (email address and phone number)

  • If possible, ask for the Tenant name and add the Tenant ID in SD.
  • Share the case number with the customer
  • Inform that you will transfer the case to a correct support

  1. Click on +Case to create a new case
  2. Please complete following action in the case:
    1. Select Status: Identifying the issue
    2. Search for the customer. If you do not find him, please create a new one and assign this contact to the case
    3. Please describe customer issue in the field Customer Statement
    4. Enter a Case Title / add misroute from original team like Developer, Student and Startup, DEV or VL Programs
    5. Write a note that question is outside the DEV/VL or Learning team support scope and thus you transfer the case to Partner Center team (in case of any doubts regarding support boundaries, please consult with Helpdesk)
    6. Select the Support language of the customer
    7. Select Country of origin
    8. change the SAP to the new correct SAP:
    9. Product Family: Partner Center
    10. Product:
    11. For MPN: MPN Benefits and Competencies
    12. For CSP: Billing and Invoicing Product version: not needed
    13. Agent Identified Root Cause (AIRC)
      1. VLS Advocates choose: Root Cause - Volume Licensing\Misroute/Redirect\Internal\Transferred in Service Desk
      2. DEV/WWL Advocates:  Leave blank
    14. Product Version and Support Topic: not needed
  3. Select +Add Entitlement
  4. Select Professional Unpaid and choose Severity C
  5. Click on Submit
  6. Before transferring the case click on save and then continue with the transfer
  7. Click on Transfer Case
  8. Select Misroute and click Next
  9. Before clicking Submit, make sure that the queue name starts with CSS PE (if not, please contact your tier 2 or TA)
  10. If the queue name is correct, click Submit. The case just has been transferred.
