What is this article about?

This article provides information on Keywords, which are words or phrases users search for when looking for a product or service (for example, shoes, cruise vacations, tennis lessons in New York). In the context of search advertising, a keyword can refer to just a single word, several words, or even a phrase.

Summary of Service: Keyword Expansion

The Keyword Expansion service by the Back Office Support (BOS) team is offered to allow advertisers to improve their keyword lists. The BOS team uses Microsoft Advertising tools to get keyword ideas and suggestions based on various criteria, such as: Search queries containing the advertiser’s keywords, top 100 performing keywords of the advertiser, verticals for net-new accounts and add match type variants for keywords currently targeted.

Intelligence (Intelligence) (the main tool used by BOS for conducting keyword expansions) finds related and relevant keywords for target customers and suggest bids that will help the ads get more impressions or be seen by potential customers. The data Intelligence provides is based on historical performance from Microsoft Advertising. The BOS team will deliver an output file with quality data for improving advertisers reach and potentially drive more traffic and leads to the advertiser’s website.
