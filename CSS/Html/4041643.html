3. Process

IMPORTANT: Steps# 1,2,3 need to be followed only for CSP Partners (Direct bill and Indirect Providers). Step# 4 and 5 need to be followed for CSP, Indirect Reseller and MPN membership Partners.

Is the requestor a CSP partner (Direct Bill or Indirect Provider)? If yes, please follow steps# 1 – 5; if no, please proceed to step# 4 and 5.

Step 1:

Very Important!

Frontline team should check first if the request is coming from someone who has Global Admin rights by checking the name and the e-mail address used for sending the request. If the partner individual does not have Global Admin rights, advocates should refer back to the tenant Global Admin.

The tenant Global Admin must provide the following documentation: 

  • This form duly completed and signed (legal name change form with physical signature, scanned/pdf copy for further review);
  • Company Registration Certificate (as per MCRA))/Certificate of Incorporation on change name;
  • Tax Registration Certificate showing the new tax/VAT registration number

Step 2:

Escalate to WOCS via child SR to WOCS Risk (as per article id 4338769) requesting approval to change the Legal Entity name for the CSP Partner.

Step 3:

Once WOCS approval is given, agents will update the Company Name in CMAT by following the CMAT steps below. (Note: if frontline advocates don’t have the right permissions, they should escalate to their Escalation team (MPN Global Support).

a. In the Account Tab, Customer Profile section, update the name in the Billing Business Name field -> this will update both the Bill-to and the Service Recipient company names on future CSP invoices;

[Asset 4490536]

[Asset 4490538]

Important notes:

Company name changes will only reflect on future CSP invoices. Requests to regenerate old CSP invoices with the new company name details should be escalated to PayOps Redmond (see VKB 4038716, CSP: How and when to contact PayOps Fargo (aka CBO/GOC) and PayOps Redmond)

Step 4: Update of Legal company name and Location company name in Partner center

Very Important!

Frontline team should check first if the request is coming from someone who has Global Admin rights by checking the name and the e-mail address used for sending the request. If the partner individual does not have Global Admin rights, advocates should refer back to the tenant Global Admin.

To update the name in the Partner Center Dashboard, advocates should use PCST (Note: if frontline advocates don’t have the right permissions, they should escalate to their Escalation team (MPN Global Support)).

Step 4.1: Update of Legal company name in Partner center

Search for the partner in PCST:

[Asset 4490542]

Click Update next to Legal Business Profile

[Asset 4508654]

Change company name and then click Submit:

[Asset 4508655]

If the Submit button is greyed out, check:

  1. City field - by design PCST does not allow any non-English character in the City field. To be able to submit the change, the City field needs to contain English characters only.
  2. If there are misplaced characters or spaces (ex. the zip code is incorrect) in any of the other fields.

Please correct the city or any other field containing errors and submit the Legal company name change. If the Submit button is still greyed out, escalate to MPN Global support.

After updating the name in PCST the entire vetting process is re-triggered (not just the email verification step). To troubleshoot vetting issues please see SDK article id 4054653.

Note: The legal entity name in the profile screen initially shows as unchanged. However, after clicking on the Update button you will see the Company Name changed and the Legal Entity Vetting status = Pending. Once the partner has passed vetting again the company name will change on the Profile page too.
