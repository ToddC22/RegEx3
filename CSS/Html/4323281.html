Procedure: Partner Center: 715-xxxxxx message; process in scope for Delivery partners (Frontline advocates)

[Asset 4090831]

Summary

Who should follow this process?

CSS Frontline advocates supporting Partner Membership Center (PMC) and Partner Dashboard (Partner Center).

What is this process about?

The article provides an overview to the Frontline support teams related to an error message that the partners might receive in both Partner Membership Center and Partner Dashboard. The 715-error message is not limited to Partner Center portal and it might be received in any of the Microsoft portals such as Azure, VSS, etc.

However, this is the article of reference that should be used for Partner Center. Please do not use any other guidance from other article related to 715 error message.

When should this process be followed?

When a partner / user is receiving the following distinct messages: 715-xxxxx, no matter what action is he trying to complete.

Is this article applicable for all regions?

Yes.

1. Overview

The Microsoft Partner Network program suffered some changes that may result in a number of system-generated messages in either PMC or Partner Dashboard. 

This message can be received due to many actions but most popular are related but not limited to:

  • Partner tried to redeem the product keys 
  • Partner tried to add/remove users
  • Partner tried to assign VS privilege for users 
  • Partner is trying to migrate from PMC to PC
  • Partner is trying to update company profile

2. Process and Impact

If either of the above scenarios or new ones occur, please escalate to Sentinel Escalations sentesc@microsoft.com team through Service Desk e-mail, keeping MPN Global Support alias in cc for visibility.

Also, please inform partners that our system is detecting a problem and we need to escalate the case to a higher level of support.

3. Escalation Template

Title / email subject: P0 or P1 -  SRXXXXXXXXXXX – PC/PMC - Message code 715-xxxxxxx

Description:

1. User’s Business Email Address

2. User’s Microsoft Account ID/email

3. For MPN Partner Center escalations, the MPN ID

4. Transaction ID from error message screen

5. Date and approximate time the attempted transaction produced the error

6. Business/Organization Name

7. User’s first/last Name

8. User’s Phone number

9. User’s Country

10. What action did partner try to complete when he was impacted by this error message?

 

The priority is established based on these criteria:

P0-Extreme High Priority
P1-Standard escalation

 

The SLA for these priorities are:

Type of Work SLA-Response SLA-Resolution
Escalation - Pri 0 1/2 hour 4 hours
Escalation - Pri 1 1/2 hour 24 hours


After Sentinel Escalations sentesc@microsoft.com team concludes their investigation of the issue, they will reply using one of the following scenarios:

Scenario 1:

  • “The issue that prevented you from completing your registration/transaction has been resolved. Please try again.”

Scenario 2:

  • “Your account does not satisfy Microsoft criteria.”


3. Based on one of the above resolutions received from Sentinel team Frontline support should deliver the below message to the partner:

Scenario 1:

“The issue that prevented you from completing your registration/transaction has been resolved. Please try again.”

Call to action for Frontline advocates: Do not close the case in the Signposting queue. Use the queue where the case was managed and make sure CRC is accurate: Manage Partner Organization / Membership / 715-xxxxxx.

Scenario 2:

“Microsoft runs on trust, and therefore we engage in a rigorous set of evaluation and certification processes across third party individuals and organizations. We’re sorry we’re unable to reactivate your account access. Our investigation found your account doesn’t meet our minimum systems or security criteria. This decision won’t be changed by opening a new support case and I’m going to close this one as resolved. Thank you for your understanding.”

 

Call to action for Frontline advocates: Close the case in the Signposting queue (a queue that doesn’t generate survey). Please do not duplicate the efforts and send the escalation back to Sentinel team because the answer will be the same.

The only scenario in which Frontline support should return the escalations to Sentinel is when the account is unblocked by Sentinel, however partner is still impacted by the same error message. In this case, please ask for the account to be permanently unblocked, not just temporary.


Very important! – Please note that the process described above is applicable for all regions. Do not escalate these scenarios to MPN Global Support anymore; you are empowered to follow the process by yourself.

Refund request - due to 715 error message

Scenario: Partner is asking for a refund because he is impacted by 715 error, however Sentinel team cannot unblock Partner’s account.

Resolution:

  • If benefits are not consumed and he escalates the issue within 30 days since his membership is activated, the case must be escalated to MPN Billing team for a refund to be completed.
  • If any part of the benefits are consumed, the Partner is not eligible to receive the refund, and the request must be denied. Do not escalate the case
